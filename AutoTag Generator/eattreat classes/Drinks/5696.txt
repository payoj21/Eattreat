5696	Sing Along With Starters At These Grub & Gig Places In Delhi	<p>The Capital city has recently been witness to some seriously exciting music festivals like the Gig Week and we couldn’t be happier. Home to a burgeoning independent scene, the city’s soundscape ranges from metal, to indie, electronica, pop, jazz and so much more. Delhi’s live music venues are stepping up to take this movement forward. With a lot on offer in terms of performances and food, gig places in Delhi are giving the citizens a taste of the good life! We’ve curated a list of gig places in Delhi that also have great grub.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Piano Man Jazz Club</strong></h4><p>Touted as ‘the cultural window for Delhi’, this place oozes good vibes. It’s as many have put it, one of the best live music and gig places in Delhi and for good reason. The décor takes you to a NYC style jazz club. You can easily spot someone in the crowd just there for the music. Food here, is a European affair and cocktails are kept classic. A musicians-first place, you can catch performances by Bollyjazz, Dhruv Visvanath or even attend musicians mixers. While here, you can try their <strong>pizza pocket pies</strong> and sip on <strong>My Thai</strong>.</p><p>Eat: Pepperoni pizza pocket pies</p><p>Drink: My Thai</p><p>Cost for two: INR 1,800</p><p>Follow TPMJC <a href="https://www.facebook.com/The-Piano-Man-Jazz-Club-826258357452277/?fref=ts" target="_blank">here</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Depot 29</strong></h4><p>Depot 29 is Ritu Dalmia’s establishment which brings together original music, performing arts and contemporary cuisine from Mexico and the Americas. With acts ranging from Vasundhara Vee to Corridors performing here, Depot 29 is host to the City’s finest musicians. They have recently begun an initiative called ‘Sound Sessions’ to showcase a diverse range of artists and provide a supportive environment to creative potential. Way to go! While here, you may like to try their signature <strong>waffles</strong> (Served with a generous dollop of ice cream) or a heady cocktail like their <strong>Monicatini</strong> (white rum, fresh ginger, fresh chilli topped with sprite).</p><p>Eat: Wild berry compote &amp; mascarpone cheese waffles</p><p>Drink: Monicatini</p><p>Cost for two: INR 1,800</p><p>Follow Depot 29 <a href="https://www.facebook.com/depot29" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Hungry Monkey</strong></h4><p>The Hungry Monkey has ‘Music under the stars’ every Thursday, hosting talented musicians and performers for a night of raw, real and unadulterated music in an open terrace seating. Perfect for the nippy evenings. They even have an extensive selection of wines, and a wine bazaar every Wednesday (You get a bottle of domestic or imported wine at MRP with an order from their stellar main course). Artists who have performed there include Sejal Morris, Petit Quartet, Manta Sidhu and more. Don’t forget to try their <strong>Rice Wine Sambal Chicken Skewers</strong></p><p>Eat: The Jack Daniels Tenderloin Steak, Rice Wine Sambal Chicken Skewers</p><p>Drink: Smoky Orange Jam</p><p>Cost for two: INR 2,300</p><p>Follow The Hungry Monkey <a href="https://www.facebook.com/hungrymonkeydelhi/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Depot 48</strong></h4><h4 style="text-align: left;"><a href="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Spanishhamandcheesequesadilla.jpg"><img title = "Sing Along With Starters At These Grub &amp; Gig Places In Delhi  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5735" src="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Spanishhamandcheesequesadilla.jpg" alt = "winter live music gigs dinner cocktail Category:Drinks alcohol   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Spanishhamandcheesequesadilla.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Spanishhamandcheesequesadilla-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Spanishhamandcheesequesadilla-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></h4><p style="text-align: left;"><em>Photo Courtesy Of : <strong>Depot 48</strong></em></p><p>The premier meeting ground for musicians and music-lovers alike, Depot 48 is a venture by Vikas Narula with a menu curated by renowned Chef Ritu Dalmia and brings together original live music and contemporary World inspired cuisine in an acoustically treated space. Music and food get equal weightage here, and one can easily spend hours here at leisure. While here, you can sample their Linguini tossed with three chillies, or choose from a varied range of<strong> quesadillas</strong>. While here, you can catch performances by PCRC, Artistes Unlimited or RIVER, from among many more.</p><p>Eat: Spanish ham and Jack cheese quesadillas</p><p>Drink: Depot Teatime (Bourbon, triple sec, cinnamon syrup,aromatic bitters and orange bitters)</p><p>Cost for two: INR 1,800</p><p>Follow Depot 48 <a href="https://www.facebook.com/depot29.48/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Roost Urban Bistro</strong></h4><p>An all-day dining modern bistro which is big on live music and is one of our favourite gig places in Delhi. You can catch a lot of upcoming live music acts from the city here. Roost offers an eclectic menu with selections like the <strong>Half Roast Chicken</strong> (From the New York menu) and <strong>Chicken and Ham Voul a Vent</strong> (from their Paris menu) being highly recommended. From their range of cocktails, our picks would be their signature <strong>Roost Paanstch</strong> (their take on the original punch which originated in India) and <strong>Hot Toddy</strong> for the winter season.</p><p>Eat: Half Roast Chicken, Chicken and Ham Voul a Vent</p><p>Drink: Hot Toddy, Roost Paanstch</p><p>Cost for two: INR 1,500</p><p>Follow The Roost Urban Bistro <a href="https://www.facebook.com/RoostBarAndBistro/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of : <strong>The Hungry Monkey</strong></em></p>

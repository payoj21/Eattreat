3440	Watch Your Beer Being Brewed At Effingut Brewerkz In Pune	<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Effingut Brewerkz has been Pune’s magnet for all beer lovers since, a few years, with a vision for crafting, experimenting, brewing fresh beer. What we love best about the place is that you can literally see your beer being brewed and served. </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">In Pune, you can see a lot of places celebrating the festival with beers, food, fun and friends but Effingut steals the show as one of the coolest sports bars in town.</span></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">What if we told you the Effingut Brewery just got 5 times more epic celebrating Oktoberfest in full spirit? </span>For the Oktoberfest, they’ve got unlimited food and beer during 8 pm &#8211; 11 pm with a fairly great price range of INR 1350 &#8211; INR 1700.

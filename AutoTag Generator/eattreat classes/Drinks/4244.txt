4244	Grey Goose Boulangerie Bleue’s Festive Cocktails To Lift Your Spirits	<p>The Grey Goose festival may have ended but the party has clearly not stopped for those living in Delhi. We’re giving you some fun recipes to try with the amazing Grey Goose Vodka.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Grey Goose Le Fizz</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Elements</strong></p><p style="text-align: center;">35ml Grey Goose vodka</p><p style="text-align: center;">15ml elderflower syrup</p><p style="text-align: center;">10ml lime juice</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula</strong></p><p>Shake all the elements over ice and double strain into a champagne glass.

6915	There’s A Hot Chocolate Festival In Town Bringing Hot Chocolate Shots For 100 Bucks!	<p>Choko La, the couverture chocolate brand, is bringing a hot chocolate festival called ‘A Cup Of Warmth’ just for chocolate lovers in Delhi NCR. This news sure does warm up our hands in this biting cold.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></h4><p>The hot chocolate festival will start on Wednesday, the 18th of January, and will go on until Sunday, the 29th of January at their Cyberhub Café in Gurgaon. They’ve got a menu of savoury treats to go with the rich, creamy hot chocolates with six exotic variants including the <strong>Classic Dark Hot Chocolate</strong>, <strong>Coffee Hot Chocolate</strong>, <strong>Cinnamon Hot Chocolate</strong>, <strong>Hazelnut Hot Chocolate</strong>, the <strong>Classic Milk Hot Chocolate</strong>, and the <strong>White Vanilla Hot Chocolate</strong>.</p><p>All of these hot chocolates can be paired with options from their café menu, like <strong>Eggs Benedict</strong>, <strong>Pancakes with Whipped Cream</strong>, <strong>Bruschetta</strong>, <strong>Wraps</strong>, <strong>Sandwiches</strong>, and more.</p><p>A shot of Hot Chocolate would be priced at INR 100, but one can also purchase any of their ready to make hot chocolate drinks that are all beautifully packed in tins that start at INR 550.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>What’s More?</strong></h4><p>Choko La is also running a contest where you can tag someone you’d like to share your ‘Cup Of Warmth’ with, while tagging Choko La on your post. You could win yourself a daily prize of a shot of hot chocolate, or you could be one of three lucky winners who get the Choko La hamper that’s worth INR 1995. Just use the hashtags #CupOfWarmth and #ChokoLa when you share your photo on Facebook and Instagram.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> Choko La Café, K2 Cyberhub, Gurgaon</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 0124 – 6949400</p><p><strong>Cost per ticket</strong>&#8211; No entry fee</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/ChokolaChocolates/?hc_ref=SEARCH&amp;fref=nf" target="_blank">Choko La</a>.</p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Choko La </strong></em></p>

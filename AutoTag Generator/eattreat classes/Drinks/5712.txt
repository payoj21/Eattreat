5712	Curtain Call: These New Bars Of 2016 Are Where We Got Drunk All Year	<p>The year 2016 has seen the launch of many bars and these bars of 2016 have more than wow-ed us. While some may have worked spectacularly, others didn&#8217;t quite as they hoped to. Here’s a roundup of some of the popular ones from this year that you must visit before the year ends to make way for new ones in the city.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Bunta Bar</strong></h4><p>This decor is as quirky as its name. Bunta Bar has an extensive list of starters on its menu that are great to have with any of the desi drinks and cocktails that you’re getting.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/BuntaBar/?fref=ts" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prankster</strong></h4><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Prankster.jpg"><img title = "Curtain Call: These New Bars Of 2016 Are Where We Got Drunk All Year  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5747" src="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Prankster.jpg" alt = "wine drinks chocolate Category:Drinks bar alcohol   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Prankster.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Prankster-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Prankster-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Prankster</strong></em></p><p>This bar is decorated with artefacts that are reminiscent of our college days. Prankster has quickly gained popularity in the ever crowded bar and restaurant scene of Gurgaon. They’ve got an interesting take on Indian food and presentation. One can expect to spend hours just thinking about long forgotten memories with college friends.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/prankstergurgaon/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Tanddav</strong></h4><p>Quite contrary to its name, Tanddav is a casual dining restaurant and bar that has a European-style decor with chandeliers and delicate, floral prints all over the upholstery. They serve Asian fare, and the waiters are happy to assist you when you want to get suggestions for alcohol pairings as well.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/tanddav/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Office Canteen Bar</strong></h4><p>This is another of the bars of 2016 that is also located centrally. Office Canteen Bar might just become your favourite watering hole after your office hours are over. This one may also fill you with nostalgia, as the walls are decorated with things like cassettes, logos of the Kismi bar, Hershey’s chocolates, etc. They too serve food from all over the world, so one can always find something that they’re familiar with.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Officecanteenbar/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>OfficeOffice</strong></h4><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/officeoffice.jpg"><img title = "Curtain Call: These New Bars Of 2016 Are Where We Got Drunk All Year  Curtain Call: These New Bars Of 2016 Are Where We Got Drunk All Year  "class="alignnone wp-image-5746 size-full" src="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/officeoffice.jpg" alt = "wine drinks chocolate Category:Drinks bar alcohol   wine drinks chocolate Category:Drinks bar alcohol   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/officeoffice.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/officeoffice-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/officeoffice-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>OfficeOffice</strong></em></p><p>The theme is inspired from the popular TV show from the 90s. Another bar that has opened in Connaught Place this past year, OfficeOffice claims to be a ‘new generation work bar and lounge’. It is a co-working space in the afternoon that converts into a casual pub in the evenings.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/officeofficecp/?fref=ts" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Niche</strong></h4><p>The place truly justifies the name. Niche is a beautifully done up place with a truly niche decor. The food here is spectacular, and the service, impeccable. They have a lovely area for outdoor seating that one could use during the day or in the evenings for private engagements as well. It is certainly one of the bars of 2016 that has raised quite a few bars.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/NicheDelhi/?fref=ts" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 class="p1" style="text-align: center;"><strong><span class="s1">Gastronomica Kitchen &amp; Bar</span></strong></h4><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Chayan-and-smiti.jpg"><img title = "Curtain Call: These New Bars Of 2016 Are Where We Got Drunk All Year  Curtain Call: These New Bars Of 2016 Are Where We Got Drunk All Year  Curtain Call: These New Bars Of 2016 Are Where We Got Drunk All Year  "class="alignnone wp-image-5741 size-full" src="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Chayan-and-smiti.jpg" alt = "wine drinks chocolate Category:Drinks bar alcohol   wine drinks chocolate Category:Drinks bar alcohol   wine drinks chocolate Category:Drinks bar alcohol   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Chayan-and-smiti.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Chayan-and-smiti-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Chayan-and-smiti-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Gastronomica Kitchen &amp; Bar</strong></em></p><p class="p1"><span class="s1">Rustic chic meets old world charm at Gastronomica Kitchen &amp; Bar.

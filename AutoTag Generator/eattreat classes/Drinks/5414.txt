5414	Single-Serve Christmas Drinks For Those Spending Christmas Alone	<p>Christmas is just around the corner, and you’re probably dizzy from all the excitement just as much as we are. What better than to sit with a cocktail, to calm those nerves. Staying in never sounded this good with these Christmas drinks.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Hot Toddy</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Elements</strong></p><p style="text-align: center;">60 ml bourbon</p><p style="text-align: center;">Boiling Water</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 tablespoon honey</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 slice of lemon</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula</strong></p><p>We absolutely love <a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//warm-drinks-winter-alcohol-immunity/">toddy</a>. In a mug, combine rum and boiling water and stir well.</p><p>Add the honey and stir again till it completely dissolves.</p><p>Garnish with a slice of lemon.</p><hr /><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Egg Nog</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Elements</strong></p><p style="text-align: center;">60 ml bourbon</p><p style="text-align: center;">⅓ cup cold heavy cream</p><p style="text-align: center;">⅓ cup cold whole milk</p><p style="text-align: center;">Splash of vanilla extract</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 egg</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 tablespoon sugar</p><p style="text-align: center;">Freshly grated nutmeg</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula</strong></p><p>In a blender, break an egg and let it blend until frothy for about a minute.</p><p>Add sugar to the frothy egg while it’s still running. Let it blend for another minute.</p><p>Add the cream, milk, honey, and vanilla, and blend until all the ingredients have combined.</p><p>Pour into a container and chill for 20 minutes. Pour it out into your choice of glass and garnish with nutmeg.</p><p><strong>#EatExtra:</strong> You can make more by multiplying the quantities by 6, but you’ll have to let it chill for an hour.</p><hr /><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Hot Buttered Rum</strong></h4><p><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Wassail_4221293123.jpg"><img title stick while serving so your Christmas drinks gets a nicer, more cinnamony flavour.</p><hr /><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Milk Punch</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Elements</strong></p><p style="text-align: center;">60 ml brandy</p><p style="text-align: center;">60 ml full cream</p><p style="text-align: center;">60 ml toned milk</p><p style="text-align: center;">30 ml sugar syrup</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ teaspoon vanilla extract</p><p style="text-align: center;">Freshly grated nutmeg</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula</strong></p><p>Combine the brandy, full cream, toned milk, sugar syrup, and vanilla extract in a cocktail shaker filled with ice.</p><p>Shake vigorously till it’s very cold, and pour it into an old fashioned glass.</p><p>Sprinkle nutmeg powder on top.</p><p><em>Featured Photo Courtesy Of: <strong><a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/dinahmp/16134186779"

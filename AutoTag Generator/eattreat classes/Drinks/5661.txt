5661	Sandy’s Cocktails & Kitchen For Bespoke Cocktails In Gurgaon	<p><strong>Chatpati Diary </strong>took us on a visit to<a href="https://chatpatidiary.wordpress.com/2016/01/28/sandys-cocktail-kitchen/" target="_blank"><strong> Sandy&#8217;s Cocktails &amp; Kitchen</strong></a>, and as the name suggests it is symbolic of everything that its creator Sandeep Verma (aka Sandy) has accumulated over decades of experience in the hospitality sector. There is plenty to look forward to here (especially some amazing cocktails in Gurgaon).</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></h4><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Ol-Fashioned-Cocktail-2.jpg"><img title = "Sandy&#039;s Cocktails &amp; Kitchen For Bespoke Cocktails In Gurgaon  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5720" src="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Ol-Fashioned-Cocktail-2.jpg" alt = "restaurant gurgaon drinks cocktail Category:Drinks alcohol   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Ol-Fashioned-Cocktail-2.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Ol-Fashioned-Cocktail-2-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Ol-Fashioned-Cocktail-2-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p>An ideal place to dine in with that special someone, families and friends alike, Sandy and his team have used all their armour in making the place an ideal ground for great conversations, whilst enjoying blues, jazz, rock and swing music and live gigs. Situated in a quite area of Gurgaon’s sector 29 it is a full-service restaurant and bar that wears its creator’s clouds on its sleeve.</p><p>A visit to Sandy&#8217;s Cocktails &amp; Kitchen will have you basking in the peaceful and relaxing atmosphere conversation pieces that adorn its walls overlooking its herb garden. With an equally envious fresco area and unique outdoor experience, Sandy takes his motto of using only the freshest ingredients to a whole new level. An inspirational Pan-Asian and European cuisine crafted with love and passion, the kitchen at Sandy’s provides a fresh twist while churning out some old favourites.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"> <strong>Feast For The Senses</strong></h4><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/lamb-shank-jpg.jpeg"><img title = "Sandy&#039;s Cocktails &amp; Kitchen For Bespoke Cocktails In Gurgaon  Sandy&#039;s Cocktails &amp; Kitchen For Bespoke Cocktails In Gurgaon  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5721" src="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/lamb-shank-jpg.jpeg" alt = "restaurant gurgaon drinks cocktail Category:Drinks alcohol   restaurant gurgaon drinks cocktail Category:Drinks alcohol   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/lamb-shank-jpg.jpeg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/lamb-shank-jpg-300x180.jpeg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/lamb-shank-jpg-768x460.jpeg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p>The food and beverage menu have been carefully crafted and curated by Sandy himself. A few signature/trademark dishes that are worth mentioning are <strong>Seafood Risotto </strong>(Calamari, Prawns &amp; Fish fume), <strong>Gamberoni Alla Griglia </strong>(Grilled king prawns served with olive oil &amp; lemon juice emulsion with capers), <strong>Braised Lamb Shanks</strong> (Cooked in its own jus and served with globe artichokes, green pea and truffle risotto and sauteed spinach, shallot confit), <strong>Pork Chops Avec Polenta </strong>(pork chops served with home made barbeque sauce and herb polenta), <strong>Tian Fu Chilli Tofu </strong>(braised silken bean curd in chilli oyster sauce with tian fu chilli), <strong>Hu-Nan Style Prawns </strong>(made with fresh chilli, wasabi and schezwan sauce) to name a few.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Mind-blowing Mixology</strong></h4><p>Its signature bar, the highlight of the property is located in the basement, which has the 1920’s -New Orleans speakeasy feel to it.  Whether you are looking for a top Old Fashioned, a magnificent Martini or new age Molecular Cocktails in Gurgaon, Sandy&#8217;s Cocktails &amp; Kitchen is eager to concoct it for you. Few of the signature cocktails at Sandy’s are – <strong>Muddy Waters </strong>(Bourbon, brandy, maple syrup, Angostura, apple juice and cinnamon dust), <strong>Smok’d Celery Bloody Bitch </strong>(Vodka, Worcester, Tabasco, crushed pepper, tomato juice topped with smoked salt and celery foam), <strong>Last Tango in Modena </strong>(Tequila, Spicy bell pepper, passion fruit puree, basil leaves and lime) to name a few. They have over 100 cocktails listed in the menu, out of which 40 are hand crafted bespoke creations by Sandy himself.</p><p>Sandy is a live wire behind the counter, who himself dispenses the most suave and novel cocktails in gurgaon if not the capital. His mixology is not only creative but exotic too. He entertains tirelessly belting out humour and drinks with equal panache and style. Sandy believes that it’s the bartender who is the soul of every function and thus invented the term “Show Tender”.</p><p>His bartending exploits throughout India have provided him with over two decades of experience throughout the bar industry. Having tended bars for the rich and famous, Sandy has also appeared on TV and in national and trade press as an authority on drinks, mixology and bartending.</p><p><em>Photo courtesy of <strong>Sandy&#8217;s Cocktails &amp; Kitchen</strong></em></p>

6817	A Festival Of Cocktails & Booze? Delhi Cocktail Week Sure Looks Promising	<p>Coming back for it’s second edition, Delhi Cocktail Week, in very succinct words, can be described as a festival of cocktails and everything booze. Having already completed round in Mumbai and Bengaluru, it is back in Delhi with some amazing boozy concoctions. So get ready for award-winning swills and some fun stuff to keep you full to the brim.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></h4><p>What is the quite amusing is that the organisers are so done with the happy hour that they’re calling this the happy week. Happening from 4 Feb to 11 Feb, they are bring 30 bars from Delhi and Gurgaon, master class, pub crawls and celebrity bartenders (of course!).</p><p>Who all to catch? Well, we are keeping our eyes peeled for Cocktails &amp; Dreams, Speakeasy, PCO, Ek Bar, Fio Cookhouse, Qla, Ping’s Orient, Olive Bar &amp; Kitchen, and Guppy among others. Just imagine, as many PCO cocktails as you wish. Can it get any better really?</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>What’s More?</strong></h4><p>Well, we are definitely putting the Most Innovative Bartender Cocktail Competition at Qla on our things to do. The same goes for the specially curated pub crawls and master classes. Hopefully we will catch the celebrity bartenders Evgenya Prazdnik, Sabanina Olga, and Boris Ivanov mixing it up!</p><p>Look out for the handy handbook and the craft cocktail menus as well. We hope you’re getting the DCW Passport, which will give you one complimentary cocktail at selected participating bars. Cheers!</p><p><strong>When:</strong> 4 &#8211; 11 February 2017</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 300++ at participating bar</p><p>Explore the <a href="https://www.facebook.com/delhicocktailweek/" target="_blank">Delhi Cocktail Week</a></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Pixabay</strong></em></p>

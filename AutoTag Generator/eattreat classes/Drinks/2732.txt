2732	3 Unusual Mocktails With The Usual Elements In Your Pantry	<p>Unannounced guests are a real thing and sometimes you just don&#8217;t have enough things stocked up to make elaborate cocktails. So, give the usual mocktails a miss and opt for these wacky yet delicious recipes that are easy to stir up in a short time.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Atomic Cranberry </strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">Sour, sweet and sassy! This drink is everything you need to awaken your senses and start partying.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Serves:</strong> 2</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prep Time:</strong> 5 &#8211; 7 mins</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Elements:</strong></p><p style="text-align: center;">160 ml cranberry juice</p><p style="text-align: center;">80 ml litchi juice</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 1/4 teaspoon lemon juice</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 cup crushed ice</p><p style="text-align: center;">150 ml soda water</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 slices of lemon</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula: </strong></p><p>Add cranberry juice, litchi juice, lemon juice and crushed ice in a shaker and shake for 30 seconds. Add soda water and garnish with a slice of lemon in each glass. Serve in a martini glass with a salt ring.</p><hr /><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Rooh Afza Mojito</strong></p><p style="text-align: center;">The soothing rooh-afza shouldn&#8217;t be limited to just water and milk. Use it for a super twisted virgin mojito.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Serves:</strong> 1</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prep Time:</strong> 7 min</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Elements:</strong></p><p style="text-align: center;">30 ml Monin mojito mint</p><p style="text-align: center;">15 ml Rooh Afza</p><p style="text-align: center;">3 teaspoons lemon juice</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 cup crushed ice</p><p style="text-align: center;">Zest from 1 lemon</p><p style="text-align: center;">10-12 fresh mint leaves</p><p style="text-align: center;">200 ml soda water</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula:</strong></p><p>Add mojito syrup, Rooh Afza, lemon juice, crushed ice in a cocktail mixer and shake vigorously for a minute. Transfer to the serving glass and add thinly grated lemon zest and mint leaves, before pouring soda water. Stir for a few seconds and serve.</p><hr /><p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/09/photo-1455621481073-d5bc1c40e3cb.jpeg"><img title = "3 Unusual Mocktails With The Usual Elements In Your Pantry  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2738" src="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/09/photo-1455621481073-d5bc1c40e3cb.jpeg" alt = "  " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/photo-1455621481073-d5bc1c40e3cb.jpeg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/photo-1455621481073-d5bc1c40e3cb-300x180.jpeg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/photo-1455621481073-d5bc1c40e3cb-768x460.jpeg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Sparkling Ginger</strong></p><p style="text-align: center;">Ginger ale is great on its own, but its even better when you can add a few things and take it up a notch.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Serves:</strong> 2</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prep Time:</strong> 5 mins</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Elements:</strong></p><p style="text-align: center;">85 ml apple juice</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 teaspoon lemon juice</p><p style="text-align: center;">5-10 thinly grated slices of ginger (as per taste)</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 cup crushed ice</p><p style="text-align: center;">300 ml ginger ale</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula:</strong></p><p>Pour apple juice, lemon juice, ginger slices in a glass and mix. Top with crushed ice and pour a can of ginger ale. It has a strong pungent ginger flavour followed by a soothing apple aftertaste. Strain out the ginger slices if the flavour gets too intense.</p><p>Throw in chips and dips with these drinks for a quick and laidback party!</p>

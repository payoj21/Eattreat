2639	4 Stellar Places To Buy Roasted Coffee From In Delhi	<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">It’s really difficult to get your hands on some really good roast coffee. More often than not, we find ourselves looking for coffee beans online. But don’t you worry, we’ve got you covered! In no particular order, these are 4 of our favourite places to buy roasted coffee from.</span></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Blue Tokai Coffee Roasters</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">It isn’t much of a surprise that Blue Tokai makes into the list of every Delhiite who fancies coffee. What better than to watch them roast your favourite flavour right in front of you in their open roasteries, amirite? You can also order your coffee online. They also host quite a lot of wonderful workshops if you want to learn more about coffee or art. </span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Explore them at  Khasra 258, Lane 3, West End Marg Saidulajab or <a href="http://bluetokaicoffee.com" target="_blank">here</a>. </span></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Jug Mug Thela</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A neighbour to Blue Tokai Coffee Roasters, Jugmug Thela has a small </span><i><span style="font-weight: 400;">chai ka thela</span></i><span style="font-weight: 400;"> look to it. Although the setup is rather small compared to traditional cafes, they have a wide range of tea and gourmet coffee. Look out for their stunning handcrafted kettles and Vietnamese or Costa Rican gourmet coffees. </span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Explore them at Shed 4, Khasra 258, Behind Kuldeep House, Lane 3, Westend Marg, Saidulajaib and <a href="http://www.jugmugthela.com" target="_blank">here</a>. </span></p><p><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/09/12045405_736418673154148_4936570951097547562_o.jpg"><img title = "4 Stellar Places To Buy Roasted Coffee From In Delhi  "class="alignnone wp-image-2640 size-full" src="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/12045405_736418673154148_4936570951097547562_o.jpg" alt = "roasters coffee beans coffee Category:Drinks beverages   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/12045405_736418673154148_4936570951097547562_o.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/12045405_736418673154148_4936570951097547562_o-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/12045405_736418673154148_4936570951097547562_o-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Kaffa Cerado</strong></em></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Kaffa Cerrado</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">If you’ve ever been a fan of Perch, you’ll know where they source their coffee from. Kaffa Cerrado is a single source speciality coffee roaster based in Delhi. You can order from them online only. They have a subscription as well as retail going on. And we absolutely swear by their African and Sumatran blends. They also have equipment on the site, in case you need some for your morning cuppa.</span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Explore them <a href="http://www.kaffacerrado.com" target="_blank">here</a>. </span></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Dewan’s</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Dewan’s has been the ‘it’ place for all coffee lovers in Delhi for the past 50 years now. It is tucked away in Lodhi Colony’s Khanna Market, not too difficult to locate since everyone knows about this little wonder.</span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Explore them <a href="http://devans.in" target="_blank">here</a> or drop in at </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">131, Khanna Market, Lodhi Colony</span></p><p><em>Featured Image Courtesy Of: <strong>Blue Tokai Coffee Roasters</strong></em></p>

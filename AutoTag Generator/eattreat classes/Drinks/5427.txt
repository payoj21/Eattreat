5427	#HeyRum // Hot Buttered Rum That’ll Add Warmth To Your Festive Meals	<p>Our <a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//?s=heyrum&amp;submit="><strong>#HeyRum campaign</strong></a> is almost at its close and while you already know how much we love rum, we&#8217;re taking this love to the next level with our incredible hot buttered rum recipe. Try it yourself for an unmatched winter booze party!</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Elements</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">1/4 cup (1/2 stick) unsalted butter, room temperature</p><p style="text-align: center;">1/4 cup dark-brown sugar</p><p style="text-align: center;">30 ml honey</p><p style="text-align: center;">3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon</p><p style="text-align: center;">3/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg</p><p style="text-align: center;">3/4 teaspoon ground ginger</p><p style="text-align: center;">A pinch of ground cloves</p><p style="text-align: center;">Salt according to taste</p><p style="text-align: center;">45 ml dark rum</p><p style="text-align: center;">3/4 cup boiling water</p><p style="text-align: center;">Cinnamon sticks to garnish</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula</strong></h4><p>Beat butter, sugar, honey, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, cloves and salt until blended and smooth. Refrigerate until mixture gets slightly firm.</p><p>Add two tablespoons of spiced butter to the measured dark rum. Pour boiling water over top and stir to mix. Garnish with cinnamon sticks and serve!</p><p>Refrigerate rest of the butter and use whenever needed.

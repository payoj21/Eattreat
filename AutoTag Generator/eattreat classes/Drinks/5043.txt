5043	#HeyRum // Warm Up This Winter With Rum Drinks At These Bars In Delhi	<p>The fog is creeping in, and the days are getting colder. What better to keep us warm than a shot of rum, or even a glassful of a <a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//5-white-rum-love-old-monk/">rum-based cocktail </a>for those lovely Sunday morning brunches? We’re telling you the best places in Delhi for you to sip on rum drinks when you need that extra heat to get through the day.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cocktails &amp; Dreams, Speakeasy</strong></h4><p>This one comes highly recommended by anyone who’s visited it. Get the classic Mai Tai, or their own incredible invention that is the Thunderclap. It’s got whisky, gin, and a double measure of their resin infused rum. Or, if you’ve got an idea for a great rum drink, you can get the bartender to make one for you with your specifications!</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/CocktailsDreamsSpeakeasy/?fref=ts" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Ek Bar</strong></h4><p>Ek Bar may not have been around for too many years, but it has gained popularity since it has opened and for good reason. Get the classic Dark &amp; Stormy that has their homemade ginger beer, or get Mary Pickford that is made with white rum, pineapple juice, cherry liqueur and grenadine.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Ek-Bar-1465872637051572/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Molecule Air Bar</strong></h4><p><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Desi-Libre.jpg"><img title = "#HeyRum // Warm Up This Winter With Rum Drinks At These Bars In Delhi  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5048" src="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Desi-Libre.jpg" alt = "winter drinks Category:Drinks beverages bar alcohol   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Desi-Libre.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Desi-Libre-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Desi-Libre-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p>You probably already know how Molecule never fails to impress with their cocktails made with complex molecular mixology. You should definitely get the Indian twist on the classic Cuba Libre—the Desi Libre, or get any of their range of LIITs—Make In India is a crowd favourite.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Moleculeggn/?fref=ts" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Gastronomica Kitchen &amp; Bar</strong></h4><p>They’ve always got a great set of artists performing, and an even better list of drinks for you to choose from. Get the Apple Cinnamon Mojito which we feel is great for the winter feels. Or, if you’d like to experiment, get the Paan and Gulkand Mojito to end your meal.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/gastronomicaindia/?fref=ts" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Social</strong></h4><p>One cannot make a list of the best places to have cocktails without <a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//bringing-bae-bombay-cyberhub-social-hit/">mentioning Social</a>. You can choose from three types of their Longest Long Island Ice Teas, or get smaller rum drinks like the South Pacific Rim or Moonshine. They even have an Old Monk Jal Jeera and the Benarasi Patiala for anyone who likes the ethnic take on rum drinks.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/CyberHubSocial/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prankster</strong></h4><p>This is a comparatively newer restaurant that has decor that’s sure to transport you back to your school days. Get the Paan Pasand Mojito, or Baba Ji Ki Booti which has great, fresh flavours—perfect for when you’re winding down at the end of the day.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/prankstergurgaon/?fref=ts" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Flying Saucer Café</strong></h4><p>One of the more spacious restaurants with great, comfortable seating in Delhi, The Flying Saucer Café has some delicious cocktails on their menu. Get the FSC No. 5 which is white rum laced with jasmine and pineapple, the Cuba Libre with hazelnut and mint, the classic Mojito which has basil and clove, or the Daiquiri with star anise and litchi.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/TheFlyingSaucerCafe/?fref=ts" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Hungry Monkey</strong></h4><p><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/12/The-Hungry-Monkey.jpg"><img title = "#HeyRum // Warm Up This Winter With Rum Drinks At These Bars In Delhi  #HeyRum // Warm Up This Winter With Rum Drinks At These Bars In Delhi  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5050" src="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/12/The-Hungry-Monkey.jpg" alt = "winter drinks Category:Drinks beverages bar alcohol   winter drinks Category:Drinks beverages bar alcohol   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/The-Hungry-Monkey.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/The-Hungry-Monkey-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/The-Hungry-Monkey-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of:</em><strong><em> The Hungry Monkey</em></strong></p><p>This restaurant wins on so many levels, it’s crazy. Get the Strawberry Moonshine and the Monolith—both of which you can even get in a sharing size, the Rum Toddy for when you’re feeling a little under the weather, the Rum Runner, or the Brain Sandwich. They even have alcohol infused food, and grilled winter rum drinks!</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/hungrymonkeydelhi/?fref=ts" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Batli 29</strong></h4><p>This place has some great beer cocktails that you need to try ASAP! The Black Mamba (dark beer and rum), and the Batli Bomb (lager light, rum, tequila, and vodka) are both things that you should get.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/batli29/?fref=ts" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Chi Asian Cookhouse</strong></h4><p>The effective use of Asian flavours <a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//jack-daniels-dal-makhni-alcohol-infused/">all across the menu</a> is what makes people come back to Chi every time. Their Kaffir Daiquiri is delicious, and a few glasses of the Bluest Frog is guaranteed to have you soaring in no time!</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/chiasiancookhouse/?fref=ts" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Lodi &#8211; The Garden Restaurant</strong></h4><p>We’re loving Lodi for <a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//4-best-places-in-the-city-for-you-to-host-your-party-this-winter/">winter brunches </a>and all the great parties that one can host in the wonderful space that they have. They’ve got a whole range of differently flavoured Mojitos; for instance, you could get the Coconut Date, Minty Sweetness, and A Toast To The Coat (this one has curry leaves in it!). You could even get the Malibu Cinnamon Breezer, Sea Breeze on Silver Sand, Colour My Rum, and Mindwash—which is exactly as boozy as it sounds.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Sewara.LodiTheGardenRestaurant/?fref=ts" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><p><em>Featured Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Lodi &#8211; The Garden Restaurant</strong></em></p><hr /><p>You can check out how much we have immersed ourselves in our love for Old Monk, <a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//love-letter-old-monk/">this is what </a>you should be reading.

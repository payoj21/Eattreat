3560	A Veloute Sauce To Make With All Your Leftover Chicken Stock	<p>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-align: left;">Another one of those French machinations, the veloute sauce is easy to make and goes perfectly everything Continental. We even use it for our seafood, if you must know. Try some here.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Serves:</strong> 3 &#8211; 4</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prep Time:</strong> 5 mins</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cook Time:</strong> 10 mins</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Elements:</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">30 grams butter</p><p style="text-align: center;">30 grams flour</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 cups white chicken stock, warmed</p><p style="text-align: center;"><b>Method</b></p><p>On low heat, add the butter and flour in a saucepan and mix until well incorporated. Let the mix brown a bit.</p><p>Add the chicken stock and stir.</p><p>Remove, cool for two minutes and serve.</p><p><b></b><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Madishetty Manasa</strong></em></p>

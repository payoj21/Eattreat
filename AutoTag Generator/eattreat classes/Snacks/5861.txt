5861	Mister Chai Does Some Excellent Regional Indian Street Food At Shangri La	<p>Mister Chai is a unique concept that is curated to give patrons a local ‘tea shop feel’. It focuses on regional Indian street food and beverages with an emphasis on tea. Unlike a conventional tea lounge, Mister Chai is a dining destination in its own right. Popular Indian street food has been reinvented and presented in exceptional style.</p><p style="text-align: left;">Experienced and talented chefs under the guidance of Executive Chef, Neeraj Tyagi have collaborated to design a menu that is as creative as it is well-thought-out.The menu is a kaleidoscope of regional dishes with a modern twist.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Chai, Chai&#8230;&amp; More Chai!</strong></h4><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/mister-chai.jpg"><img title = "Mister Chai Does Some Excellent Regional Indian Street Food At Shangri La  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5862" src="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/mister-chai.jpg" alt = "tea street food coffee Category:Snacks cafes beverages   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/mister-chai.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/mister-chai-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/mister-chai-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p>Mister Chai’s beverage menu lives up to the restaurant’s name providing a plethora of choices with its tea selection and street food. It is all set to provide guests with an authentic local teashop experience in the heart of the city’s business and entertainment district.</p><p>The swanky new menu is designed in such a way that neither you’ll be worried about your pocket nor the taste.

5640	Forget Fruits, Get Cash Delivered To Your Doorstep With Grofers	<p>Yes Bank has tied hands with e-grocer Grofers to now get cash to your doorstep. That’s right. This facility can be availed when customers order online, allowing them to get cash of up to Rs 2,000.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></h4><p>The cash service, open to all bank account holders has started across Mumbai, Gurgaon and Bengaluru. Other cities will be added to the list gradually. A statement by Yes Bank said, “Grofers, the on-demand e-commerce mobile and web application, is opening up a new avenue for people to get cash delivered at home, by partnering with Yes Bank”</p><p>Grofers delivery executives will carry PoS machines (powered by Yes Bank) where customers can swipe and get cash within the comfort of their homes.</p><p>Other grocery start ups like Grocermax has also begun offering ‘doorstep liquidity’, by home delivering cash up to Rs 2,000 to customers who place orders worth Rs 1,500.</p><p>Offline retailers like Big Bazaar and INOX have also been offering this service to ease pressure on the banking system which is stretched beyond capacity due to heavy rush for cash withdrawals</p><p><em>Read the full story <a href="http://retail.economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/food-entertainment/grocery/yes-bank-joins-grofers-for-doorstep-cash/55973243" target="_blank">here</a>.</em></p><p><em>Featured Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Office Chai</strong></em></p>

7148	Move Over Honey Chilli Potato, Here’s Chilli Baby Corn 	<p>We know how much you like your Honey Chilli Potato, but trust us, this recipe for Chilli Baby Corn is what you need to change how you have snacks with your drink. <em>Chakhna</em> is must, no? The spicy-sweet, deep fried baby corn goes well with your evening drink. A plateful with your chai or coffee is enough to send you to food heaven.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Serves:</strong> 3-4</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prep Time:</strong> 10 mins</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cook Time:</strong> 15-20mins</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Elements</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">½ cup corn flour</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 tablespoons water</p><p style="text-align: center;">15 baby corns, halved</p><p style="text-align: center;">Oil, for deep frying</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ inch ginger, chopped</p><p style="text-align: center;">5 cloves garlic, chopped</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 onions, diced</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 green bell pepper, diced,</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 teaspoon soy sauce</p><p style="text-align: center;">2&nbsp;teaspoon vinegar</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 teaspoon tomato ketchup</p><p style="text-align: center;">Chopped garlic for garnish</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula</strong></h4><p>Place the corn flour in a bowl and stir in the water to form a thick paste.</p><p>Evenly coat the baby corn with this paste and set aside.</p><p>Heat the oil in a deep frying pan and fry the baby corn halves for five-seven minutes or until golden brown and crispy. Set aside</p><p>Heat a spoon of oil in a separate pan and brown the ginger and garlic. Add in the onions and bell peppers and stir-fry for five minutes. Add in the fried baby corn along with the soy sauce, vinegar and ketchup and cook further for about five minutes, until dry and crispy.</p><p>Garnish with sautéed, chopped garlic.</p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of:<strong>&nbsp;nettsu via Visualhunt&nbsp;</strong></em></p>

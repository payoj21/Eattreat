8488	Don’t Miss The Black Berry Mochi, Banana Sushi & Japanese Pizzas At Yum Yum Cha, CyberHub	<p><strong>Indian Food Freak</strong> is at CyberHub again, and after their awesome <a href="http://eattreat.in/magazine/burma-burma-vegetarian-in-cyberhub-gurgaon/">Burma Burma expedition</a>, we&#8217;ve been looking forward to their other stories. Here we go with their version of what Yum Yum Cha has on offer when it comes to Asian food in Gurgaon and especially, CyberHub.</p><hr><p>Walking down Cyber Hub at Cyber City, Gurgaon, one’s eyes are irresistibly drawn to&nbsp;Yum Yum Cha. The pastel shades of the origami pots placed outside makes one want to peek in and find out what the festivity is all about. Feast is what&nbsp;Yum Yum Cha&nbsp;has to offer in its modestly sized but seemingly expansive restaurant with natural light streaming in and the soothing white and pastel colours of origami figures dotting the walls, hanging on the ceiling, table service, simply everywhere.</p><h4><strong>In A Nutshell</strong></h4><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/20160827_131710.jpg"><img title = "Don&#039;t Miss The Black Berry Mochi, Banana Sushi &amp; Japanese Pizzas At Yum Yum Cha, CyberHub  "class="alignnone wp-image-8542 size-full" src="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/20160827_131710.jpg" alt = "restaurants japanese gurgaon desserts Chinese Category:Restaurants asian   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/20160827_131710.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/20160827_131710-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/20160827_131710-768x460.jpg 768w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/20160827_131710-825x493.jpg 825w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/20160827_131710-410x246.jpg 410w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/20160827_131710-150x90.jpg 150w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p>The menu card invites you to join in on a Pan-Asian flavoured jamboree with dishes represented in cartoons and served in two sizes- “little bundles of joy” and “huge bowls of happiness”. The menu lists in addition to the usual suspects, <strong>Japanese&nbsp;Takoyaki&nbsp;fried dumplings</strong> made in pans very similar to our South India panniyaram ones; paper hotpots and the delightful eye candies-&nbsp;<strong>Mochi ice creams</strong>. For the purists, there are the pure sushi rolls, nigiri, sashimi etc. but the locals are enticed with variations –spicy, creamy and simply, tasty.</p><h4><strong>On The Silver Platter</strong></h4><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/20160827_131455.jpg"><img title = "Don&#039;t Miss The Black Berry Mochi, Banana Sushi &amp; Japanese Pizzas At Yum Yum Cha, CyberHub  Don&#039;t Miss The Black Berry Mochi, Banana Sushi &amp; Japanese Pizzas At Yum Yum Cha, CyberHub  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8541" src="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/20160827_131455.jpg" alt = "restaurants japanese gurgaon desserts Chinese Category:Restaurants asian   restaurants japanese gurgaon desserts Chinese Category:Restaurants asian   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/20160827_131455.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/20160827_131455-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/20160827_131455-768x460.jpg 768w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/20160827_131455-825x493.jpg 825w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/20160827_131455-410x246.jpg 410w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/20160827_131455-150x90.jpg 150w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p><strong>Chicken dimsum</strong>&nbsp;flavoured with&nbsp;Chilli oil&nbsp;and fried browned garlic give a peppy zing to the meal.&nbsp;<strong>Crystal Duck</strong>, on the other hand, is subtle; the slivers of&nbsp;Peking duck&nbsp;wrapped in soothing flavours of hoisin and cherries.&nbsp;<strong>Hot and Spicy Jade</strong>&nbsp;tests our chopstick wielding skills, the translucent rice shell breaking down and spilling the contents at a mere touch. Work needs to be done on making the shells stay intact.</p><p><strong>Hotate Mentayaki</strong>&nbsp;is spot on, the&nbsp;scallop&nbsp;pairing well with a pleasing mayo sprinkled with cod roe.</p><p><strong>Snow pea salad</strong>&nbsp;is simplicity personified, the tender beans shredded and seasoned with nutty sesame seeds and chestnuts.&nbsp;<strong>Japanese pizzas</strong>&nbsp;are novel, one half topped with shavings of excellent red tuna dressed with truffle oil and citrusy ponzu sauce, and the other half, a demure but delicious peking duck.</p><h4><strong>Sushi Somethings</strong></h4><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/20160827_132424.jpg"><img title = "Don&#039;t Miss The Black Berry Mochi, Banana Sushi &amp;

3128	Quickly Whip Up This Baked Nachos Pizza With Cheddar	<p>Baked Nachos Pizza sounds complicated, but believe you us, its really simple. And quick! An evening snack when you&#8217;re being a potato, this will tide you over till dinner.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Elements:</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">A bag of 150 gm nachos (we like using the <span class="il">Cornitos</span> Extra Cheesy, Cheese and Herbs flavours)</p><p style="text-align: center;">50 gm cheddar cheese, grated</p><p style="text-align: center;">30 gm onion, finely chopped</p><p style="text-align: center;">30 gm tomato, seeded and diced</p><p style="text-align: center;">30 gm pickled black olives, sliced</p><p style="text-align: center;">50 gm red and green bell pepper, sliced</p><p style="text-align: center;">50 gm jalapeños, sliced</p><p style="text-align: center;">Oregano seasoning, to taste</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula: </strong></h4><p>Arrange a layer of nachos crisps on a dish and top it with all the ingredients. Now add  grated cheddar cheese on top.</p><p>Keep it in pre-heated oven for 1 minute until cheese melts.</p><p>Serve it hot immediately. Add oregano seasoning as per taste</p><p><em>Courtesy: <strong>Cornitos</strong></em></p>

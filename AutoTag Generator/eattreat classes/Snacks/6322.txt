6322	3 Cafes in Noida Sector 18 That Get Your Cuppa Coffee Just Right	<p>The cafes in this ever bustling marketplace may be many, but we all have our own favourites that we go to every time&#8211;whether it&#8217;s for the familiar faces that we see here, for the food, or for the decor. We&#8217;ve picked out the cafes in Noida Sector 18 that you should not miss if you ever find yourself near this side of town.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Our Story Bistro &amp; Tea Room</strong></h4><p>This cute two-storeyed cafe at the back of the very busy sector 18 market may be easy to miss, but is worth a visit. The place is beautifully decorated (as all tea rooms should!) and they have pretty floral prints and pastel colours everywhere. They serve continental food, tea, coffee, and other chilled drinks. The music is not too loud, so you can actually just hang back and have a nice conversation with people. This is one of the cafes in Noida sector 18 that even has shelves filled with books, and board games too!</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> G-38, 1st Floor, Sector 18, Noida (Near Karim’s)</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> +91 9599786807 &amp; +91 9599786808</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 1,000 for two people (approx.)</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/ourstorybistro%20/" target="_blank">Our Story Bistro &amp; Tea Room</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Paddy’s Cafe</strong></h4><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/14188574_1791002561179959_5346002585907429800_o.jpg"><img title = "3 Cafes in Noida Sector 18 That Get Your Cuppa Coffee Just Right  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6374" src="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/14188574_1791002561179959_5346002585907429800_o.jpg" alt = "snacks Noida Category:Snacks cafes beverages   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/14188574_1791002561179959_5346002585907429800_o.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/14188574_1791002561179959_5346002585907429800_o-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/14188574_1791002561179959_5346002585907429800_o-768x460.jpg 768w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/14188574_1791002561179959_5346002585907429800_o-825x493.jpg 825w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Paddy&#8217;s Cafe</strong></em></p><p>The name has nothing to do with St. Patrick or being Irish.

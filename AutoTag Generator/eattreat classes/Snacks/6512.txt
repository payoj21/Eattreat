6512	#EatCheap: Enjoy Local Delicacies From Around India On A Budget—Right Here In Delhi!	<p>Delhi has a lot to offer if what one is looking for is essentially an epicurean experience. Given that this city is a kaleidoscope of myriad cultures, there is a lot to take in. Nothing defines Delhi’s diversity better than the variety of local delicacies on offer.</p><p>Whether you’re looking to enjoy a whole and hearty meal from your domicile, or are an ardent foodie wanting to experiment with flavours, there is something for everyone. What’s more, it can be done on a budget. Here’s a list of our picks for regional favourites on a budget.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Zaffran – Ikshita’s Kitchen For Authentic Home-Cooked Kashmiri Food</strong></h4><p>Craving an authentic Kashimiri meal at home? Call Zaffran. Lightly spiced, flavoursome and authentic to the T, their <strong>Goshtaba</strong> is to die for. We also loved their <strong>Yakhni</strong> (mutton prepared in yoghurt gravy). They are a delivery establishment based in Noida.</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> +91 9711317898</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 300 for two</p><p><strong>Explore</strong> <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Zaffran-Ikshitas-Kashmiri-Kitchen-611363935678106/?fref=ts" target="_blank">Zaffran-Ikshita&#8217;s Kitchen</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Gharua Exaj For Home Style Assamese Fare</strong></h4><p>Gharua Exaj, in Lajpat Nagar, has a distinct homely air attached to it. While there, you will fall in love with the sincerity with which the food is cooked, every flavour is unique and every dish prepared with utmost care. The best part is you can enjoy an Assamese meal without paying a bomb. We thoroughly enjoyed having the <strong>Bhetki Curry With Mustard Seeds</strong>, along with steamed rice.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> D-28, Near M Cinemas, Amar Colony, Lajpat Nagar 4, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 41044299</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 500 for two</p><p><strong>Explore</strong> <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Gharua-Exaj-614966008534269/" target="_blank">Gharua Exaj</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Viva O Viva In Chanakya Puri For Goan Fish Curry</strong></h4><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/VIVA-O-VIVA.jpg"><img title = "#EatCheap: Enjoy Local Delicacies From Around India On A Budget—Right Here In Delhi! So carry cash (but you won’t be spending a bomb so don’t fret).</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 14, Goa Niwas, Bir Tikendrajit Marg, Behind Akbar Bhawan, Chanakyapuri, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 26118370</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 800 for two</p><p><strong>Explore</strong> <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Viva-O-Viva-Authentic-Goan-Food-Dining-Place-329521697123345/?fref=ts" target="_blank">Viva O Viva</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Litti.in For Litti Chokha &amp; More</strong></h4><p>If you want to try Bihari cuisine, and don’t want to spend too much dough, then go for Litti.in. With its variety of Bihari delicacies, you won’t be disappointed. They deliver only, so call them for all your Litti Chokha cravings.</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> +91 9650114758</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 300 for two</p><p><strong>Explore</strong> <a href="https://www.facebook.com/litti.in/" target="_blank">Litti.in</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Kerala Hotel At INA For Fish Curry &amp; Appams</strong></h4><p>Tucked away in a small corner at INA, Kerala Hotel is a hidden gem for its authentic Keralite cuisine at an affordable price. The folks here do a spicy <strong>Fish Curry</strong> which we absolutely loved. The <strong>Prawn Curry,</strong> too, is worth a try with its fresh and zingy taste. We also loved the <strong>Egg Biryani</strong>, which went well with our choice of curries. Rating high on the love-meter was the <strong>Prawn Fry</strong>, that’s just the amount of crispy we need to make our tummy happy. So what’re you waiting for? If spicy seafood is what you seek, head over to Kerala Hotel.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 211-A, Mohan Singh Market, INA Market, INA, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> +91 9810549978</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 400 for two</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Maa Taara For A Taste Of Bengal</strong></h4><p>What’s a regional favourites list without mention of Bengali food? And if it is Bengali food that you’re craving, then Maa Taara at CR Park has you sorted. The food here, is well prepared and delicious. Their <strong>Mutton Kosha</strong>, <strong>Pomfret Fry</strong> and <strong>Fish Curry</strong> are worth a try.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 45, 46, &amp; 47, Market 2, Chittaranjan Park, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 30806281</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 500 for two</p><p><em>Featured Photo Courtesy Of <strong>Litti.in</strong></em></p>

3571	The Yummy Mango Salsa That’ll Overtake Your Regular Mango Chutney	<p style="text-align: left;">Fresh and tangy salsa in minutes! We love a perfectly spiced salsa and when it comes in mango, then there&#8217;s no stopping the nacho bingeing. Are you ready for the challenge?</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Serves:</strong> 4 &#8211; 5</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prep Time:</strong> 10 minutes</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cook Time:</strong> 10 minutes</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Elements:</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">3 ripe mangoes, diced</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 medium red bell pepper, chopped</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ cup onions, chopped</p><p style="text-align: center;">¼ cup cilantro leaves, chopped</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ jalapeño, chopped</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 large lime, juiced</p><p style="text-align: center;">Salt to taste</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula:</strong></h4><p>In a bowl, combine the mango, red bell pepper, onions, cilantro, and jalapeños.</p><p>Pour in the lime juice, and sprinkle some salt.</p><p>Mix well, and your salsa is ready.</p><p><strong>#EatExtra:</strong> To get a strong flavour, let the salsa rest for about 10 minutes before digging in. You can have this with chips and even add as a relish to wraps to add that extra tanginess.</p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Madishetty Manasa</strong></em></p>

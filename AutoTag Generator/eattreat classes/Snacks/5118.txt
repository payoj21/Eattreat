5118	This Awesome Book Cafe In Delhi Lets You Exchange Books For Food	<p>If catching up on your unread book whilst  dunking your choco chip cookies in a glass of warm milk is your idea of a satisfying morning, Café XCO in Delhi is the perfect book cafe for you! But there’s more to it. You will get free company as well as free food just for exchanging your books with the fellow readers who are just as much of readers as you are!</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></h4><p>Having 8-10 sitting chairs, it&#8217;s also a great “adda” for Miranda college students. This café is popular for its cheesy burgers, brownies, white-sauce pastas and refreshing cold coffee (obviously!). Though the prices are quite pocket-friendly; for example, the delectable cheeseburgers are for just INR 70, if you have a book or two in a good condition you can get  your eats for free.</p><p>So, in this demonetization period, if you are feeling the pinch but still want to indulge, this is should be your go to place. They also let you exchange books forever or for a while or even keep your books in there for others to read. We are absolutely loving the concept of this book cafe and we hope you go take a look-see soon!</p><p>Explore more <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Xchangeshack/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>

6313	#EatCheap: Try These Restaurants When Your Wanderlust Kicks In (On A Budget!)	<p>Delhi&#8217;s gastronomical landscape is brimming with world cuisine, and we know how much it pinches when you&#8217;re yearning to experiment with different tastes, but don&#8217;t wish to burn a sizeable hole in your pocket. So, purely for the wanderlust that is slowly taking over your soul, we&#8217;ve curated a list of all restaurants that have international cuisine on a budget, where you can score something different for a reasonable price.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>L&#8217;Amour Fou Afghan</strong></h4><p>Located in the heart of Kalkaji, L&#8217;Amour Fou Afghan serves authentic Afghani delicacies. What really sets it apart, is the service. Ever-courteous and polite, they&#8217;ll offer their help in navigating through the menu. We recommend their Yakhni Murg (tender pieces of chicken cooked in traditional afghani spices and chicken broth) and for the vegetarians, the Ashak is worth a try.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 31, Krishna Market, Kalkaji, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 41094029</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 550 for two</p><p><strong>Explore</strong> <a href="https://www.facebook.com/LamourFouAfghan/?fref=ts" target="_blank">L&#8217;Amour Fou Afghan</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Sam&#8217;s Cafe</strong></h4><p>Sam&#8217;s Cafe in Paharganj is famous for its buff steaks and rooftop terrace. It&#8217;s one of the must-visits in Paharganj, and for good reason. We love their steaks and the cheeseballs are worth a try too. They serve European fare and the menu also features Chinese and North Indian.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> Vivek Hotel, Main Bazaar, Paharganj, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 46470555</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 750 for two</p><p><strong>Explore</strong> <a href="https://www.facebook.com/samscafedelhi/?fref=ts" target="_blank">Sam&#8217;s Cafe</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"></h4><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Sushiya</strong></h4><p>If you&#8217;re looking to try some Japanese, then look no further, for Sushiya has all of us sorted. Pick up a meal-in-a-box (their Chicken Teriyaki Don is pretty good), a Sushi box (we recommend the Salmon Paradise Box) or even some wholesome Miso Soup. A small outlet, but it surely packs a whole lotta punch.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 1st Floor, Select Citywalk Mall, Saket, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> +91 9312444086</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 800 for two</p><p><strong>Explore</strong> <a href="https://www.facebook.com/SushiyaOnline/?fref=ts" target="_blank">Sushiya</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Ichiban</strong></h4><p>We&#8217;ve been frequenting Ichiban for all our pan-asian cravings. Located in the popular Pandara Market traditionally known for butter chicken, Ichiban has been dishing out some authentic Thai, Japanese and Chinese cuisine since as long as we can remember. Try their Tom Kha Soup, and Shredded Lamb Bell Pepper.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 9, Pandara Road Market, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 33107555</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 1,200 for two</p><p><strong>Explore</strong> <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Ichiban-Restaurant-137726209608103/?fref=ts" target="_blank">Ichiban</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Berco&#8217;s</strong></h4><p>If comfort food be thy seek, then Berco&#8217;s is indeed the answer. Doing Asian and international cuisine on a budget, we usually trudge to CP to try their fare, fairly often. An establishment dating back to 1982, Berco&#8217;s has been doling out some awesome Chinese and Thai food. We love their Veg Butter Pepper Garlic Noodles. Yum.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> G-2/43, Middle Circle, Connaught Place, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 33106478</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 1,100 for two</p><p><strong>Explore</strong> <a href="https://www.facebook.com/BercosIndia/?fref=ts" target="_blank">Berco&#8217;s</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Hudson Cafe</strong></h4><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/14680580_1785874648369042_5201188047809586553_n.png"><img title = "#EatCheap: Try These Restaurants When Your Wanderlust Kicks In (On A Budget!)

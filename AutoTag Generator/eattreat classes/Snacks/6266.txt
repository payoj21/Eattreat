6266	Say Bye To Frozen Dinners & Pick These Budget Takeaways For A Quick Dinner	<p>We don’t need to tell you how times have changed and how more and more people opt for frozen dinners instead of making an effort to make fresh food. Sure, you can always get that plate of momos packed from outside your office (Kent’s in Defence Colony makes the best!), but the question is whether you really want to do just that. What if you get company? (Cue in tune from a horror show.) What we can tell you is about some cool takeaway restaurants in Delhi, where you can get freshly made hot food that you can take away.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Lhasa Restaurant</strong></h4><p>If you’ve ever craved for good Tibetan food, but are too busy or lazy to go all the way to Majnu Ka Tila to get some, you can stop right here. Go to Lhasa restaurant and get your favourites packed so you can sit and eat in the comfort of your home. The best part is that it’s in South Delhi and the prices won’t burn a hole in your pocket!</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 198/52, Ramesh Market, East of Kailash, New Delhi.</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 64557575</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 500 for two</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/LhasaRestaurant/?fref=ts" target="_blank">Lhasa Restaurant</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Kolkata Biryani House</strong></h4><p>You probably love biryani as much as we do. But, more often than not, you end up not having time to make one when you’re in the mood for it. Head to Kolkata Biryani House in CR Park (or even at Gurgaon or Noida) and get your fill of steaming hot plates of traditional Kolkata style biryani with eggs and potatoes. Takeaway restaurants in Delhi have some pretty great things going for them.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> No. 49, Market No.1, CR Park, New Delhi.</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 41749595 &amp; 011 41749696</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 500 for two</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/kolkatabiryanihouse/?fref=ts" target="_blank">Kolkata Biryani House</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>DomDom Biryani</strong></h4><p>CR Park can get a tad crowded, what with it being a popular food hub and all. So, if you still want that plateful of authentic Kolkata Biryani but you’re too impatient to wait, head to DomDom Biryani in South Ex. You can take it from us, the food is damn nice and you’re going to end up licking your fingers after your meal!</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> A Block, South Extension 2, New Delhi.</p><p><strong>Say hello</strong> at 088008 86530</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 800 for two</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/domdombiryani/" target="_blank">DomDom Biryani</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Shama Chicken Corner</strong></h4><p>Clearly, getting biryani packed is a lot easier than making it. If you’re not in the mood for a rice dish, you could get one of their <strong>kormas</strong> with their very light <strong>rumali roti</strong>.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> Shop 1, Fazilpur Chowk, Near Omaxe City, Sohna Road, Gurgaon</p><p><strong>Say hello</strong> at 096548 97132</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 500 for two.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>IndoCheen</strong></h4><p>We know you love your Chindian food. <strong>Honey Chilli Potatoes</strong>, <strong>Momos</strong>, <strong>Chowmein</strong> are things that you can thrive on forever and ever.

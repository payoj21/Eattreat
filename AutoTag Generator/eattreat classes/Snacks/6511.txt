6511	Eattreat Food Guide: The Top Cafés, Bars & Restaurants In Rajouri Garden To Check In At	<p>Picking out just a couple of the many restaurants, cafes, bars, and dessert places that exist in the food hub that sees the launch of new restaurants every other month is no small task. You can walk into Rajouri Garden and find what you’re looking for, no matter where you look. We’ve still tried to pick out the restaurants in Rajouri Garden that you absolutely need to go to on days when you want a change in your routine.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Restaurants</strong></span></h4><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The California Boulevard</strong></h4><p>Dine in style at this Hollywood-themed restaurant that serves continental and Asian food, and a few familiar Indian dishes too. If you have longed for a peaceful and quiet lunch or dinner at a restaurant in Rajouri Garden, this is where you need to go. You should book your table in advance because you might have to wait to be seated here. Choose from the ‘Chef’s Special Set Menu’, or make your way around the many flavours they have to offer. Get the <strong>Prawn Chips</strong>, <strong>Chicken Adana Kebabs</strong>, <strong>Californian Capricorn Burger</strong>, or their <strong>Meat Fondue</strong>, and chase it with any of their delicious cocktails and non-alcoholic beverages that they do so well!</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> J 2/5, B.K. Dutt Market, Rajouri Garden</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 3014 6033</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 2,000 for two people (approx).</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/TheCaliforniaBoulevard/" target="_blank">The California Boulevard</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>National Highway 44</strong></h4><p>Also known as NH-44 &#8211; Pan-Indian Cuisine, this is a place that looks like the colourful dhabas alongside the highways, one instantly feels welcomed as soon as they’ve stepped in. If you’ve ever been on a road trip exploring culture and food, this place will bring back memories of pit stops that were meant to last for a few minutes but got stretched to more than an hour. Get the <strong>Patthar ke Kabab</strong>, <strong>Lahsooni Palak Kofta</strong>, and the <strong>Tandoori Chaap</strong> here.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 1st Floor, C-13, Vishal Enclave, Rajouri Garden, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 33106179</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 1,100 for two people (approx) with alcohol.</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/NH-44-Pan-Indian-Cuisine-1121114467900865/" target="_blank">National Highway 44</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Habibi</strong></h4><h4 style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/habibi.jpg"><img title = "Eattreat Food Guide: The Top Cafés, Bars &amp; Restaurants In Rajouri Garden To Check In At  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6581" src="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/habibi.jpg" alt = "rajouri garden Indian Category:Restaurants cafes bar   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/habibi.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/habibi-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/habibi-768x460.jpg 768w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/habibi-825x493.jpg 825w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></h4><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Habibi</strong></em></p><p>If good, authentic Middle-Eastern food is what you’ve been craving, this is for you. Habibi wows everyone with hearty food from the Middle East, and they show you quite wonderfully that this cuisine is so much more than Shawarmas. They even have<strong> Arabian Style Pizzas</strong> and <strong>Hot Mezze Platters</strong> on the menu. What’s more is that they have live belly dancing on Fridays!</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> C-13, 1st Floor, Opposite Metro Pillar 414, Vishal Enclave, Rajouri Garden, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello</strong> at 011 33107880</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 1,250 for two people (approx).</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Habibirajouri/" target="_blank">Habibi</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Cafés</strong></span></h4><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>What Else Café</strong></h4><p>They serve a little bit of each of North Indian, Chinese, Mexican, and Italian food that people normally look to order at cafés. This is more of a hang out place than a coffee house and they have all the popular dishes and interesting cocktails and shakes on their menu. You’ll probably want to come here more often with friends every weekend.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> J-2/21, B.K. They not only serve quick bites but full-fledged meals as well. The portions of food are quite generous for the amount that they charge. This is perfect for this who just want food that doesn’t take too much time to prepare. They even have a Man vs Food style challenge for those who dare to try their ‘<strong>World’s Spiciest Burger</strong>’ with the Bhut Jholakia sauce! Get your choice of grub with a side of their <strong>Kumara Fries</strong>, and any of the <strong>Frappés</strong> in their menu.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> Z-9, Opposite Metro Pillar 420, Rajouri Garden, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> +91 8800807807</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 500 for two people (approx).</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/theburgerclubindia/?fref=ts" target="_blank">The Burger Club</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Bars</strong></span></h4><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Factory</strong></h4><p>This factory themed resto-bar serves delicious multi-cuisine food and delectable drinks to match. Anyone who’s been here falls in love with how well the theme has been incorporated in every inch of the decor, the uniforms of those who work here, and the food. Get the <strong>Chicken Taco with Maggi Noodles</strong> and the <strong>Beetroot Halwa</strong> here.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> C-13, Vishal Enclave, Rajouri Garden, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> +91 9821780639</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 1,300 for two people (approx) with alcohol.</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/FactoryBistroAndBar/" target="_blank">Factory</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Lights Camera Action &#8211; Air Bar</strong></h4><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/LIGHTS-CAMERA-ACTION.jpg"><img title = "Eattreat Food Guide: The Top Cafés, Bars &amp; Restaurants In Rajouri Garden To Check In At  Eattreat Food Guide: The Top Cafés, Bars &amp; Restaurants In Rajouri Garden To Check In At  Eattreat Food Guide: The Top Cafés, Bars &amp; Restaurants In Rajouri Garden To Check In At  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6582" src="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/LIGHTS-CAMERA-ACTION.jpg" alt = "rajouri garden Indian Category:Restaurants cafes bar   rajouri garden Indian Category:Restaurants cafes bar   rajouri garden Indian Category:Restaurants cafes bar   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/LIGHTS-CAMERA-ACTION.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/LIGHTS-CAMERA-ACTION-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/LIGHTS-CAMERA-ACTION-768x460.jpg 768w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/LIGHTS-CAMERA-ACTION-825x493.jpg 825w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Lights Camera Action</strong></em></p><p>This Bollywood themed restaurant has become a favourite for many. The indoor seating may be a little too dim, and so you should move to the Air Bar instead. Dialogues from films and quirky film posters adorn the walls. Some of the dishes here have been given names of popular characters and catchphrases too. Try the <strong>Pyaar Tuney Quesadillas</strong>, <strong>Humari Murgi Chali Dusrey Desh Ma</strong>, and <strong>Nandu Sabka Bandu Pasta</strong> when you’re here.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> J-2/6 B, 3rd Floor, BK Dutt Market, Rajouri Garden, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 4704 1334</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 1,300 for two people (approx) with alcohol.</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/LightCameraAction.Delhi/" target="_blank">Lights Camera Action &#8211; Air Bar</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Drunk House</strong></h4><p>This is a great place to actually get drunk while you spend your time there. the service is quick, and the food is great. You can feel yourself slowly getting high as you sip on your drink and get one of their hookahs while munching on things like the <strong>Tandoori Soya Chaap</strong>, the <strong>Kandhari Chicken</strong>, and the <strong>Butter Garlic Chicken</strong>.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> A-15, 2nd Floor, Vishal Enclave, Najafgarh Road, Rajouri Garden, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 33105254</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 1,300 for two people (approx) with alcohol.</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/thedrunkhousecafe/" target="_blank">The Drunk House</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Dessert Places</strong></span></h4><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Goosebumps</strong></h4><p>This bakery and café is extremely new to the area. They just opened before Christmas and they make some of the creamiest ice creams, custom cakes, and a lot of other bakery products. They even serve wood-fired pizzas, pasta, and waffles! You can easily spend quite a bit of time just deciding what it is that you want. Get the <strong>Blueberry Pastry</strong>, <strong>Ferrero Rocher Ice Cream</strong>, and the <strong>Cookie Dough Pizza</strong>.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> Z-11 Rajouri garden Opposite Metro Pillar No. 421, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> +91 9911735552</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 300 for two people (approx.).</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/pg/GoosebumpsRajouri/about/" target="_blank">Goosebumps</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Natural Ice Cream</strong></h4><p>Everybody loves a nice yummy ice cream even on days when it’s still cold outside. Ever since Natural entered Delhi, people have made it a point to go there just to get a scoop of their <strong>Tender Coconut</strong> ice cream. Other flavours you need to get in case you haven’t already, include <strong>Jamun</strong>, <strong>Jackfruit</strong>, <strong>Chikoo</strong>, and <strong>Roasted Almonds</strong>.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> J-2/10, BK Dutt Market, Rajouri Garden, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> +91 8080801984</p><p>Pay INR 150 for two people (approx.).</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Naturalicecream/?fref=ts" target="_blank">Natural Ice Cream</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Mister Gulati Bakers</strong></h4><p>They don’t have tables or a seating area, but as soon as you’ve had a spoonful of their cakes, you’re going to wish they did. They’ve been serving the same great quality cakes for the longest time. You can get all your cakes from here by ordering in advance, but they have a few standard flavours that are immediately available anyway. Get the classic <strong>Pineapple</strong> and the <strong>Choco Truffle Cake</strong>, or get one with the <strong>Fresh Fruits</strong> and the one with <strong>Snickers</strong>.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> J-7/80 A, Nehru Market, Rajouri Garden, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 33105130</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 500 for two people (approx.).</p><p><em>Featured Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>The Drunk House</strong></em></p>

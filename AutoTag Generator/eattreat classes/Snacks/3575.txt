3575	This Versatile Rocket Basil Pesto Recipe Goes With Any Bland Sandwich	<p style="text-align: left;">Our rendition of pesto! Pesto is our guilty secret. We like it on our pizzas, sandwiches, pastas and almost with everything else. If you&#8217;re as mad about this dip as us, read on!</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Serves:</strong> 3-4</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prep Time:</strong> 10 minutes</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cook Time:</strong> 10 minutes</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Elements:</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">2 cloves garlic</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ cup pine nuts</p><p style="text-align: center;">1½ cup basil leaves</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 cup rocket leaves</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ teaspoon lemon zest</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 tablespoon lemon juice</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 cup olive oil</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese</p><p style="text-align: center;">Salt to taste</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula:</strong></h4><p>Blend together the garlic cloves and pine nuts in a grinder.</p><p>Add in the basil, rocket, lemon zest, lemon juice, salt and olive oil and grind until you reach a smooth consistency.</p><p>Now throw in the grated Parmesan and your pesto is ready!</p><p><strong>#EatExtra:</strong> Store in an air-tight container, and drizzle a thin layer of olive oil over the top to seal it in. You could use this as a salad dressing, spread for your canapés when you’re entertaining, or even use it with pasta.</p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Madishetty Manasa</strong></em></p>

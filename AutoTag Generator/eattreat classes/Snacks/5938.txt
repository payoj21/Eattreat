5938	The Famous Chole Kulche Food Cart Is Now A Food Truck In Gurgaon!	<p style="text-align: left;">Chole Kulche lovers in Gurgaon, rejoice!<a href="http://indianexpress.com/article/trending/trending-in-india/this-gurugram-woman-started-a-kulchha-chana-thela-to-save-her-family-from-financial-crisis-2950083/" target="_blank"> Urvashi Yadav, the internet sensation </a>famous for her Chole Kulche cart in HUDA Sector 14 has finally obtained a vendor’s license and plans to launch a food truck very soon.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></h4><p style="text-align: left;">Social media was abuzz with Urvashi’s story &#8211; she had set up her Chole Kulche cart to support her family. The thirty-four-year-old former school teacher Urvashi Yadav has been selling chole kulche and parantha from a thela (wooden cart). The sudden shift in occupation happened after her husband met with an accident — second in six years — and doctors advised a hip replacement for him. After the mishap, Urvashi feared a bleak future for her family and took it upon herself to support it financially. Till May 2016, she used to supplement the family income by working as a nursery school teacher.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>An Inspiring Journey</strong></h4><p>Her obtaining the license by the Municipal Corporation of Gurgaon (MCG) is “an important milestone on her journey to the next venture – a food truck”.</p><p>Her stall’s popularity was testified by the immense positive response she got at the ‘digi dhan mela’ on Monday, where she served her famous chole kulche to politicians, police officials, bureaucrats and the organisers of the event.</p><p>After the demonetisation move, Urvashi has also started accepting payments through Paytm. We can&#8217;t wait to visit her food truck and hope it starts operating real soon!</p><p><em>Read the full story <a href="http://www.hindustantimes.com/topic/Gurgaon&#039;s-chhole-kulche-women" target="_blank">here</a>.</em></p><p><em>Featured Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Wikimedia Commons</strong></em></p>

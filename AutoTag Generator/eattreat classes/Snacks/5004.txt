5004	Chef Recipe // Chef Saby’s Peach & Brie Grilled Cheese With Cranberry Mustard	<p>Chef Sabyasachi Gorai gives us a great recipe on how to use the beauteous superfruits that are the US Cranberries. This is a chef recipe that you have to keep handy for unannounced guests!</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Elements</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">2 large sized baguettes</p><p style="text-align: center;">15 gms softened butter</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>For the filling</strong></p><p style="text-align: center;">50 gms brie</p><p style="text-align: center;">100 gms soft pears (peeled)</p><p style="text-align: center;">70 ml balsamic vinegar</p><p style="text-align: center;">250 ml cranberry juice</p><p style="text-align: center;">40 gms fresh rosemary</p><p style="text-align: center;">40 gms chopped cranberries</p><p style="text-align: center;">Black pepper to taste</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>For the cranberry mustard sauce</strong></p><p style="text-align: center;">15 gms whole grain mustard</p><p style="text-align: center;">100 gms dried cranberries</p><p style="text-align: center;">20 ml white wine</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula</strong></h4><p><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Poached-Pears-Cranberries.jpg"><img title = "Chef Recipe // Chef Saby&#039;s Peach &amp; Brie Grilled Cheese With Cranberry Mustard  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5008" src="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Poached-Pears-Cranberries.jpg" alt = "sweet snacks Recipes Kitchen Category:Bakery   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Poached-Pears-Cranberries.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Poached-Pears-Cranberries-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Poached-Pears-Cranberries-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p><strong>For the glaze</strong></p><p>Heat balsamic and cranberry juice in a small pan, with finely chopped rosemary and pepper. Once reduced, add generous slices of pear to the pan and coat nicely. Remove from pan once done.</p><p><strong>For the mustard</strong></p><p>Deglaze the same pan with white wine and add fresh cranberries.</p><p>Remove from heat and allow this to cool before blending it to a smooth sauce like consistency.</p><p>Add the mustard to the cranberry sauce. Blend well and set aside to cool.</p><p><strong>Assembly</strong></p><p>Heat a skillet/ grill pan on medium. Butter each side of the baguette, and place the buttered side down on the skillet.</p><p>Layer the brie and chopped cranberries generously on top of the bread, and carefully place the pear segments.</p><p>Drizzle the remaining balsamic reduction on top and cook for another few minutes on medium, till the cheese begins to melt. Serve with the cranberry mustard sauce.</p><p>This chef recipe is perfect for those winter evenings, when you want something snacky.</p>

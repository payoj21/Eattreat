4616	Ek Bar’s Winter Menu Stuns With State-Inspired Dishes & Drinks	<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Ek Bar&#8217;s new food and cocktail menu is going to make you drool and crave for more of their grub. Their new menus are inspired from the lush culture of our country and Indian street food, with delicacies from different states that tell us stories long gone with a concept of ‘once upon a time’.</span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">They’re already known for the vast range of cocktails that they do (YUS Royal Punch),  but the new ones get better having delightful ingredients that are unique and are previously unexperimented. They’ve got a whole new menu of just cocktails and they’re surely going to blow your mind.</span></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Sher Singh</strong></span></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">(Vodka + apple juice + lime + date syrup) This cocktail is a delight for all those who like a sweet tinge to their alcohol. This one’s named after King Sher Singh Suri from the city of Mysore because of the sweet apple flavors in the Cocktail.</span></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Queen Victoria</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">(Whiskey + ginger + mango flavors) Mangoes, a specialty of West Bengal and the name derived from the historic reigning queen of West Bengal. This was the perfect start to the meal because no great journey ever started with a salad!  </span></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Kala Amritsari</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">(Vodka + plum syrup + lime juice + egg white + garnish plum) There couldn’t have been a better combination of Vodka and plum.</span></p><hr /><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Now being a Gastronomical Bar, they’ve got to serve some extraordinary food with an Indian street food twist.  They surely have succeeded and how.</span></p><p><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/11/received_1343930538973523.jpeg"><img title = "Ek Bar&#039;s Winter Menu Stuns With State-Inspired Dishes &amp; Drinks  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4647" src="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/11/received_1343930538973523.jpeg" alt = "street food restaurants regional Indian fast food Category:Snacks bar   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/received_1343930538973523.jpeg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/received_1343930538973523-300x180.jpeg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/received_1343930538973523-768x460.jpeg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Noori Seekh Kebab</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">If you ever have dilemmas of whether to order Mutton or Chicken &#8211; this one’s your saviour.

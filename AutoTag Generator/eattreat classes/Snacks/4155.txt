4155	Hygiene Happy Needs? 5 Places For Street Food In A Restaurant	<p>While we love street food (who in Delhi doesn’t?), we are often reluctant to eat at a roadside joint due to hygiene. And it&#8217;s pretty bad during winters because we are just too lazy to stand out in the cold. So, here is a list of restaurants where you can get a chockful of street side food while sitting in the comfort of a heater.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Imly</strong><b> </b></h4><p>Revolutionising the concept of street food, Imly is all set to open in Gurgaon and serve their patrons a scrumptious plate of golgappas. Their street fare includes dishes from different cities, including Delhi, Mumbai, Madras, Rajasthan, Punjab and Uttar Pradesh. They haven&#8217;t forgotten the pizza lovers and included a “Videshi” section just for you!</p><p>Explore more about Imly <a href="https://www.facebook.com/imlyxpress/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Bikanervala</strong></h4><p>A food-chain so enormous, it&#8217;s spread all over the country and abroad. Pioneers in combining the concept of street food and casual dining, Bikanervala is chain known to all. It’s the first place we head to during Navratri and anytime when we want to eat chaat, dhokla or even chole puri in a proper setting. And on proper seating amirite.</p><p>Explore more about Bikanervala <a href="http://www.bikanervala.com" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Nukkadwala</strong></h4><p><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/11/14695517_1888644458022590_2906273809544736835_n.jpg"><img title = "Hygiene Happy Needs? 5 Places For Street Food In A Restaurant  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4160" src="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/11/14695517_1888644458022590_2906273809544736835_n.jpg" alt = "street food snacks restaurants connaught place Chandni Chowk cafes   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/14695517_1888644458022590_2906273809544736835_n.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/14695517_1888644458022590_2906273809544736835_n-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/14695517_1888644458022590_2906273809544736835_n-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Nukkadwala</strong></em></p><p>Most have heard the name and can relate it to street food, Nukkadwala is an expanding chain which has a limited menu with different kinds of street food. You can head here for a quick meal while working or to get some things packed for home.</p><p>Explore more <a href="https://www.nukkadwala.co.in" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cafe Hawkers </strong></h4><p>Head to Cafe Hawkers for a relaxed evening with friends for chaat, tikki, pakora and phuchkas. A colourful and funky setup, it is located at Delhi’s centre, Connaught place. The name itself will draw you in and make you want to order some delicious street food from different parts of the world. And the best part? It&#8217;s pretty easy on the pocket.</p><p>Explore more <a href="https://www.facebook.com/CafeHawkers/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Lakhori &#8211; Haveli Dharampura</strong></h4><p>A fine-dine restaurant located amidst the hustle and bustle of Chandni Chowk, Lakhori offers an exceptional view, comforting service and delicious street food. A 200-year-old restored Haveli, it is best visited for breakfast to gorge on the classic aloo puri or stuffed paranthas. Don&#8217;t miss the fresh lassi when you are here!</p><p>Explore more <a href="http://www.havelidharampura.com/lakhori/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><p><em>Featured Photo Courtesy Of:<strong> Imly</strong></em></p><p>&nbsp;</p>

3542	Appetising Much? Small Plates Week Comes To Delhi	<p>If, like us, variety intrigues you more than quantity, then Small Plates Week is your kind of celebration! A culinary adventure inspired by tapas-style dining, Small Plates Week is being launched in Delhi for the first time and we couldn&#8217;t be more excited!</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></p><p>Small Plates Week is going to feature 13 of Delhi’s finest informal restaurants, including some of our favourites like Burma Burma, Perch, The Fatty Bao and Ziu. Each restaurant will showcase 16 small plates, 12 savoury and 4 desserts served directly to the table.</p><p>You can order and reorder any of these dishes and sample a large part of the menu. So, no more dilemma of selecting and picking your favourites; just order all! We are also expecting the likes of SodaBottleOpenerWala (both branches), Dhaba by Claridges (Epicuria and Saket), Ek Bar, Masala House, Ping&#8217;s Cafe Orient, Rustom&#8217;s and The Hungry Monkey.</p><p>Being held from 14 &#8211; 23 October, the reservations go live on 10 October, at 12 pm (noon). So keep your laptops ready, because you don&#8217;t want to miss out on this!</p><p>Make reservations <a href="https://www.insider.in/go/small-plates-week" target="_blank">here</a>. Explore more about it <a href="https://www.facebook.com/SmallPlatesWeek/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>

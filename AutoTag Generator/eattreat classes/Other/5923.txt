5923	Amazon’s Got A Wicked New Private Label & We Can’t Wait!	<p>The e-commerce behemoth from the US, Amazon, is going further into the private label business with its new food label called Wickedly Prime.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;">First Things First</h4><p>The business giant had debuted <a href="https://www.amazon.com/b?ref=ae_surl_0&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;node=10166275011&amp;tag=thedrewitpi02-20" target="_blank">Amazon Elements</a> for daily essentials about two years ago, and Wickedly Prime is the first brand that has made affiliation with Amazon known.</p><p>As is evident from the name, this will be exclusive to Amazon Prime Members. They’re going to cater to the foodie crowd with their own line of snacks like popcorn, tortilla chips, and soft shell almonds available in a variety of flavours.</p><p>Amazon has other private labels too, like ‘Presto!’ which is a detergent, ‘Buttoned Down’ that sells formal dress shirts for men, ‘Happy Belly’ handles basics like nuts, tea, and cooking oil; and ‘Mama Bear’ is meant for baby products.</p><p><em>Read the full story at <a href="http://www.indiaretailing.com/2016/12/26/food/food-grocery/amazon-introduces-new-private-food-label-wickedly-prime/" target="_blank">IndiaRetailing</a>.</em></p>

3735	Organic Ice-Cream, Honey, And More At The Women Of India Festival	<p>The Women Of India Festival is on, and it is featuring hundreds of products by women entrepreneurs at Dilli Haat near INA Market.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></p><p>The Women Of India Festival will continue till 23rd October. They have about 230 stalls and 372 participants who’ve brought an array of items to the festival, including organic products like honey, tea, oils, kitchen composters, and even ice-cream. The list of participants includes Delhi and 23 states.</p><p>The festival, organised by the Ministry of Women And Child Development, is a move to benefit women entrepreneurs and also to promote organic food and products. It is intended to highlight the advantages of organic products to health and the environment since they’ve been proven to contain more vitamins, minerals, and nutrients than similar foods produced with chemical fertilisers, pesticides, and preservatives.</p><p>The festival also provides a platform for women engaged in this economy and encourage the development of sustainable and easily accessible sales outlets for organic producers from remote areas.</p><p><em>Read the full story <a href="http://netindian.in/news/2016/10/15/00039877/organic-products-display-%E2%80%98women-india-festival-2016%E2%80%99-delhi" target="_blank">here</a>.</em></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Pexels</strong></em></p>

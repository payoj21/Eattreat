3415	3 Reasons To Celebrate The Festive Season At The Goddess Festival	<p><a href="https://www.facebook.com/devanghouse/" target="_blank">Devang House</a> is back with something exciting yet again and this time it is The Goddess Festival which will have everything from food, workshops, music and more. Navratras don&#8217;t need to be boring with fasting and unhealthy food. Here&#8217;s everything you can do instead:</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Goddess-Themed Yoga Nights</strong></h4><p><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/10/unnamed-2.jpg"><img title = "3 Reasons To Celebrate The Festive Season At The Goddess Festival  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3418" src="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/10/unnamed-2.jpg" alt = "vegan Organic festival Chanakyapuri New Delhi   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/unnamed-2.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/unnamed-2-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/unnamed-2-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p>Keeping the essence of Devang behind each of their events, such as organic food, healthy living, community, and the celebration of Indian culture, they&#8217;ve come up with a bunch of events like the Yoga Nights. Learn some of the most effective moves with Mansi Mahajan, an internationally renowned yogi and martial artist.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Navratri-Themed Brunches</strong></h4><p>Apart from doing their usual Organic Brunch and Pop Up, Devang is doing a pretty great line-up of Navratri-themed stuff. Each day has a different Navratri Thali and they are also organising a Vegan Indian Cooking with the executive chef. Learn how to make a <strong>Puffed Rice and Roasted Channa Snack</strong>, <strong>Kerala Banana Kebab with Coriander Chutney</strong>, <strong>Saboodana (Tapioca) Khichdi</strong> and <strong>Pumpkin Halva</strong>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Musical Concerts</strong></h4><p>They are also hosting a bunch of really soothing musical sessions such as an acoustic one with Azaan Khan.

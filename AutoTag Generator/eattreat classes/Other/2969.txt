2969	#EatTreatDeets: A Food Plaza Is Coming To Barakhamba Metro Station	<p>The Delhi Metro is going a step ahead this time and giving us an entire food plaza at the Barakhamba Metro station of the Blue Line. So Noida guys and Dwarka peeps rejoice! For you will be able to access the&nbsp;Barakhamba Food Plaza in all its glory.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></p><p>According to <a href="http://www.thehindu.com/todays-paper/tp-national/tp-newdelhi/soon-a-food-plaza-at-busy-barakhamba-station/article9120075.ece" target="_blank">this article</a> by <i>The Hindu</i>, The DMRC is planning to set up the facility in the land right next to the station. A metro official told <i>The Hindu</i>, that they have 2,400 sqft of land and that the proposal says that it will “be given on lease basis where the lessee will have to develop, operate and maintain the food plaza or cafeteria”.</p><p>Targeting office-goers and corporate workers, DMRC is also going to provide the commenters with a seating area and a place to relax. So we are looking forward to this single storey coming up. Move over Rajiv Chowk, Barakhamba is where it’s at.</p><p>Read more about this change <a href="http://www.thehindu.com/todays-paper/tp-national/tp-newdelhi/soon-a-food-plaza-at-busy-barakhamba-station/article9120075.ece" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Wikipedia Commons</strong></em></p>

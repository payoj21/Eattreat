3763	Asian Hawker’s Market Is Here, And Here’s A List Of Everything To Gorge On	<p>India’s biggest Asian food festival is here for the third time in two years &#8211; Asian Hawker&#8217;s Market. The last season had <strong>Litchi Wantons</strong>, <strong>Pan-Fried Momos</strong>, <strong>Okonomiyaki Pancakes</strong>, <strong>Char Sui Baos</strong>, and yummy cocktails. We’re literally daydreaming right now.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></p><p>As in the previous two seasons, AHM will have 21 stalls and 14 carts with small plates, easy-on-the-wallet prices, and a careful curation of participants. There will be live entertainment, including the flash mobs that everyone loved. As usual, it is full of surprises and will see participation from some of the most well-known, award-winning restaurants and chefs in the industry.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>New Comers</strong></h4><p><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/10/Authentic-Asian-flavours-at-Asian-Hawkers-Market.jpg"><img title = "Asian Hawker&#039;s Market Is Here, And Here&#039;s A List Of Everything To Gorge On  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3765" src="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/10/Authentic-Asian-flavours-at-Asian-Hawkers-Market.jpg" alt = "Thai festival fast food Chinese asian   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/Authentic-Asian-flavours-at-Asian-Hawkers-Market.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/Authentic-Asian-flavours-at-Asian-Hawkers-Market-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/Authentic-Asian-flavours-at-Asian-Hawkers-Market-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p>If you’re on a diet, it is sure to go flying out of the window because of the endless list of ambrosial delicacies for you to try. The debutants include <strong>Jom Jom Malay</strong>, <strong>East</strong> &#8211; the popular Pan-Asian restaurant of Westin Gurgaon, <strong>Wok Tok</strong> from The Grand New Delhi, <strong>Ping’s Café Orient</strong>, and <strong>Burma Burma</strong> is bringing dishes from their award-winning vegetarian menu. You’re also going to see <strong>Wai Wai City</strong> bring the many versions of their ever-popular instant noodles after their first foray into the quick service restaurant space.</p><p>Another noteworthy addition to this edition is <strong>Noshi</strong>, the home-delivery brand of the phenomenally successful, Yum Yum Cha.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Old Favourites</strong></h4><p><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/10/Benihana-at-Asian-Hawkers-Market-2016.jpg"><img title = "Asian Hawker&#039;s Market Is Here, And Here&#039;s A List Of Everything To Gorge On  Asian Hawker&#039;s Market Is Here, And Here&#039;s A List Of Everything To Gorge On  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3766" src="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/10/Benihana-at-Asian-Hawkers-Market-2016.jpg" alt = "Thai festival fast food Chinese asian   Thai festival fast food Chinese asian   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/Benihana-at-Asian-Hawkers-Market-2016.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/Benihana-at-Asian-Hawkers-Market-2016-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/Benihana-at-Asian-Hawkers-Market-2016-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p>Zoravar Kalra’s <strong>Pa Pa Ya</strong> is back yet again all the way from Mumbai with their modernist Pan Asian cuisine, and you will also see your other favorites like the live teppanyaki grill from <strong>Benihana</strong>, the baos from <strong>Fatty Bao</strong> that wowed everyone so much that they all sold out quickly, and the always in-demand sandwiches from <strong>BigFatSandwich</strong>. We’re also excited that <strong>Guppy</strong> is coming back for this edition with their famed sushi rolls, and <strong>Asian Haus</strong> is getting their katsu curry!</p><p>In short, you need to block the weekend of the 21 October and postpone everything else and attend the Asian Hawker’s Market. Come armed with stretchy pants and a huge appetite for some yummy food and delectable drinks.</p><p>On from 21st Oct to 23rd Oct.<br />Explore Asian Hawker&#8217;s Market <a href="https://www.facebook.com/asianhawkersmarket/?fref=ts" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>

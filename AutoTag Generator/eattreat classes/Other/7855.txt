7855	Gourmet Treats, Artisan Meats & Everything New That’s Coming To Palate Fest	<p>We’re counting down the days hours to <a href="http://eattreat.in/magazine/8-street-food-stalls-at-palate-fest/" target="_blank">the Palate Fest</a> and we’re more than just ecstatic about all the new brands, restaurants, and bakeries that are bringing their smorgasbord of delights to the fest. With all of these mind-blowing gourmet and street food options, amazing music, and more lined up for this edition, it’s safe to say that the Palate Fest got more spectacular than ever. We&#8217;re telling you all that&#8217;s new at Palate Fest that you absolutely cannot miss out on.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>1. And if that&#8217;s not all, <strong>Twisted Tacos</strong> is bringing more Mexican food. And guess what, the <strong>Ethiopian Embassy</strong> is bringing their colourful culture in the form of bite-sized delicacies as well.</p><p><strong>Big Fat Sandwich</strong>, <strong>Blue Tokai</strong>, and <strong>Wok Me</strong> are all coming to the Palate Fest together with their beauteous sandwiches, lovely noodle boxes, and coffee that&#8217;s going to get you through the crazy slew of activities planned for each day!</p><p>Catch <strong>Artisan Meats</strong> grilling sausages and flippin’ some burgers made with their gourmet meats, and<strong> Jamie’s Italian</strong> tossing some mouth watering pizzas. <strong>Bunta Bar</strong>, <strong>Circus</strong>, and <strong>Elation</strong> are bringing their fusion specialities as well. If you only need to grab a bite before you make your way over to <a href="http://eattreat.in/magazine/activities-at-the-palate-fest/" target="_blank">Imagine Fest next door</a>, you can go to <strong>Caara</strong>&#8216;s stall where they&#8217;ve got their famous pesto and some gorgeous looking gourmet delights.</p><p>&nbsp;</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>2.

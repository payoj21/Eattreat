7011	Why Hate On Gobi Masala When You Can Make This Offbeat One	<p>The humble gobi masala gets a lot of flak for being too boring. <a href="http://eattreat.in/cauliflower-soup-with-bacon-recipe/">Cauliflower infused with spice</a>s, what can be so great about that? This offbeat gobi masala recipe can spice up your regular meals.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Serves: </strong>3-4</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prep Time: </strong>10 minutes</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cook Time: </strong>15-20 minutes</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Elements:</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">1 teaspoon cayenne pepper</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 teaspoon turmeric powder</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 tablespoon gram flour (besan)</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 teaspoon ground coriander</p><p style="text-align: center;">120 grams (1/2 cup) creamed coconut, dissolved in 200ml boiling water</p><p style="text-align: center;">Salt to taste</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 tablespoon vegetable oil</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 teaspoon black mustard seeds</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 large cauliflower, cut into florets</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 tablespoon fresh coriander leaves, chopped</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula:</strong></h4><p>In a bowl add the cayenne pepper, turmeric powder, gram flour, ground coriander and salt to the creamed coconut water and whisk till it forms a paste. Set this to the side.</p><p>Heat oil in a pan and add the mustard seeds, once they begin to splutter add the cauliflower florets and mix well. Now add the paste that had been set aside and continue to mix well, cook until the cauliflower becomes tender but not too soft. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with warm naan or rice!</p><p>Also check out this awesome <a href="http://eattreat.in/cauliflower-soup-with-bacon-recipe/">Cauliflower Bacon Soup</a>.</p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of:Â <strong>Madishetty Manasa</strong></em></p>

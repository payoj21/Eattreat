3499	Coming Soon! RAW Pressery May Use Leftovers To Make Chips & Petfood	<p>RAW Pressery uses the freshest and most organic fruits and vegetables for making delicious and healthy cold press juices. They now plan to reuse the leftovers to minimise waste in innovative ways, especially with healthy snacks.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></p><p>After having garnered orders that go up to about 1,00,000 bottles per month, RAW Pressery is planning to make edible items out of the waste from the fruits and vegetables that go into the making of their cold pressed juices.</p><p><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/10/14492349_570527563140089_4996312080616653913_n.jpg"><img title = "Coming Soon! They feel that there will be enough takers for a healthier, natural alternative to processed food and we can’t help but agree with them.</p><p>RAW Pressery has already tied up with some of the farmers to sell them fruits and vegetables at economic rates for it to be turned into manure.

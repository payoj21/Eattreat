3138	An INR 1,600 Crore Patanjali Food Park Is Coming To Noida	<p>After expanding into ‘shuddh desi swadeshi’ jeans and plans to start their own dairy farm, Patanjali Ayurved is going to establish a food-park in Noida.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></p><p><em>The Financial Express</em> reported that “Patanjali Ayurved, promoted by yoga guru Ramdev, may announce an investment of Rs 1,600 crore shortly for setting up a herbal food park in Noida in the poll-bound Uttar Pradesh to meet its domestic as well as global demand.”</p><p>This food-processing plant and herbal park will be set up sometime during Diwali and honestly, we aren&#8217;t very sure how we feel about this. While a lot of naysayers are constantly putting the brand down, their exponential growth is obviously quite commendable.</p><p>Now, Patanjali may be capitalising on the health fads that are currently overturning the field of F&amp;B, the actual results of the products are questionable. But whatever said, the <em>PTI</em> has reported that officials have confirmed the development and the identification of land is at a progressed stage.</p><p>The only pros that we can see in this situation is the employment of 10,000 youths and soft loans to farmers in surrounding areas for producing various agricultural products.

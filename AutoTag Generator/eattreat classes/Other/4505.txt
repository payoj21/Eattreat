4505	World Cuisine Spot Tourist Opens Doors In Janpath For The Explorer At Heart	<p>The makers of Unplugged Courtyard have brought a new world cuisine watering hole in town. Tourist, a street food bar, is the latest tavern for those who yearn for adventure, with the journey of a tourist as its theme. The cuisine at Tourist brings together street food flavours from the hidden corners of the world.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/11/AMINABAD-KI-GALOUTI.jpg"><img title = "World Cuisine Spot Tourist Opens Doors In Janpath For The Explorer At Heart  "class="alignnone wp-image-4512 size-full" src="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/11/AMINABAD-KI-GALOUTI.jpg" alt = "street food restaurants new italian global Category:Snacks bar american   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/AMINABAD-KI-GALOUTI.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/AMINABAD-KI-GALOUTI-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/AMINABAD-KI-GALOUTI-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p>The restaurant is spread lavishly across three floors with luxurious interiors. Their doors opened in Janpath only recently, and they’re inviting anyone who is a voyager and adventurer at heart. You can surely look out for some delectable dishes like the Burmese <strong>Khao Suey</strong>, <strong>Aminabad ke Galouti</strong>, and even your favourites from Mumbai.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/TouristJanpath/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><p><em>Photos Courtesy Of: <strong>Tourist</strong> </em></p>

7013	Bacon In Your Cauliflower Soup? Here’s Something Interesting To Try	<p>Hate the idea of cauliflower in your meal? Well, we might make you fall in love with this unexciting veggie. Low in carbs, hearty and flavourful; the combination of cauliflower soup<a class="m_7182303093289169403gmail-spGb-f"> with bacon recipe</a> is a sure win-win! (yeah, bacon can make pretty much anything and everything yummy). Though this soup essentially has a velvety texture, adding a few dollops of cream with only make this cauli-wonder creamier!</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Serves:</strong> 4</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Total time:</strong> 45 minutes</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Elements:</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">2-tablespoon olive oil</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 cup thinly sliced leeks, (only the whit and light green part)</p><p style="text-align: center;">4 cloves garlic, pressed</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 large potato, peeled and diced</p><p style="text-align: center;">3 ½ cups chopped cauliflower florets</p><p style="text-align: center;">3 cups chicken broth</p><p style="text-align: center;">¼ teaspoon ground black pepper</p><p style="text-align: center;">100 grams bacon</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ cup gorgonzola blue cheese</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 onion, thinly sliced</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 teaspoon chives for garnish</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula:</strong></h4><p>In a large saucepan, heat oil on medium-low. Add leeks and cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes. Add garlic and cook, stirring frequently, for 1 minute. Add potato, cauliflower, broth, and pepper and increase heat to bring to a boil; reduce heat to low, cover and simmer until potatoes and cauliflower are very tender, 20 minutes. Remove from heat.</p><p>In the mean while take the bacon and heat it a skillet until golden brown and cooked to desired crispiness. Chop and set aside.</p><p>With an immersion blender, purée soup until smooth. (carefully transfer soup to a blender, in batches if necessary)</p><p>Return the soup puree back to the saucepan and on low heat. Stir in the cheese and bacon at this stage. Cook this for 5 minutes.</p><p>Garnish with chopped chives and bacon.</p><p><strong>#EatExtra</strong> Cauliflower, a cruciferous vegetable, contains an array of phytonutrients including beta-carotene, quercetin and ferulic acid, all of which help reduce oxidative stress in the body. So this cauliflower soup with bacon recipe is all you need.</p>

3535	Healthier Version Of Your Favourite Shahi Paneer With Honey & Almonds	<p>We present to you, a healthier rendition of the absolutely delicious Shahi Paneer for you to indulge in some yummy goodness without having to worry so much about the calories.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Serves:</strong> 2-3</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prep Time:</strong> 15 minutes</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cook Time:</strong> 20 minutes</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Elements:</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">1 cup tomato, finely chopped</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ cup onions, finely sliced</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ cup cashew nuts</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 dry red chillies</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 ½ cup water</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 tablespoons oil</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 teaspoon garlic paste</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 teaspoon cinnamon powder</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 cloves garlic, chopped</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 cardamoms</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 bay leaf</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 teaspoons dried fenugreek leaves</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 teaspoons garam masala</p><p style="text-align: center;">4 tablespoons tomato puree</p><p style="text-align: center;">Salt, to taste</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 tablespoons yoghurt</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ teaspoon honey</p><p style="text-align: center;">¼ cup water</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 cup paneer (cottage cheese), cubed</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 tablespoons almond cream/cooking cream</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula:</strong></h4><p>In a pan, add all the ingredients for the gravy and set to boil on a high flame for 10-15 minutes. keep stirring until the tomatoes soften. Remove from the heat and set aside to cool.</p><p>Now, place this mixture into a blender and grind until you have a fine smooth paste. Keep this to the side.</p><p>Heat oil in a pan. Add the garlic paste and sauté on medium heat for about a minute. Now add the cinnamon, cloves, cardamom and bay leaf and continue to sauté for close to a minute.</p><p>Now throw in the dried fenugreek leaves, garam masala, tomato puree and the gravy paste you had set aside. Give everything a good stir and continue to mix for about 2-3 minutes on medium heat.</p><p>Sprinkle in the salt, then stir in the yoghurt and make sure to keep stirring so everything mixes well together.</p><p>Pour the honey, water, paneer and almond cream/cream. Mix well and continue to cook on medium heat for 2-5 minutes and your delicious Shahi Paneer is ready.</p><p>Garnish with a tablespoon of almond cream on top and serve hot with warm naan (butter naan for that extra kick) or plain rice.</p><p><strong>#EatExtra:</strong> In this recipe, the cream, butter, and sugar is replaced with honey and almond cream. You can use what it originally calls for if you like.</p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Madishetty Manasa</strong></em></p>

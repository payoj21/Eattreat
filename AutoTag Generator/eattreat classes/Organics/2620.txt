2620	#EatEffect // 7 Foods That Will Re-energize Your Hair & Stop Hair Loss	<p>With the festive season coming in, and the endless parties and functions (need we mention, weddings?) that you’re going to attend one after the other; your skin and hair are going to take a toll. Here are a few foods that you should include in your regular diet for you to have healthy, lustrous hair and stop hair loss, all at the same time..</p><h4 style="text-align: center;">1. Oats</h4><p>Oats are rich in silica, which make your hair healthy and strong, but also shine and silky. You can start your day off with a bowl of oats, milk and fruits.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;">2. Eggs</h4><p>Egg whites contain biotin, which works against brittle hair and hair loss. They are also rich in cysteine, which increases the thickness of hair.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;">3. Spinach</h4><p>Leafy greens such as spinach are nutritious and filled with antioxidants. Spinach is also a great source of iron, the lack of which causes hair loss and damage.</p><p><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/09/photo-1445445096960-c6fd1a3b901e.jpeg"><img title = "#EatEffect // 7 Foods That Will Re-energize Your Hair &amp; Stop Hair Loss  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2629" src="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/09/photo-1445445096960-c6fd1a3b901e.jpeg" alt = "Organic ingredients healthy detox Category:Organics   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/photo-1445445096960-c6fd1a3b901e.jpeg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/photo-1445445096960-c6fd1a3b901e-300x180.jpeg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/photo-1445445096960-c6fd1a3b901e-768x460.jpeg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;">4. Pumpkin</h4><p style="text-align: left;">Pumpkins are rich in vitamin A, which is responsible for sebum production. It acts as a natural conditioner and promotes a healthy scalp.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;">5. Salmon</h4><p>Salmon is a great source of both Protein and Vitamin D, which are both crucial for strong hair. Additionally, the omega-3 fatty acids found in this fish help in keeping the scalp hydrated and healthy.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;">6. Sweet Potato</h4><p>Sweet potatoes boost your hair growth and contain beta-carotene with fights against dry, dull, dandruff-prone hair and scalp.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;">7. Walnuts</h4><p>Rich in biotin, Vitamin E, and omega-3 fatty acids, these nuts protect your hair from sun damage and prevent hair loss. Walnuts also contain copper, which retains the natural, rich colour of your hair.</p><hr /><p>This article is part of the EatTreat campaign to eat better and live more consciously.

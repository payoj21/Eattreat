4172	Stay Hydrated: Eat Your Water Instead Of Drinking It	<p>If you are one of those people who don&#8217;t feel thirsty or just take less water than required, it&#8217;s time to compensate for the low H2O levels in your body. Increase your water intake through these healthy fruits to avoid skin dryness with winter on its way!</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Orange</strong></h4><p>Containing 87% water, gulping an orange while soaking in the sun, is a quintessential winter experience. A great way to increase your water and fiber intake, oranges are healthy and tangy-licious! Yes, kumquats are fruits lesser known to Indian people. Its part of the citrus family rarely grown in India, but often imported from the neighbouring countries like Nepal and China. A funky fruit which you can eat without peeling, skin on! High in vitamin C and water content it is believed to improve skin health.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>CranberriesÂ </strong></h4><p>Hard to get hold of, cranberries are just smacking delicious!

6576	Burn The Fat Without Burning A Hole In Your Pocket At These Places In Indirapuram	<p>Off late, a lot of eateries have opened in the friendly neighbourhood that is Indirapuram, with a few of them dedicated only to serving healthy food. The best part is that the food at these places is not only delicious, but the prices don&#8217;t burn a hole in your pocket! We&#8217;ve picked the three spots you need to check off your list for healthy food in Indirapuram.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Health Buzzz</strong></h4><p>They not only have a wide variety of items for you to pick from, but they also mention the calorific and nutritional value on the menu. So, even if you’ve been hitting the gym to work out you can plan your meals accordingly. They’ve got great options for vegans, vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike. You can even get their combo packs and they’ll deliver your choice of meal each day at the time that you specify to them.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> Shop 23, Ground Floor, Omaxe Plaza, Shakti Khand 2, Indirapuram, Ghaziabad</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> +91 9971551291 &amp; +91 9821932412</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 350 for two people (approx.).</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/hbuzzzdotcom/?hc_ref=SEARCH&amp;fref=nf1" target="_blank">Health Buzzz</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Khopchas</strong></h4><p>This is the newest in this area.

4614	Healthy Snacks That’ll Help With Your Memory This Exam Season	<p>Are upcoming university exams haunting your nights and squeezing the fun out of  your days? Semester exams are the upcoming biggie across India and we&#8217;re helping you out with your prep munchies, so try out these healthy snacks that you can munch in between your study breaks.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Egg Chaat</strong></h4><p>Known for its calcium properties, it’s a great mood lifter (who doesn’t like <strong>egg bhurji</strong>?) and the choline in it helps in making your brains sharper than it already is (especially during exam season)! Healthy snacks like <strong>egg chaat</strong> only requires sliced boiled eggs, red chillies, onions, coriander, tomato, salt and a drizzle of olive oil.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Dark Chocolate</strong></h4><p>Chocolate doesn&#8217;t ask questions, chocolate understands. You deserve something sweet after rattling your brain for hours right? Ditch that high-calorie brownie and indulge in the seductive dark chocolates that improve blood flow to the heart as well as the brain.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Green Leafy Veggies</strong></h4><p>Winter season&#8217;s coming up and so will be mustard leaves (sarso) hitting the markets soon! Get a whole handi of the yummy sarso saag made and chomp away till makke di roti do us part. Other green veggies like spinach and kale are certainly good for your memory because of the magic vitamin content.

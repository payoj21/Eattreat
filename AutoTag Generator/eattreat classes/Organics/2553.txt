2553	Devang House’s Artichoke, Bean & Sun-dried Tomato Salad Recipe	<p>To enjoy the <a href="https://www.devang.house/blogs/news/top-6-reasons-to-have-apple-cider-vinegar-everyday">numerous benefits</a> of apple cider vinegar, try it in this hearty salad straight from the kitchens of Devang House! The European flavours of basil, sun-dried tomato and artichoke are balanced with a sweet and tart dressing of brown sugar and apple cider vinegar.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Serves:</strong> 3-4</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prep Time:</strong> 15 mins</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cook Time:</strong> 5 mins</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Elements:</strong></p><p style="text-align: center;">1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 cup French beans, blanched</p><p style="text-align: center;">30 grams or 2 tablespoons red bell peppers, roasted and diced</p><p style="text-align: center;">10 leaves basil, hand torn</p><p style="text-align: center;">15 grams or 1 tablespoon parsley, chopped</p><p style="text-align: center;">15 ml or 1 tablespoon lemon juice</p><p style="text-align: center;">15 ml or 1 tablespoon <a href="https://www.devang.house/collections/tonics/products/apple-cider-vinegar" target="_blank">apple cider vinegar</a></p><p style="text-align: center;">5 grams or 1 teaspoon brown sugar</p><p style="text-align: center;">5 grams or 1 teaspoon pepper</p><p style="text-align: center;">15 grams or 1 tablespoon parmesan cheese, grated</p><p style="text-align: center;">10 grams or 2 teaspoons black olives, chopped</p><p style="text-align: center;">30 ml or 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil</p><p style="text-align: center;">Salt, to taste</p><p style="text-align: center;">30 grams or 2 tablespoons garlic, peeled and chopped</p><p style="text-align: center;">5 grams or 1 teaspoon thyme</p><p style="text-align: center;">8 whole artichoke hearts</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula:</strong></p><p>Gather all the ingredients and process vegetables.</p><p>In a bowl, add the sun-dried tomatoes, French beans, bell peppers, basil, parsley, lemon juice, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, pepper, half of the parmesan cheese, black olives, half of the extra virgin olive oil and salt. Whisk together well.</p><p>In a hot pan, sauté garlic in remaining olive oil, add thyme and chargrill the artichoke. Once complete, toss all the ingredients together.</p><p>Plate the salad and finish with remaining Parmesan cheese and a sprig of parsley.</p><p>Devang recommends you buy all ingredients organic for a healthy and nourishing meal for you and the planet.

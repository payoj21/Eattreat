4548	6 Friendly Vegan Restaurants In Delhi To Help With Our New Year Resolutions	<p>Veganism is a lifestyle choice that many are adopting off late, what with the growing awareness of the treatment towards farm animals and the different ways that one can make a difference to the way we give back to the environment. Here are some vegan restaurants in Delhi we head to when the going gets tough on the meaty path.</p><p>We at EatTreat are talking about the <a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//eateffect-greenisthenewblack-eat-green/" target="_blank">#EatEffect</a> this week, and we’re telling you the best places in Delhi where you can get vegan-friendly meals.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cafe Shunya</strong></h4><p>This newly opened place at Cross Point Mall in Gurgaon is not just aesthetically pleasing, but they also serve vegan-friendly and vegetarian food. One must get some of their many detox teas to wind down after a long day. Drop in if you&#8217;re anywhere close to this side of town and gorge on some yummy treats from here, all made from locally sourced ingredients. They&#8217;ve got vegan-friendly dessert options too!</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 115, 1st floor, Cross Point Mall, Gurgaon<br /><strong>Say hello at</strong> 0124 4072555 &amp; +91 9911570655<br />Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/cafeshunya/?fref=ts" target="_blank">Cafe Shunya</a>.<br /><strong>Pay</strong> INR 750 for two people (approx.).</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Greenr Cafe</strong></h4><p><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Greenr.jpg"><img title = "6 Friendly Vegan Restaurants In Delhi To Help With Our New Year Resolutions  "class="alignnone wp-image-4549 size-full" src="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Greenr.jpg" alt = "vegan Organic healthy food healthy Category:Organics cafes   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Greenr.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Greenr-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Greenr-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p>This cute little cafe is situated in the winding lanes of Shahpur Jat and is one of those places where you can find vegan food that doesn’t make you want to cringe. This is one of our favourite vegan cafes in Delhi.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> The Ashok, 50-B, Kautilya Marg, Diplomatic Enclave, Chanakyapuri, New Delhi<br /><strong>Say hello at</strong> +91 9599681082<br />Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Devang-House-936302949829283/?fref=ts" target="_blank">Devang House</a>.<br /><b>Pay</b> INR 1,300 for two people (approx.).</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Rose Café</strong></h4><p>Rose Café, the quaint breakfast &amp; brunch joint in Saket, has a whole lot of vegan options to choose from. Not really known as one of the vegan restaurants in Delhi, we love their vegan offerings.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 1, Westend Marg, Saidulajab, Saket, New Delhi<br /><strong>Say hello</strong> at 011 65991116<br />Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/rosecafenewdelhi/" target="_blank">Rose Café </a><br /><b>Pay</b> INR 1,000 for two people (approx.).</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Smoke House Deli</strong></h4><p>Smoke House Deli has launched a healthy menu that has organic healthy ingredients and superfoods.

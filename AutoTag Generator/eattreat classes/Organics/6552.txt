6552	Banana & Prune Porridge : Your Breakfast With A Twist	<p>Our twist to the traditional porridge, this Banana &amp; Prune Porridge is all kinds of healthy and happy. Instead of your usual bland fare, we recommend you try this. It&#8217;s a healthy breakfast recipe and easy to make and you don&#8217;t need all kinds of gourmet ingredients to whip this up!</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Serves:</strong> 2-3</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prep Time</strong><b>: </b> 5 mins</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cook Time:</strong> 12-15 mins</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Elements</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">1 banana, mashed</p><p style="text-align: center;">4 prunes, roughly chopped</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 cups milk</p><p style="text-align: center;">¼ cup sugar</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 vanilla pod, deseeded</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 cup oats</p><p style="text-align: center;">A<b> </b>blender</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula</strong></h4><p>In a blender, add the banana and prunes and blend until mushy. Set aside.</p><p>Pour the milk into a saucepan, stir in the banana-prune mixture and simmer on low heat for five to seven minutes. Now mix in the sugar and the vanilla, add the oats and cook for another five minutes. Cool for about five minutes and serve.</p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of:<b> Pixabay </b></em></p>

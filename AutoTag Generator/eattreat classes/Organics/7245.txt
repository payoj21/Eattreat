7245	Try This Amaranth & Pumpkin Seed Pesto Recipe To Liven Up Snack Time	<p>Get your dose of greens in the yummiest way possible! This amaranth and pumpkin seed pesto recipe is one of the most offbeat recipes we&#8217;ve ever tried and of course, we had to share it. This is a healthy dip that you can eat with crackers or pita chips if you&#8217;re hungry.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Serves:</strong> 3</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prep Time:</strong> 15 mins</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cook Time:</strong> 10 mins</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Elements</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">1 bunch amaranth leaves</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 bunch mint leaves</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 bunch parsley</p><p style="text-align: center;">2-3 cloves garlic</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ cup pumpkin seeds</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 tablespoons olive oil</p><p style="text-align: center;">Sea salt to taste</p><p style="text-align: center;">Freshly ground black pepper to taste</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula</strong></h4><p>In a medium mixer, add the amaranth leaves, parsley, mint, pumpkin seeds, garlic and olive oil. Blend till you achieve a pesto-like consistency, before seasoning with salt and pepper. Serve with oven-fresh garlic bread.</p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of:<b> </b><strong>Madishetty Manasa</strong></em></p>

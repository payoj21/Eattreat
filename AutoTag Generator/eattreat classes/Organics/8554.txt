8554	Salad With Candied Pears For A Green Clean Day Of Healthy Eating	<p>A bowlful&nbsp;of zest to keep fresh! Make this healthy green salad recipe with pears for when you just want to take a day off from eating oily food, and just need a little something for your pre-workout fix.</p><p><strong>Serves:</strong> 2</p><p><strong>Prep Time:</strong> 45 minutes&nbsp;</p><p><strong>Cook Time:</strong> 20 minutes&nbsp;</p><h4><strong>Ingredients:</strong></h4><p>100 gms mixed salad leaves (of your choosing)</p><p>8-10 red radishes, thinly sliced&nbsp;</p><p><strong>For the candied pears</strong></p><p>2 small pears, sliced very thin</p><p>5 tablespoons granulated sugar</p><p><strong>For the dressing</strong></p><p>10-12 mint leaves, roughly chopped</p><p>1 tablespoon honey</p><p>Â½ lemon, juiced</p><h4><strong>Method:</strong></h4><p><strong>For the candied pears</strong></p><p>Preheat the oven to 175 degrees Celsius and line your tray with a baking sheet. Place the sugar on a plate and evenly coat the pears on all sides.&nbsp;</p><p>Place the sugared pears on the tray and bake for 10 minutes each side. Take out of the oven and set aside to cool for at least 30 minutes. The pears will harden a little as they cool.</p><p><strong>For the dressing</strong></p><p>Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl.</p><p>Place the salad leaves and the radish in a salad bowl. Arrange the candied pears on top, pour over the dressing and toss lightly.</p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Rakhee Yadav</strong></em></p>

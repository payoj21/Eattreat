5586	These Warm Comfy Meal Bowls In Delhi Are Comfort Food Goals	<p>The existence of meal bowls makes our work day that much easier, for we don&#8217;t need to spend much time to get a meal to our tables. And without making the effort of prepping and cooking dishes. It saves on time and a whole lot of effort. The best part about it is that you can get yours anywhere to dig in. You don&#8217;t require a table and a million different types of cutlery to eat your meal in peace. We&#8217;ve got a list of places for you to check out for you to make your life a little bit easier.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Café Shunya</strong></h4><p>Barely 40 days old, they&#8217;ve already begun to get repeat customers for the healthy vegan/vegetarian options that they have to offer.

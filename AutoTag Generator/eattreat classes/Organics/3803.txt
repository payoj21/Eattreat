3803	17 Places For Soya Chaap In Delhi That Are As Good As Butter Chicken Any Day	<p>We believe that a plate of this can successfully hold a candle to any butter chicken place. But the jury&#8217;s out and we would like you to go and try each of these out at these places for soya chaap in Delhi and tell us how it was.</p><h4 style="text-align: left;"><strong>1. The <strong>Butter Chaap Roll</strong> is on our to-eat list but the <strong>Soya Malai Chaap</strong> is juicy and spicy with flavour oozing out. It comes slathered in a white sauce and we can assure you that we died and went to heaven. And then came back for the momos.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> M-69, Opposite Yes Bank, M Block Market, Greater Kailash (GK) 1, New Delhi</p><p><span style="line-height: 1.5;"><strong>Say hello at</strong>&nbsp;09958270290, +91 9953828571, +91 9953828470</span></p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 120 for one plate</p><p><span style="line-height: 1.5;">Explore more </span><a style="line-height: 1.5;" href="https://www.facebook.com/hungerstrike786/?fref=ts">here</a><span style="line-height: 1.5;">.&nbsp;</span></p><h4><strong>2. Chawla’s</strong></h4><p>The <strong>Crum Chaap</strong> by Chawla’s is a dish to behold. This is a&nbsp;bucket that reminds us of the 8-pc KFC bucket. What was it that we loved? One of those&nbsp;places for soya chaap in Delhi that we keep going back to.</p><p><strong>Head to &nbsp;</strong>G-5, Usha Chamber, New Rajdhani Enclave, Preet Vihar, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at&nbsp;</strong>011 22412000, 011 65788478, +919899933584, +917042883066, +917042884066.</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 180 for one plate&nbsp;</p><p>Explore <a href="http://chawlas2.com">Chawla&#8217;s</a></p><h4><strong>3.

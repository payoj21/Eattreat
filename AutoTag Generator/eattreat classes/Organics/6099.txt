6099	Get Fit This 2017 With Bicycle-Themed Ciclo Cafes Soon Opening In Gurgaon	<p>TI Cycles, in a joint venture with Absolute Speciality Foods Chennai Pvt Limited is set to expand its chain of Ciclo Cafes (bicycle cafes). The Chennai-based joint venture company had set up its first Ciclo Cafe in Chennai in 2015 and is following up with what it calls their biggest such bicycle café in the world in Hyderabad, Gurgaon and more.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>New Cafes</strong></h4><p>Soon to be launched in January 2017 are cafes in <strong>Gurgaon</strong>, and two more in <strong>Bengaluru</strong> and <strong>Coimbatore</strong> respectively by April next. Ciclo Café provides a relaxed and friendly environment with the cycling lifestyle as a backdrop for lovers of cycling to spend time with their families, with simple café food.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Bicycle Market</strong></h4><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/15697415_1196755743749234_6700057585619264880_n.jpg"><img title = "Get Fit This 2017 With Bicycle-Themed Ciclo Cafes Soon Opening In Gurgaon  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6127" src="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/15697415_1196755743749234_6700057585619264880_n.jpg" alt = "snacks News new in town healthy Category:Snacks cafes   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/15697415_1196755743749234_6700057585619264880_n.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/15697415_1196755743749234_6700057585619264880_n-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/15697415_1196755743749234_6700057585619264880_n-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p>Referring to the country&#8217;s bicycle market, Arun Alagappan, President of TI Cycles said about 16-17 million units are sold annually but there has not been much growth as youth are taking to motorbikes.</p><p>Within the segments of bicycles, about 95 percent of the market is for cycles in the INR 5,000 range. Rest of them are priced above INR 5,000. Significantly, trends point towards a likely increase in sales volume of cycles in the INR 5,000-12,000 range as people tend to upgrade.</p><p>The purchase of cycles in the school-going kids has been growing with a good number of them buying more than one cycle over few years of their school-going days.</p><p>The sales of bicycles have been particularly going up in the IT hubs of Chennai, Bengaluru, Pune and the NCR and in Kerala and West Bengal in particular where girls and women ride them.</p><p>The Ciclo Cafe has bicycles priced from INR 5,000 that go up to INR 8 lakh for racing bicycle range.</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/theciclocafe/" target="_blank">Ciclo Cafe</a>.</p><p><em>Read the full story <a href="http://www.thehindubusinessline.com/companies/ti-cycles-to-expand-its-ciclo-cafe-chain/article9442009.ece" target="_blank">here</a>.</em></p><p><em>Photos Courtesy Of:<a href="https://www.facebook.com/theciclocafe/" target="_blank"><strong> Ciclo Cafe</strong></a></em></p>

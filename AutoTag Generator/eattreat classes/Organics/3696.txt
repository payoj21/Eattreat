3696	The Beetroot Pachadi Recipe To Brighten & Ripen Up Your Meal	<p>This beetroot flavoured raita from South India looks beautiful and tastes even better! We like to sit with a bowl of this and then eat it like a dip with vegetable fingers or spread it on bread or roti.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Serves:</strong> 3 &#8211; 4</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prep Time:</strong> 10 minutes</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cook Time:</strong> 15 minutes</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Elements:</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>For the chutney</strong></p><p style="text-align: center;">1/3 cup grated coconut</p><p style="text-align: center;">1-2 green chillies</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ inch ginger</p><p style="text-align: center;">¼ teaspoon mustard seeds</p><p style="text-align: center;">¼ teaspoon cumin seeds</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 medium beetroots, grated</p><p style="text-align: center;">Water</p><p style="text-align: center;">A pinch of turmeric powder</p><p style="text-align: center;">Salt as needed</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ cup thick yoghurt</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>For the tempering</strong></p><p style="text-align: center;">1 teaspoon oil</p><p style="text-align: center;">1-2 red chillies</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ teaspoon mustard seeds</p><p style="text-align: center;">A few curry leaves</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula:</strong></h4><p><strong>For the chutney</strong></p><p>In a blender, grind the grated coconut, green chillies, ginger, mustard seeds and the cumin seeds into a smooth paste.</p><p>Cook the grated beetroot with a little water in a pan. Add the salt and turmeric powder and cook until the beetroot begins to soften.</p><p>Next, add the coconut paste and cook for a few minutes on a low flame. Remove and set aside to cool.</p><p>Add the yoghurt and sprinkle in some salt as per your taste.</p><p><strong>For the tempering</strong></p><p>Heat the oil in a pan and add the mustard seeds. Once they begin to splutter, add the red chillies and curry leaves, and cook for about a minute. Pour over the chutney and serve.</p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Kunal Chandra</strong></em></p><p><strong>Adapted from <a href="http://www.padhuskitchen.com" target="_blank">Padhu&#8217;s Kitchen</a></strong></p>

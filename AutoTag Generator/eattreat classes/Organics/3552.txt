3552	A Healthy, Happy Meal With Quinoa, Mushroom & Chorizo with Basil	<p>&nbsp;</p><p>Quinoa is healthy, easy and quite filling. So if you&#8217;re looking for a recipe that makes quinoa tasty as well, you&#8217;ve come to the right spot. We are loving the smokiness of the mushrooms and the pungent basil aftertaste. Try it!</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Serves:</strong> 2 &#8211; 3</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prep Time: </strong>10 mins</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cook Time:</strong>15 mins</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Elements:</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">¼ cup El Olivo Organic White Quinoa</p><p style="text-align: center;">1½ cups water</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 teaspoon salt</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ tablespoon olive oil</p><p style="text-align: center;">3 cloves garlic, chopped</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 chorizo sausages, sliced</p><p style="text-align: center;">4-5 button mushrooms, sliced</p><p style="text-align: center;">7-8 basil leaves, roughly chopped</p><p style="text-align: center;">¼ teaspoon black pepper</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 teaspoon salt</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula:</strong></h4><p>Add the water and salt to a pot and cook the El Olivo Organic White Quinoa for about 10-12 minutes. Set aside.</p><p>In a saucepan, heat the olive oil and add the chopped garlic. Cook for about two minutes on medium heat before adding the mushrooms with a pinch of salt and sautéing further for two-three minutes. Remove from the pan and set aside.</p><p>In the same pan, cook the sausages for five-seven minutes. Remove and set aside.</p><p>Place the quinoa, mushrooms and sausages in a bowl, add the chopped basil and toss together.</p><p>Serve warm.</p>

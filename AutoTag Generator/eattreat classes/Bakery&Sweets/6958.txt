6958	Upcoming #FoodFestival Checklist: Asian Hawker’s, Palate Fest & Grub Fest Are Back!	<p>The year 2016 saw the second and third editions of food fests that quickly rose to popularity. Our favourite ones (hello Asian Hawker&#8217;s Market &amp; The Grub Fest!) are back in town, and we can’t wait for the days to go by quickly so we can start gorging on all that food again. Mark your calendars and save the dates of these much-loved food fests that are coming soon.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Asian Hawkers Market &#8211; IV</strong></h4><p>The third edition saw some spectacular entries from Jom Jom Malay (their satay was amazing), and Zorawar Kalra’s Pa Pa Ya; both of which have now opened their doors at Ansal Plaza and Select Citywalk respectively. Many restaurants of five-star hotels had brought some of their specialities to this format, and so did Veeba, the title sponsor of the fest.</p><p>Asian Hawkers Market is known for having a wide variety, and yet a careful curation of participants, and for the easy-on-the-pocket prices. Last edition’s favourites were the baos from Fatty Bao, sushi rolls from Guppy, and the katsu curry from Asian Haus. We can’t wait to see who’s bringing what this year.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> Select Citywalk</p><p><strong>Date</strong> 10th &#8211; 12th February</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> Free entry</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/asianhawkersmarket/?fref=ts" target="_blank">Asian Hawkers Market</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Lloyd Palate Fest</strong></h4><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/14712803_1395635670463722_3883042330325668862_o.jpg"><img title = "Upcoming #FoodFestival Checklist: Asian Hawker’s, Palate Fest &amp; Grub Fest Are Back!

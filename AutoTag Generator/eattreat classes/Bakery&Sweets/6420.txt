6420	Our Recipe Of Almond Flour Brownies That Are Healthy & Deliciously Chocolatey	<p>Gluten-free brownies that taste just as good! Almond flour is so healthy and well, when it comes to healthy brownies, there really isn&#8217;t any reason for us to stop ourselves, is there?</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Serves:</strong> 5-6</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prep Time:</strong> 15 mins</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cook Time: </strong>35 mins</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Elements</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">5 tablespoons butter</p><p style="text-align: center;">1¾ cups sugar</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ tablespoon salt</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 teaspoon vanilla extract</p><p style="text-align: center;">3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder</p><p style="text-align: center;">3 eggs</p><p style="text-align: center;">1½ cups almond flour</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 teaspoon baking powder</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula</strong></h4><p>Pre-heat the oven to 200 degrees Celsius.</p><p>Grease a baking pan (at least two inches deep) with butter and set aside.</p><p>In a saucepan on medium-low heat, add the butter, sugar and salt and cook until the sugar melts. Transfer into a bowl and let cool for a few minutes. With a spatula, mix in the vanilla, cocoa and eggs. Continue to mix until smooth and shiny.</p><p>Mix in the almond flour and baking powder. Now pour this batter into the greased baking pan, spreading to the edges, and place in the pre-heated oven.</p><p>Bake the brownies for about 35 minutes, until a toothpick comes out almost clean when inserted into the centre.</p><p>Remove from oven and let cool for 10 to 15 minutes. Cut into squares and enjoy!</p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of:<strong> Madishetty Manasa</strong></em></p>

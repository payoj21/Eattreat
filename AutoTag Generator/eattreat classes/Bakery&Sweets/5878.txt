5878	The Quintessential Banoffee Pie Recipe That You Need To Bookmark	<p>Banoffee pie is the simplest thing to have on cold winter days accompanied by a cup of warm tea. This Banoffee Pie recipe will break it down for you and you&#8217;ll be able to set one in a jiffy.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Elements</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>For the base</strong></p><p style="text-align: center;">100g butter</p><p style="text-align: center;">250g crushed digestive biscuits</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>For the caramel filling</strong></p><p style="text-align: center;">100g butter</p><p style="text-align: center;">100g packed brown sugar</p><p style="text-align: center;">400ml condensed milk</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>For the topping</strong></p><p style="text-align: center;">Grated chocolate</p><p style="text-align: center;">Whipped cream</p><p style="text-align: center;">3 bananas</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula</strong></h4><p><strong>For the crust</strong></p><p>Mix the melted butter and digestive biscuits and line it in a greased cake tin. Set aside to chill for 10 minutes.</p><p><strong>For the filling</strong></p><p>In a nonstick saucepan, put the butter and sugar and stir together on low heat till the sugar has dissolved.</p><p>Tip in the condensed milk and rapidly bring to boil for about a minute.</p><p>Turn off the heat as soon as you get a nice gooey caramel.</p><p>Pour into the cake tin with the crust and chill for at least an hour or until it needs to be served.</p><p><strong>For the topping</strong></p><p>Thinly slice the bananas and arrange them over the caramel.</p><p>Mix a few slices in with the whipped cream and top up the pie.</p><p>Sprinkle grated chocolate over the pie and it&#8217;s ready to serve!</p><p><strong>#EatExtra:</strong> You can add a little cinnamon powder and a little nutmeg powder to the caramel filling for this banoffee pie recipe to shine.</p>

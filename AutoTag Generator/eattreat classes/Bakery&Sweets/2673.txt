2673	The Moistest & Most Delectable Cupcakes In Gurgaon, Just For You!	<p>Gurgaon is suddenly on our maps for the nicest bakery items in NCR. We scouted out some of the most delectable cupcakes and muffins in G-Town just for you (and for us, of course)!</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The CupNCake Factory</strong></h4><p>Opened in 2012 at Sohna Road, in a very short span this place has won the place in many peoples hearts (or stomachs?). They make extremely delicious cupcakes and cakes from scratch and even customise them!</p><p><strong>#EatExtra:</strong> Red Velvet, Chocolate Fudge, Strawberry Crush, Caramel Apple and Lemon Raspberry</p><p>Drop in at 5, Ground Floor, Ninex City Mart Mall, Sohna Road, Gurgaon.</p><p><em>Featured Photo Courtesy Of:&nbsp;<strong><a href="https://www.facebook.com/TheCupNCakeFactory/?fref=ts" target="_blank">The CupNCake Factory</a></strong></em></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Bombaykery</strong></h4><p>If you love Red Velvet Cupcakes, this is just the right place for you. The Bombaykery is one of the latest additions in Gurgaon and we’re totally digging it! They have cupcakes, tarts, cheesecakes and Belgian Chocolate Fudge (which they also sell per kg). Ideal for gifting&nbsp; or for a get together with friends!</p><p><strong>#EatExtra:</strong> Red Velvet Cupcakes, Sea Salt Nutella Cookies</p><p>Explore more <a href="https://www.facebook.com/bombaykery/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Wishing Chair</strong></h4><p>This has got to be one of the cutest cafés in Gurgaon. It’s a tiny café tucked into one corner of a home-décor shop. The quirky, warm ambience and the sinfully delightful cupcakes, especially the chocolate one with sprinkles are the perfect combination for a bakery. Who doesn’t love a bakery where cupcakes &amp; whimsical home décor shopping go hand in hand?</p><p>Explore more <a href="https://www.facebook.com/wishingchairgurgaon/?fref=ts" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/10468214_649797671807107_5903466899362319291_o.jpg"><img title = "The Moistest &amp; Most Delectable Cupcakes In Gurgaon, Just For You!

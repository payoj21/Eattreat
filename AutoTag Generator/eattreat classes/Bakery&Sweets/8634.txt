8634	Dark Chocolate And Orange Hot Chocolate Is Our Way Of Bidding Farewell To Winters	<p>The perfect late winter<a href="http://eattreat.in/magazine/mexican-hot-chocolate-recipe/"> hot chocolate </a>with a citrusy flavour of orange! This hot chocolate with orange recipe reminds us of the long gone winter. But hey, we can celebrate the beginning of summer with this too right?</p><h4><strong>Hot Chocolate with Orange Recipe</strong></h4><p><strong>Serves:</strong> 1</p><p><strong>Prep Time: </strong>10 mins</p><p><strong>Cook time: </strong>10 mins</p><h4><strong>Ingredients:</strong></h4><p>2/3 cup chopped dark chocolate&nbsp;</p><p>1 cup milk&nbsp;</p><p>2 teaspoons sugar&nbsp;</p><p>1/4 cup fresh orange juice&nbsp;</p><p>1 tablespoon Cointreau</p><h4><strong>Method:</strong></h4><p>Mix all of the above ingredients in a saucepan on low heat until the chocolate melts and sugar dissolves. Keep stirring. Remove from heat and add a slice of candied orange on top! Ta da!</p><p><b>Photographed By: </b><a href="http://eattreat.in/magazine/make-hot-chocolate-pros/">Gayatri Sakhuja</a></p>

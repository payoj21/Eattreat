8378	6 Jalebi And Rabri Spots In Delhi For A Hot, Crispy & Sweet Indian Dessert	<p>It&#8217;s always nice to have warm sweets in cold weather, even if it makes you put on a few hundred grams. At that moment, when you&#8217;re watching them being made, and you bite into the crispy, soft, juicy dessert drenched in sugar syrup, nothing else matters anyway. We&#8217;ve been gorging on a lot of jalebis off late and so we&#8217;ve put this together for you so you can get your jalebis in Delhi to satisfy all those mid-day cravings.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>1. Senor Dhan Singh</strong></h4><p>This shop is almost 4 decades old and continues to serve delicious jalebis, day after day. They make them with pure desi ghee and these jalebis are super juicy and thick, just the way it should be. You can also get their kachoris and bread pakoras in case you don’t feel like keeping the lingering taste of sugar in your mouth before you move on.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> Mandir Wali Gali, Yusuf Sarai Market</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>2. Bangla Sweet House</strong></h4><p>Get your jalebis made in from of our eyes at Bangla Sweet House in CP. These sweet, orange, squiggly treats are made in pure ghee, and you can get your plate to go. Better still, have it right there so you can get more.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 115-117, Bangla Sahib Marg, Gole Market</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 23362257,011 23344371</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/banglasweethouse/" target="_blank">Bangla Sweet House</a>&nbsp;</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>4. Haldiram&#8217;s</strong></h4><p>You can go to a Haldiram’s almost anywhere in the city for fresh jalebis made in a hygienic environment. They&#8217;re always busy throughout the year even on weekdays, and even more so on festivals. You can get yours whenever the craving strikes!</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> L-6, Outer Circle, Connaught Place</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 47685300</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 48 for 100 grams.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>5.

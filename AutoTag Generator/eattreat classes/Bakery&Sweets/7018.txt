A bunch of restaurants in the city are picking out the best dishes from their menus to present to you in a set buffet-style menu that you can gorge on while relaxing in the environs of their beautiful interiors. Here&#8217;s where you can head to when you&#8217;re looking to spend a little quality time at one of the Sunday brunches in Delhi.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Fatty Bao</strong></h4><p>The Ping Pong Brunch at Fatty Bao promises amazing Asian delights in unlimited amounts.

5996	Ey Macarona! Your Dose Of Tiny Tasteful Macarons In Delhi	<p>If you’ve walked past a bakery, spotted cutesy, pretty and colourful cookies that look like mini-burgers, and not drooled over them – then we don’t know what stuff you’re made of. Macarons are a sweet meringue-based confection, with a ganache, buttercream or jam filling sandwiched between two cookies. Here are some places you can get the best macarons in Delhi.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Sugarama</strong></h4><p>Sugarama is a celebration of taste and creativity. A humble effort by a Cordon Bleu trained pastry chef to surprise the palette and deliver delight, and how! Sugarama does some of the best macarons in the city, with variants like<strong> Chocolate Mint</strong>, <strong>Orange</strong>, <strong>Salted Caramel</strong>, <strong>Vanilla Pepper</strong> or even <strong>Cola</strong> macarons. You have to try it, to believe it.</p><p>Head to 87, Shahpur Jat</p><p>Follow Sugarama <a href="https://www.facebook.com/sugaramapatisserie/?fref=ts" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Theos</strong></h4><p>A premium patisserie and chocolaterie that Noida proudly calls its own, Theos does a wide range of delectable delights and confectionaries. Sink your teeth into these buttery, creamy and melt-in-your-mouth goodness.</p><p>Head to B-1/20, Central Market, Sector 50, Noida</p><p>Follow Theos <a href="https://www.facebook.com/theosfood/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>L’Opera</strong></h4><p>It’s crunch at first bite, and then soft and chewy as you reach the centre. L’Opera does a variety of flavours in vivid colours. Choose from <strong>Dark Chocolate</strong>, <strong>Coffee</strong> and <strong>Passion Fruit</strong>, and there’s still plenty more. These are some of the best macarons in Delhi that we&#8217;ve had.</p><p>Head to 5-B, Khan Market</p><p>Follow L’Opera <a href="https://www.facebook.com/loperaindia/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Wenger’s</strong></h4><p>The iconic bakery located in the inner circle of Connaught Place, does mouth-melting macarons that will not burn a hole in your pocket. Head here, for their amazing desserts and grab a milkshake at Keventer’s after. We have a soft spot for their <strong>pineapple</strong> ones.</p><p>Head to A-16, Block A, Connaught Place</p><p>Explore more <a href="http://www.wengers.in/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Baked Beauty by Sanya Sarguru</strong></h4><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/bbmacarons89007_n.jpg"><img title = "Ey Macarona!

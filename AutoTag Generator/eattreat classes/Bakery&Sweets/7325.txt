7325	Looking For The Best Waffles In Delhi? You’ve Come To The Right Place	<p>We love us some waffles. It&#8217;s one of the easiest all-day meal options there is. We&#8217;ve put down a list of places we like to go to when we don&#8217;t feel like whipping up a batch ourselves. Head to these restaurants and cafés serving waffles in Delhi when the craving&#8217;s got you.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Depot48</strong></h4><p>They’ve got about 4 types of waffles ranging from the <strong>Classic Waffles</strong> with butter and maple syrup, and fruity ones with <strong>Berry Compote and Mascarpone cheese</strong>. All of their waffles come with Vanilla ice cream. You can get extra sauce on chargeable basis.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> N3, Second Floor, N Block Market, GK II, Greater Kailash, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 4508 1948</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 490/- onwards per plate (approx.)</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/depot29.48/" target="_blank">Depot 48.</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Wafflesome</strong></h4><p>Everybody who comes to Wafflesome falls irrevocably in love with their <strong>Nutella Waffles</strong>. The crunchy waffles, and the slightly soft centre, and the yummy hazelnut chocolate and the scoop of vanilla ice cream combined make for a truly divine treat! You can have this with a mug of <strong>Hot Chocolate</strong> for an even more indulgent experience.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> M-69, M Block, Greater Kailash I, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 4905 1555</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 120/- onwards per plate (approx.)</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/wafflesome/" target="_blank">Wafflesome</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Stop My Starvation</strong></h4><p>They’ve got both sweet and savoury waffles that they serve all day, because why should having waffles be only a breakfast thing, amirite? They’ve got a slightly boozy upgrade with the <strong>Old Friends Rum &amp; Raisin</strong>&nbsp;waffles. Head over for this, or their crepes and pancakes.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> M 28 E, M Block Market, Greater Kailash (GK) 1, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> +91 8447729298, +91 9899363486</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 100/- onwards per plate (approx.)</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/stopmystarvationgk/" target="_blank">Stop My Starvation</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Wenger&#8217;s Deli</strong></h4><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/Banana_bread_waffles_with_syrup.jpg"><img title = "Looking For The Best Waffles In Delhi?

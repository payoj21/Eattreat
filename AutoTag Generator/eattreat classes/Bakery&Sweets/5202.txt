5202	Raining Sweet & Savoury Pancakes! 20 IHOP Outlets Are Coming To India	<p>Yes, you heard us right! DineEquity Inc. in collaboration with Kwal’s Group is bringing 20 IHOP restaurants to India. The first restaurant is slated to open in Cyber Hub, Gurgaon in early 2017, bringing a mix of sweet and savoury pancakes.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></h4><p>IHOP has been a leader and innovator for over 58 years, in all things breakfast any time of day. The chain offers 65 different signature dishes, fresh made-to-order breakfast options, a wide selection of lunch and dinner options as well as meals under 600 calories. Dark chocolate peppermint pancakes, anyone?</p><p>A leader in family dining, IHOP is set to offer a unique experience to Indian Families with a menu that is customised to suit the Indian palate. And they have savoury pancakes too!</p><p><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/12/13920366_10154016035318152_9165448654665676685_o.jpg"><img title = "Raining Sweet &amp; Savoury Pancakes! 20 IHOP Outlets Are Coming To India  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5237" src="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/12/13920366_10154016035318152_9165448654665676685_o.jpg" alt = "sweet savoury gurgaon desserts Category:Bakery breakfast   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/13920366_10154016035318152_9165448654665676685_o.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/13920366_10154016035318152_9165448654665676685_o-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/13920366_10154016035318152_9165448654665676685_o-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p>While the first location is in Cyber Hub, Gurgaon, there are 19 more locations (WHAT!?) across multiple states of northern and southern India slated to open over the next decade.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>What’s On The Menu</strong></h4><p>IHOP is known for its affordable everyday dining experience, with warm and friendly service.

6182	We Always Head To These Restaurants When The Craving For Dessert Kicks In	<p>Haven’t you ever been to a restaurant where you’ve ordered dessert and fallen in love with it as soon as you’ve had your first spoonful? Isn’t that feeling just the best? We’ve picked out our favourite dessert places near you, the next time you’re at these places. Better still, go whenever you’re in the mood for sweet things.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong> Le Bistro Du Parc</strong></h4><p>The <strong>Valhrona Dark Chocolate Fondue</strong> comes highly recommended from this adorable little French restaurant in Defence Colony.

6143	#EatCheap: Budget Meals For Two Under INR 600 In Khan Market	<p>Named in honour of Khan Abdul Ghaffar Khan (Popularly known as Frontier Gandhi), Khan Market is the classy, up-market and posh shopping market located in South Delhi, close to India Gate. It also has the highest rentals per square foot in the world. We love Khan Market, for its trendy cafes, bookstores and umpteen restaurants. There’s plenty to do here, even on a budget. Don’t know how getting some cheap food in Khan Market is possible?</p><p>We show you how you can have a decent time in Khan Market, even when you’re boot-strapped. So whether you’re visiting Khan Market at the fag end of the month, or are just broke in general – fret not! We’ve got you covered with our list of places to eat at in under INR 600 for two.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Beer &amp; Nachos With Salsa at The Beer Café</strong></h4><p>The Beer Café opened up recently at Khan Market, and we couldn’t be happier! Order a plate of Stadium Style Nachos (INR 195), with a pint of beer (INR 125 approx) and spend your time lavishly on their terrace.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> Plot 8, Main Market, Khan Market</p><p><strong>Say Hello at</strong> +91 9711316004</p><p><strong>Explore</strong> <a href="http://www.thebeercafe.com/" target="_blank">The Beer Cafe</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Beer &amp; Fries at The Chatter House</strong></h4><p>Want to grab a beer and have something to munch on while soaking up the ambience at The Chatter House? Visit them during Happy Hours (12 PM-8 PM, everyday) and avail their low prices on alcohol. Order cocktails at INR 195 each, with a side order of their House Cut Fries (INR 220). Say hello to cheap food and drinks at The Chatter House.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 58, 1<sup>st</sup> and 2<sup>nd</sup> Floor, Khan Market</p><p><strong>Say Hello at</strong> 011 41502666</p><p><strong>Explore</strong> <a href="https://www.facebook.com/The-Chatter-House-175268949520271/?fref=ts" target="_blank">The Chatter House</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Coffee at Perch</strong></h4><p>Don’t want to have liquor? Head to Perch, for their amazing selection of coffees that are priced reasonably. Order their Cappuccino, at INR 150. Savor the ambience and the good music while sipping on hot coffee, with some good conversation to add to your experience.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 71, 1<sup>st</sup> Floor, Khan Market</p><p><strong>Say Hello at</strong> +91 8373976637</p><p><strong>Explore</strong> <a href="https://www.facebook.com/perchindelhi/?fref=ts" target="_blank">Perch</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>All Day Breakfast At Café Turtle</strong></h4><p>Café Turtle, located right above Full Circle Bookstore is the place to be if you’re a wannabe writer, or a book-lover. Enjoy your meal while brushing shoulders with the literati of New Delhi. Choose from their selection of all day breakfasts, all priced between INR 125-225. We’d recommend their Country Beans and Mash (INR 225) and from their desserts, pick Lemon Cake with Lemon Sauce (INR 210).</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> Shop 23, 1<sup>st</sup> &amp; 2<sup>nd</sup> Floor, Khan Market</p><p><strong>Say Hello at</strong> 011 24655641</p><p><strong>Explore</strong> <a href="https://www.facebook.com/fullcirclebooks/?fref=ts" target="_blank">Café Turtle</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Salads &amp; Brushchettas at La Vie Pizzeria</strong></h4><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/12485953_455191811350199_1080124157009498268_o.jpg"><img title = "#EatCheap: Budget Meals For Two Under INR 600 In Khan Market  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-7061" src="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/12485953_455191811350199_1080124157009498268_o.jpg" alt = "reasonable new delhi khan market desserts Category:Bakery cafes budget   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/12485953_455191811350199_1080124157009498268_o.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/12485953_455191811350199_1080124157009498268_o-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/12485953_455191811350199_1080124157009498268_o-768x460.jpg 768w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/12485953_455191811350199_1080124157009498268_o-825x493.jpg 825w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/12485953_455191811350199_1080124157009498268_o-410x246.jpg 410w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/12485953_455191811350199_1080124157009498268_o-150x90.jpg 150w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>La Vie</strong></em></p><p>La Vie does wood fired pizzas &amp; bruschettas, fresh from the oven (yum!). While the pizzas are a bit expensive for us, we usually go for the salads or the appetisers which all come under INR 600 for two people.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 51-A, Khan Market</p><p><strong>Say Hello at</strong> 011 33105260</p><p><strong>Explore</strong> <a href="https://www.facebook.com/LaViekhanmarket/?fref=ts" target="_blank">La Vie</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Sandwiches at The Artful Baker</strong></h4><p>If you aren’t feeling full already, head to The Artful Baker for some artisanal desserts. Try their Chocolate Caramel Pastry (INR 175) and Oreo Cheese Cake (INR 199). They also have this really nice Arabian Sandwich and Mediterranean Sandwich for INR 200. If you&#8217;re done with your cheap food for the day, end your meal on a sweet note here.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 13-B, Khan Market</p><p><strong>Say Hello At</strong> 011 43508332</p><p><strong>Explore</strong> <a href="https://www.facebook.com/artfulkhanmarket/?fref=ts" target="_blank">The Artful Baker</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Rolls at Khan Chacha’s</strong></h4><p>Khan Chacha’s Rolls are famous all over the country, if not the world. A visit to Khan Market is incomplete without paying homage to Khan Chacha by gorging on some melt-in-your-mouth Chicken Tikka Rolls (INR 200) or Mutton Kakori Roll (INR 230). You might even spot a celeb eating here.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 1<sup>st</sup> Floor, Shop 50, Middle Lane, Khan Market</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 43680449</p><p><strong>Explore</strong> <a href="https://www.facebook.com/khanchacha/?fref=ts" target="_blank">Khan Chacha’s</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Mutton Barra and Roomali Rotis at Azam&#8217;s Mughlai</strong></h4><p>Feast all your senses on some exquisite Mughlai fare, at Azam&#8217;s Mughlai. Azam&#8217;s is nestled in the famous middle lanes of Khan Market, bang opposite SodaBottleOpenerWala. You can try their Mutton Barra (INR 350 for 4 pieces) with Roomali Rotis (INR 10 per roti), or try their rolls served with a refreshing mint chutney. Whoever said you can&#8217;t get budget grub and cheap food in Khan Market?</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 1-B, Khan Market</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 24617846</p><p><strong>Explore</strong> <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Azam-Mughlai-719354391516925/?fref=ts" target="_blank">Azam&#8217;s Mughlai</a></p><p><em>Featured Image Courtesy Of <b>La Vie</b></em></p><p>&nbsp;</p>

7376	New Restaurants, Bars & An Asian Flea Market: Asian Hawker’s Market Is Back	<p>One of our favourite food fests is back in town for another smashing season with even more things planned for the connoisseurs of Asian food. Asian Hawker’s Market Delhi is here with a fourth edition with Veeba as it’s market sponsor for the third time in a row.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>First things First</strong></h4><p>Veeba is going to make the pre-Valentine’s Day weekend truly saucy by launching their new range of products for the very first time, including six new kinds of mayonnaise, Italian sauces, cheese dips and salsa.</p><p>You’ll be seeing new entrants like Lite Bite Foods’ new Korean restaurant <strong>Hahn’s Kitchen</strong>, that’s opening at the very suave One Horizon Center in Gurgaon. Riyaz Amlani’s <strong>Social</strong> is launching a brand new Singaporean menu that it has never showcased before.

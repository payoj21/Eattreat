5850	This Lemon Posset & Passionfruit Jelly At The Imperial Knocked Our Socks Off	<p>Chef Vivek Chauhan from The Imperial shares his stellar recipes for Passionfruit &amp; Strawberry Jelly, and Lemon Posset &#8211; both of which can be prepared in a jiffy. Try these delectable delights at home and don&#8217;t forget, the <a href="http://eattreat.in/oranges-lemons-limes-things-citrus-winter-season/">benefits of lemon </a>in the coming season can be fully enjoyed in this stunning tangy Lemon Posset.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Passionfruit &amp; Strawberry Jelly</strong></h4><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Elements</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">300 ml passionfruit puree</p><p style="text-align: center;">100 ml water</p><p style="text-align: center;">140 gm sugar</p><p style="text-align: center;">10 gm gelatin</p><p style="text-align: center;">8 strawberries</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula</strong></h4><p>Boil passionfruit puree, sugar with water on stove.</p><p>Stir the soaked gelatin in the above mix.</p><p>Chop strawberry into dices.</p><p>Cool the mix over ice-bath.</p><p>Pour the mix into glasses and mix chopped strawberry into it.</p><p>Refrigerate for 2 hours.</p><p>Garnish with chantilly and strawberry.</p><hr /><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Lemon Posset</strong></h4><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/lemon-posset.jpg"><img title = "This Lemon Posset &amp; Passionfruit Jelly At The Imperial Knocked Our Socks Off  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5890" src="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/lemon-posset.jpg" alt = "winter Recipes Easy desserts christmas Category:Bakery   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/lemon-posset.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/lemon-posset-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/lemon-posset-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Elements</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">150 ml double cream</p><p style="text-align: center;">36 gm castor sugar</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 lemon zest</p><p style="text-align: center;">24 ml lemon juice</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>For Garnish:</strong></p><p style="text-align: center;">Rossette of cream</p><p style="text-align: center;">Strawberry slice</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula</strong></h4><p>Boil double cream and castor sugar &amp; lemon zest on the stove.</p><p>Remove from the stove and add lemon juice &amp; stir it once to mix it evenly.</p><p>Leave it to rest for half an hour and then fill it in the glasses.</p><p>Refrigerate to set for 2 hours.</p><p>Garnish with cream, strawberry &amp;

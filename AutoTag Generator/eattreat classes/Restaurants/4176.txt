4176	The Studio By Gurdas Maan Is What You Need To Look Out For In 2017	<p>Punjabi singer-actor Gurdas Maan is partnering with Delhi-based Now Capital to bring together things that are very important to him—a chain of restaurants called ‘The Studio &#8211; by Gurdas Maan’ that will highlight the culinary and performing arts.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></h4><p>The menu at The Studio will highlight Punjabi cooking through a ‘farm to fork’ approach and will provide a unique fine-dining experience. Every dish on the menu has its own story—whether it is the <strong>Chilli Chicken</strong> from the hinterlands of Punjab, the <strong>Amritsari Chhole</strong>, or the fusion <strong>Rice Pudding</strong> from London.</p><p>The restaurant will feature music that ranges from live improvised collaborations between established artists from the music industry to planned musical-theatre productions by new and upcoming talent. Maan says that it will be the perfect way to coalesce his two dreams—creating a place to celebrate food and music.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Artsy Initiatives</strong></h4><p>Through this initiative, Maan hopes to provide a platform to groom, mentor, and promote those dedicated to the arts. He also hopes to share his food memories of the fresh food that come straight from the farms in Punjab, and the fresh take on Indian food that is available around the globe.</p><p>The founder and partner of Now Capital, Sahil Baweja says that they plan to open the flagship in early 2017 and will expand in Delhi, Chandigarh, Punjab, and Mumbai. They also plan to enter international markets.</p><p><em>Featured Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Gurdas Maan</strong></em></p>

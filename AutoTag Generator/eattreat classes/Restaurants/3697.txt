3697	Rail Museum Is All Set To Open Vintage Coach Hotels & A New Restaurant!	<p>Rail Museum is revamping their already awesome park and coming up with new attractions that include a light and sound show, a restaurant called The Rails and vintage hotels, that we just can’t get over.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></p><p><em>The Hindu</em> reported that the IRCTC (Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation) has been roped in to operate and manage the restaurant, The Rails. The Rails is newly constructed inside the museum that will showcase different cuisines. IRCTC Spokesperson told <em>The Hindu</em>, &#8220;The restaurant, which is to be made operational soon, has a replica of the Central Dome portion of Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus (CST), Mumbai, which is a UNESCO World Heritage building.”</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Palace On Wheel Hotels?!</strong></p><p>Trying to make the museum an evening entertainment spot for us Delhi-wallas, the plan seems set to convert the Palace On Wheel coaches into vintage hotels. The restaurant is targeted towards the upmarket segment, and for those who want to have a meal when tired, or taking a break. With footfalls of about 2,000 on weekdays and 5,000 on weekends and holidays, we’re pretty sure this restaurant is going to be a hit!</p><p><em>Read the full story <a href="http://www.thehindu.com/news/cities/Delhi/rail-museum-all-set-for-a-revamp/article9217568.ece" target="_blank">here</a>. </em></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Kasturi Roy</strong></em></p>

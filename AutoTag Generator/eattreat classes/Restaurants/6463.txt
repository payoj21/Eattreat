6463	The Gourmet High Street Is Back & It’s Bringing The Big Leagues!	<p>Delhi-NCR’s only food event where you can learn and indulge, The Gourmet High Street is back with its third edition from 10<sup>th</sup> to 12<sup>th</sup> February 2017 at DLF Cyber Hub, Gurgaon. The first food festival in Delhi, 2017 is looking good.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></h4><p>The three-day culinary food festival in Delhi 2017, is an international platform exhibiting every aspect of food, and helping promote Indian cuisine across the globe. The Gourmet High Street is the only festival that celebrates food everywhere, from Chef’s demo to Mixology sessions, and Chef’s workshops, Gourmet markets and more, experience them all here.</p><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/10556477_1710906229143698_4466215088614752480_n.jpg"><img title = "The Gourmet High Street Is Back &amp; It&#039;s Bringing The Big Leagues! In addition, one also gets to interact with and get inspired by leading celebrity chefs, mixologists and sommeliers. Our new venue Cyber Hub is already a crowd puller for the pleasing number of eating options it offers. We are excited to host the festival at a destination which is a food lover’s paradise.”</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>What To Catch At The Gourmet High Street?</strong></h4><p>Some of the highlights include Culinary Grind (An exclusive platform hosting League Of Extraordinary Chefs like <strong>Sanjeev Kapoor</strong>, <strong>Vikas Khanna</strong>, <strong>Amrita Raichand</strong>, <strong>Saransh Goila</strong>, <strong>Sarah Todd</strong>, <strong>Vikramjit Roy</strong>, <strong>Vicky Ratnani</strong> and more, who will conduct cooking demos), Cooking Studio (A chance to learn cooking techniques from the masters in person), I Left It All For Food (A zone for foodpreneurs), Budding Chefs Kitchen (A place for kids to learn about food). There’s something for everyone at this food festival in Delhi 2017.</p><p>So save your dates, for the Gourmet High Street.</p><p><strong>When</strong> 10<sup>th</sup>-11<sup>th</sup>-12<sup>th</sup> February 2017</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> DLF Cyber Hub, Phase 2, NH 8, Gurugram</p><p><strong>Entry is free</strong></p><p><strong>Explore</strong> <a href="https://www.facebook.com/thegourmethighstreet" target="_blank">The Gourmet High Street</a></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of <b>The Gourmet High Street </b></em></p>

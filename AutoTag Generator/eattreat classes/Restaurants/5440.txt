5440	Hotel Broadway’s Chor Bizarre Is Now Open At Bikaner House, Delhi	<p>India’s first ‘chor bazaar’ themed restaurant, Chor Bizarre at Hotel Broadway in Daryaganj, is now bringing it’s selection of eclectic food from all over the country, and quirk to Bikaner House.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></h4><p>Chor Bizarre is the country’s first themed restaurant that captures the spirit of the ‘chor bazaar’, or ‘thieves&#8217; market’, within its kaleidoscopic interiors, and is dedicated to serving regional Indian food from all parts of the country. The decor is put together from various bazaar’s in the country and encourages diners to go beyond the immediate and explore the objects d’art displayed creatively in each corner of the restaurant.</p><p>Just like the artefacts that make up the decor in the restaurant, they will be serving regional Indian food from all parts of the country. Chor Bizarre was first established in Hotel Broadway in Daryaganj, and has branches in Lavasa (Pune) and London.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/chorbizarre/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>

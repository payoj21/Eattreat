5034	Luxurious Mehrauli Restaurants For That Gorgeous View Of The Qutub Minar	<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The entire street (Kalka Dass Marg) has restaurants you need to visit; each offering something different and unique with a view of Qutab Minar. Nothing less than a treat to the eyes and your taste buds, these Mehrauli restaurants need to be on your bucket lists! Let’s explore the Mehrauli Restaurants tribe:</span></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Dramz</strong></h4><p>If you’re up for some <a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//favourite-wine-cocktails-spike-weekend/">great cocktails </a>and food which will help you pass the night,  go ahead and visit this whisky bar and lounge. With a view of Qutab and ample seating on the terrace, this place is worth a shot (geddit?).</p><p>Drop in at 1580/1, KD Marg, Near Qutub Minar, Mehrauli, New Delhi. Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Dramz-470363416398575/">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Olive, Qutub</strong></h4><p>One of the oldest and most renowned restaurants, this brings a magical experience with its all-white décor, cozy interiors, courtyard and delicious meals which can make your day super special. It’s an all-time favourite restaurant, with its menu offering flavours and tastes like never before! Sunday brunches are extremely popular here, so visit them for a delectable experience.</p><p>Drop in at Haveli No. 6-8, Kalkadass Marg, Mehrauli. Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/OliveAtTheQutub/?fref=ts">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Lavaash by Saby</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">One of the newbies joining the Mehrauli restaurant tribe is Lavaash by Saby. An Armenian restaurant, this is set in the Ambawatta complex offering the most delicious meals of top notch quality. We love their <a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//thebiglist-25-home-bakers-shops-booziest-alcoholic-cakes/">Old Monk Mousse</a> and their bright bold décor. And most of all, we love the food made with love and passion by Chef Saby and Chef Megha Kohli. Explore Armenian food and we promise you’ll fall in love with this place!  </span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Drop in at H5/1 Ambawatta 1, Kalkadass Marg, Mehrauli. Explore more <a href="https://www.facebook.com/lavaashbysaby/?fref=ts">here</a>. </span></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cherie One Qutub</strong></h4><p><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/12/12247149_959563047465923_5934419305093736707_n.jpg"><img title = "Luxurious Mehrauli Restaurants For That Gorgeous View Of The Qutub Minar  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5075" src="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/12/12247149_959563047465923_5934419305093736707_n.jpg" alt = "restaurants mehrauli luxury high-end dinner Category:Restaurants   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/12247149_959563047465923_5934419305093736707_n.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/12247149_959563047465923_5934419305093736707_n-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/12247149_959563047465923_5934419305093736707_n-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Modern interiors and seating overlooking the courtyard and the Qutab Minar, makes Cherie nothing but one of the most romantic places to visit. We recommend this wholeheartedly for an evening of sweet nothings and romantic whispers. </span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Drop in at 1501, Kalka Das Marg, Mehrauli, New Delhi. Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/cheri.1qutub/?fref=ts">here</a>. </span></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Thai High</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">One of our personal favourites, Thai high is nothing but a delight every single time. They’ve now expanded and have a shack on the terrace as well.  With orange walls reminding you of quaint streets of Spain, and with a view of none other than the Qutab Minar and other monuments, this restaurant with Thai cuisine to blow your mind away. Thai High is a clear winner in terms of quality and quantity of food, ambience and service. </span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Drop in at 1091/1, Ambawatta Complex, Kalka Das Marg, Mehrauli, New Delhi. </span>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/www.thaihigh.in/?fref=ts" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Q’la</strong></p><p>A luxurious and contemporary restaurant which offers global flavours to you with a view of the Qutab Minar &#8211; the ultimate winner.  Italian, French and Spanish influences with imported meats, seafood and cocktails take you away on a journey. Candlelight ambience, scrumptious meals with intricate details and décor making it a dreamy place to dine in. For all the shopaholics, the ground floor has several retail stores of couture designers.</p><p>Drop in at 4-A, Next to Qutab Minar, Seven Style Mile, Kalka Das Marg, Kila, Mehrauli. Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/QlaOfficial/">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>En</strong></h4><p><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/12/15167676_571103903080804_201857338142117836_o.jpg"><img title = "Luxurious Mehrauli Restaurants For That Gorgeous View Of The Qutub Minar  Luxurious Mehrauli Restaurants For That Gorgeous View Of The Qutub Minar  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5074" src="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/12/15167676_571103903080804_201857338142117836_o.jpg" alt = "restaurants mehrauli luxury high-end dinner Category:Restaurants   restaurants mehrauli luxury high-end dinner Category:Restaurants   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/15167676_571103903080804_201857338142117836_o.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/15167676_571103903080804_201857338142117836_o-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/15167676_571103903080804_201857338142117836_o-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>En</strong></em></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Did we hear <a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//maki-sushi-how-eating/">Japanese food </a>with a view of the Qutab?

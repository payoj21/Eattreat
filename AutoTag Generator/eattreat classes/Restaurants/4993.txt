4993	Andrea’s Eatery: The Ultimate Traveller’s Haunt Is Making Its Way To Saket	<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Andrea’s Eatery, a modern European-style bistro is coming to </span>Select CITYWALK, which will be plating world cuisine in a signature style. Coming from the founder of successful restaurants like Amici and Baci, Andrea&#8217;s Eatery has one thing we absolutely can&#8217;t wait to check out &#8211; travel-inspired food.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></h4><p>The brainchild of  Andrea Pauro, who is a<span style="font-weight: 400;"> gourmand and an avid traveller, Andrea&#8217;s Eatery brings to life food that a traveller will love from European, Middle Eastern and South East Asian countries. The menu is giving us dishes that are grouped according to origins of the produce, placed in a travel journal highlighting ingredients and the stories around these dishes. </span></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>What Can You Expect</strong></h4><p>Slated to open sometime in mid-Dec, Andrea&#8217;s Eatery will showcase <strong>Sharing Platters</strong> full of yummy goodness, choices from the <strong>Greens and Orchard</strong> section, which features salads made with organic produce, indulgent <strong>Pasta Bowls</strong>, signature pizzas and mains inspired from the land, sea, dairy farm and vegetable garden.</p><p>The restaurant will also have a curated list of beverages inclusive of wine and beer. The eatery has a mural on one wall and will focus on well thought-out food.</p><p>Are you ready to get on this journey?

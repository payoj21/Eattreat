8233	Concept Shawarma In Def Col Has Shawarmas So Good That You’ll Like The Paneer One Too	<p>If you’re living in South Delhi, you must have heard of Concept ka shawarma in Defence Colony at least once in your lifetime. The shawarmas at Concept are a total steal. Not only are they known for the <strong>Chicken Shawarma</strong>, but they&#8217;re also known for their <strong>Paneer Shawarma</strong>. Imagine, if someone can like Paneer Shawarma, it must be really good. So here goes to trying on of the best shawarmas in South Delhi.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>In A Nutshell</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Located under the flyover, this place serves the best shawarma in South Delhi. And it’s not only limited to shawarma, they even have a small restaurant with a seating for 4-5 groups where they serve the Indian menu, including delicacies like <strong>butter chicken</strong>, <strong>kadhai chicken</strong> and much more.</span></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Perfect Spot</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">If you’re having shawarma, you can either get it delivered or take away. Sit peacefully and eat in your car. And if you’re going for the Indian food, since it’s a small restaurant, you can sit anywhere you like.</span></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>On The Silver Platter</strong></h4><p>The shawarma here is one of the best shawarma’s you’ll get in South Delhi. The chicken is roasted nicely and the rolls are soft and thin. With loads of mayo and the green chutney, this is the perfect place if you’re craving for shawarma today evening.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Raise A Glass To</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Since they just serve cold drinks, we’d recommend you have your favourite cold drink with the spicy food. We hope it soothes you tongue.</span></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Bitter Pill</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Since they serve such good food, we just hope they do something about the seating area to improve the food experience one has here.</span></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Food For Thought</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We promise you’ll never see them empty as you can see everyone munching on them. You can see a lot of people eating these in their cars. You should definitely try this place if you’re looking for good shawarma. We hope you have a Happy Shawarma Day!</span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Head to</strong> 129, Flyover Market, Defence Colony, New Delhi</span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 41004138, 011 41004139</span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Pay</strong> INR 600 for two people (approx.)&nbsp;</span></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Delhi Food Walks</strong></em></p>

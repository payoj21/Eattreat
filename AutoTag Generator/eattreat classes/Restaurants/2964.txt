2964	4 All-You-Can-Eat Buffet Spreads In Gurgaon That Are Classy AF 	<p>Buffet meal? Count us in! Extensive choices, varied cuisines, scrumptious desserts and more &#8211; buffets are an experience like none other. Gurgaon has some mesmerising spreads which will leave you stuffed like a potato and still wanting more! So, read on to see which restaurants made it to our list.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Seasonal Taste, The Westin Gurgaon</strong></h4><p>Westin offers one of the most luxurious and lavish buffet spreads, leaving you spoiled for choices like never before. Their cheese platter has exceptional quality, making it a perfect way to start the meal. The seafood here is fresh and done just right,&nbsp;and the Indian section is sure to appease. Do not miss their waffles and pancakes if you drop-in for breakfast.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> The Westin Hotel, Sector 29, Gurgaon</p><p><strong>Say hello</strong> at 0124 497 7831</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 3,000 for two people (approx.)</p><p>Explore<a href="https://www.facebook.com/SeasonalTastesAllDayDiningTheWestinGurgaonNewDelhi/" target="_blank"> Seasonal Tastes</a>&nbsp;</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cafe G, Crowne Plaza Today&nbsp;</strong></h4><p>Known for its Sunday brunch, Cafe G has a loyal base of patrons for their remarkable buffet experience. The effortlessly formal interiors with personal seating sections make it ideal for an official business lunch or cosy family dinner. Their dessert section is top-notch with usuals like baked custard, mousse, choux, warm chocolate brownie, and specials like baklava. We can assure you that the sugary treats will leave you satisfied.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> Crowne Plaza, NH-8, Sector 29, Gurgaon</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 3500 for two people (approx.)</p><p>Explore&nbsp;Crowne Plaza <a href="https://www.ihg.com/crowneplaza/hotels/us/en/gurgaon/ndegg/hoteldetail" target="_blank">here</a></p><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/LE-MERIDIEN_099.jpg"><img title = "4 All-You-Can-Eat Buffet Spreads In Gurgaon That Are Classy AF   "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2965" src="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/LE-MERIDIEN_099.jpg" alt = "luxury gurgaon fine-dine Category:Restaurants Buffet   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/LE-MERIDIEN_099.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/LE-MERIDIEN_099-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/LE-MERIDIEN_099-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <a href="http://www.lemeridiengurgaondelhincr.com/latestrecipe" target="_blank"><strong>Le Meridien</strong></a></em></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Latest Recipe, Le Meridien Gurgaon&nbsp;</strong></h4><p>Latest Recipe offers an artistic buffet with intriguing artwork to amuse your creative side and delicious food to satiate your hunger.

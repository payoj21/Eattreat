Hog At These Cafes & Restaurants In West Delhi	<p>Karol Bagh is a food hub that we barely go exploring considering the fact that this place is more well-known for all the cheap shopping options for every occasion. We&#8217;ve put down a list of the places that you must go to, the next time you find yourself looking for cafes and restaurants in Karol Bagh.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Bars</strong></span></h4><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Boheme Cafe Bar</strong></h4><p>A tiny little rustic looking place, you can spend your time here with friends for a change in pace. They make Chinese food better than the other things on their menu, so that’s probably what you should look for if you’re looking to grab a bite while you’re here.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 938 16-A/1, WEA, Near Metro Station, Karol Bagh, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> +91 9910089416</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 1,100 for two people (approx.) with alcohol</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/bohemekarolbagh/?fref=ts">Boheme Cafe Bar</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Chargrill Resto Bar</strong></h4><p>They’ve got all the right kind of chakhna to go with your drinks. Get your pint of beer and have some nice <strong>Herb Crusted Fries</strong>, <strong>Beer Battered Onion Rings</strong>, or <strong>Sweet Potato Wedges</strong>. They’ve got your usual North Indian and Chinese food on the menu, so you can be assured of getting your favourites from here.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 4/66,1st Floor, Padam Singh Road, Karol Bagh, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong>&nbsp;+91 99104 52108</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 900 for two people (approx.) with alcohol</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Restaurants &amp; Cafés</strong></span></h4><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Art Of Spices</strong></h4><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/kathi-roll.jpg"><img title = "Shopping In Karol Bagh? You’ve got to try it to believe it. The best part is that it’s also inexpensive, so students around this area are often found flocking to it after college hours. <strong>We love the Double Egg Double Chicken Roll</strong>.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> B-1/3, Behind City Hospital, Pusa Road, Karol Bagh</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> +91 9811342929 &amp; +91 9811352929</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 500 for two people (approx.)</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Art-of-spices-100132753402453/">The Art Of Spices</a>.&nbsp;</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Walk-In Street</strong></h4><p>A street themed café, they serve Mexican, Italian, North Indian, and Chinese food. The cafe is built over three levels and you can even get a whole floor to yourself for a private gathering. Do not forget to try the <strong>Peri Peri Chicken</strong>, the <strong>Juicy Chicken Burger</strong>, and sip on <strong>Italian Smooch</strong>.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 6/88, Padam Singh Road, Opposite Vishal Mega Mart, Karol Bagh</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> +91 9999901956 &amp; +91 8587920384</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 1,000 for two people (approx.)</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/thewalkinstreet/?fref=ts">Walk-In Street</a>.&nbsp;</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Suruchi</strong></h4><p>They make the most wholesome, delicious thalis here. It’s quite the popular place for those who can’t do without vegetarian food. The thalis have unlimited servings of 4 different kinds of vegetable dishes, apart from the large portions of accompaniments that it comes with. The only thing that’s slightly irksome is that the prices of all their dishes have a slightly marked up price during weekends.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 15-A/56, Ajmal Khan Road, Opposite Roopak Store, Karol Bagh, New Delhi</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 45000255, 011 45000266</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR ₹700 for two people (approx.)</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/pages/Suruchi-Restaurant-Karol-Bagh/250048581699587">Suruchi</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Red Brick Café</strong></h4><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/red-brick.jpg"><img title = "Shopping In Karol Bagh?

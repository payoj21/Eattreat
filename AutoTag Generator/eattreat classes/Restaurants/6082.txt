6082	Check Eat Off: Bucket List Of New Eateries & Restaurants Coming In 2017	<p>2016 saw the opening of many restaurants in Delhi NCR. Now that the year has ended, we’re looking forward to all the new ones coming up in the city. We’re telling you the restaurants opening soon that we’re the most excited about.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Theobroma’s</strong></h4><p>This dessert place from Mumbai is famous for its decadent brownies. Before they announced that they’re opening stores in the NCR, one had to request people to go to Mumbai to get a boxful of these delicious treats. They’re <a href="http://eattreat.in/theobroma-fundraising-talks-outlet-delhi/" target="_blank">opening in three locations</a> in NCR as of now.</p><p>Where: Connaught Place, DLF Mall Of India, and DLF Cyberhub</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Jom Jom Malay</strong></h4><p>They’re all set to inaugurate on the 1st of January and provided us with a sneak preview of the kind of food they do at the 3rd edition of Asian Hawker’s Market. This restaurant will be serving Malaysian and Bornese food, and also has influences from India, China, and Indonesia, and will open at the newly revamped Ansal Plaza.</p><p>Head to Ansal Plaza. Check out more <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Jomjom-malay-1670617439917678/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Ashok &amp; Ashok Meat Dhaba</strong></h4><p>The iconic joint from Sadar Bazaar is finally making its way over to Connaught Place. Their <strong>Mutton Curry</strong> is so good, <a href="http://eattreat.in/mutton-delicacies-can-try-delhi-arent-just-karims/" target="_blank">queues line up</a> a few hours before they open for business and the dish is over within an hour!</p><p>Head to 36, Municipal Market, Outer Circle, Connaught Place</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"></h4><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Colonnade CP</strong></h4><p>All set to wow patrons in 2017, the <a href="http://eattreat.in/colonnade-cp-coming-heres-everything-will-house/">Collonade will be opening soon</a> with multiple restaurants including Mamagoto, Cafe Delhi Heights, The Beer Cafe and The Permit Room. The place will also be hosting Fat Lulu&#8217;s and Punjabi By Nature for now.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Junction</strong></h4><p>The Junction is already open with the Potet<a href="http://eattreat.in/delhi-first-fries-place-snack/">, the first ever fries-only place</a> in Delhi. They will also be soon opening a pizzeria and sushi bar. The bar is yet to get a license, but be rest assured, you&#8217;ll hear about it here first.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Sizzlers Café by Gola</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The new venture by the very famous Gola Sizzlers is ready to <a href="http://eattreat.in/ansal-plaza-the-cyberhub-of-south-delhi/">open at Ansal Plaza</a> with its wonky range of sizzlers in cuisines like North Indian,  Mughlai,  Continental, Asian and more.

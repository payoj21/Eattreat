4023	Elevation Burger Is Coming To India With Healthy Burgers!	<p>After Johnny Rockets and Carls Jr, another wave of high-end burgers might be flooding India soon! The American chain, Elevation Burger, after making its presence in the Middle East with locations like Dubai, is now planning to expand in India with major hubs like Delhi, Mumbai and Bengaluru.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></p><p>Elevation Burger known as an organic fast casual restaurant, is infamous for its vertigo burger with patties stacked up to 10 in number (Dare to go all out?). They have a unique process of assembling the burger where you can choose how big a stack you want.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>What Can You Expect?</strong></p><p>Claiming use of ingredients that are 100% organic, grass-fed, free-range, environment-friendly and healthier, we hope they maintain these values on expansion to Delhi. While they do have vegetarian, vegan and chicken burgers, most of the menu comprises of beef burgers, so we will have to wait and see how they modify or stick to it, upon launching in India.</p><p><em>Read the complete article <a href="http://www.business-standard.com/article/companies/new-wave-of-burger-chains-look-to-enter-india-116101500842_1.html" target="_blank">here</a>.</em></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Elevation Burger</strong></em></p>

3518	New Bars And Restaurants To Check Out in Sector 29, Gurgaon	<p>Sector-29, Gurgaon is not only the hub for food trucks but also for restaurants and bars, and more so in the last few months. The recent openings have swamped this food hub, so, read on to see our list of restaurants you shouldn&#8217;t miss.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prankster</strong></h4><p>A recent addition to Gurgaon’s food hub, Prankster is a pub unlike any other. A grand entrance with high ceilings adorned with bright red and white colours transports you to another dimension. The restaurant has different themes with unique light fixtures and embellishments. The food here is as quirky as the ambience and each dish is inspired by a nostalgic story from the past, served in a twisted manner.</p><p>Explore Prankster <a href="https://www.facebook.com/prankstergurgaon/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Veda &amp; </strong>Djinggs</h4><p>This iconic restaurant from Connaught Place has expanded to Gurgaon, and in a location accessible to all. Veda shares the restaurant space with a new venture, Djinngs and the collaborated establishment serves classic North Indian food as well as Chinese. We can finally revel in the delicious <strong>Dahi Kebabs</strong> from Veda and mouthwatering <strong>dim sums</strong> from the Chinese counterpart!</p><p>Explore Veda and Dijinggs <a href="https://www.facebook.com/VedaDjinggs/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Matchbox</strong></h4><p><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/10/13909212_1383930248287898_8822147715117430164_o.jpg"><img title = "New Bars And Restaurants To Check Out in Sector 29, Gurgaon  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3536" src="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/10/13909212_1383930248287898_8822147715117430164_o.jpg" alt = "Sector 29 Gurgaon restaurants north Indian fast food Chinese   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/13909212_1383930248287898_8822147715117430164_o.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/13909212_1383930248287898_8822147715117430164_o-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/13909212_1383930248287898_8822147715117430164_o-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of:<strong> Matchbox</strong></em></p><p>A classic British pub with sports flags used as decoration, laid back seating and a bar table on the side. Hit the bar to watch the next football game on their large screen as you cheer for your favourite team. Down a beer pint or two, while gorging on some delicious <strong>filo wraps</strong>.</p><p>Explore Matchbox <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Matchboxggn/">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>AnnCensored &#8211; अन्नCensored</strong></h4><p>The restaurant is divided along the theme of censored and ann-censored. Revolving around the concept of serving desi as well as fusion food, the menu includes unique dishes like <strong>butter chicken croquettes</strong> and <strong>beetroot tikki chaat</strong>. Their <strong>tiki drinks</strong> have us intrigued, making it one of the top places to cross off our to-visit list.</p><p>Explore AnnCensored-अन्नCensored <a href="https://www.facebook.com/AnnCensoredCafe/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Dockyard</strong></h4><p>Opened in August, this microbrewery has two floors and an outside seating.

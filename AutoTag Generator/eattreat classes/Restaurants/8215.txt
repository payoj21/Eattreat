8215	#EatCheap: 6 Places You Can Always Go To For Shawarma In Delhi	<p>We love shawarma on windy cloudy days, and on hot sunny days, and in the chilly winter too. Even more so when it&#8217;s raining. So what, we&#8217;re saying is, we love shawarmas so much, we&#8217;d be down to get one no matter what day or weather. We&#8217;ve put this together for you so you can get your fill of shawarma in Delhi at these places.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>1. Al Bake</strong></h4><p>Located in the New Friends Colony market, this is one of the most loved places in Delhi for shawarma. Their most sold dishes include <strong>Paneer shawarma, Chicken shawarma and Tandoori chicken</strong>. Their shawarmas are very juicy and tender, served with yummy mayonnaise. Although&nbsp;their sitting arrangement is a bit congested, the service is quick and the flavours are delicious.</p><p><strong>Head to</strong> 21-23, Community Centre, New Friends Colony</p><p><strong>Say hello at</strong> 011 41672588,011 41802588</p><p><strong>Pay</strong> INR 50 per plate</p><p>Explore <a href="https://www.facebook.com/AlBake/" target="_blank">Al Bake&nbsp;</a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>2.

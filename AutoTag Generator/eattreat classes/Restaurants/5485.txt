5485	Spend Your Sunday At These Winter Brunches In Delhi	<p>Sweater weather is great, because now you can lounge in the sun while sipping on your choice of drink and have your meal peacefully without the hot sun bothering you. The days have become pleasant, and so we’ve put together a list of places you must go for your lazy Sunday brunches.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Sorrento At Shangri-La&#8217;s &#8211; Eros Hotel, New Delhi</strong></h4><p>Laying emphasis on the authenticity of hearty Italian flavours, Sorrento offers a family style Italian feast for its patrons. Alfresco seating combined with soothing music and bona fide Italian flavours, this feast at Sorrento will take their diners to a journey through the streets of Italy. The set menu boasts of Neapolitan Pizzas, Homemade Style Pastas, and Risottos with choice of grilled meats/seafood. They&#8217;ve also got an Antipasti counter that has a display of cheese, a selection of cold cuts, a Cicchetti selection that offers Bruschetta with many choices, with a perfect end in the Dolce section.</p><p>When:<strong> </strong>Every <span data-term="goog_998738314">Sunday</span><br />Packages: INR 3750 plus taxes per person for Brunch with cocktails / Prosecco<br />Timing: <span data-term="goog_998738315">Noon to 3:30 pm</span></p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/ShangrilaNewDelhi/?ref=br_rs" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Fio &#8211; Country Kitchen and Bar</strong></h4><p>Set inside the Garden of Five Senses in Saket, Fio has a slightly youthful vibe. This is also a great spot for your brunch date, and the setting makes you feel like you’re dining in the woods.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/fiocountrykitchenandbar/?surface=rese" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Royal China</strong></h4><p><span class="m_4600404210597740073gmail-il">Royal</span> <span class="m_4600404210597740073gmail-il">China</span>, offers authentic Chinese fare and bring a new menu on the eve of Christmas with an unlimited dim sum brunch that includes unlimited wine, sangria, mulled wine, and soft beverages.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/royalchinadelhi/?fref=ts" target="_blank" data-saferedirecturl="https://www.google.com/url?hl=en&amp;q=https://www.facebook.com/royalchinadelhi/?fref%3Dts&amp;source=gmail&amp;ust=1482310293803000&amp;usg=AFQjCNHqAstoBdkWN5U1mCIPyxUuHZAntg">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"> <strong>Caffé Tonino</strong></h4><p>Choose from a selection of delicious Italian food in the warm interiors of Caffé Tonino.

5515	Five Star Restaurant Food Will Now Be Available In Small Villages In MP	<p>Thinking of going to a remote place like Chhindwara in Madhya Pradesh? Don’t be surprised if you encounter a ‘five-star’ quality meal there, prepared by local youths. Five star restaurant food will soon be available here.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></h4><p>In a first-of-its-kind tripartite initiative, Mumbai’s famed <a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//expensive-restaurants-across-india-spend-big-bucks/">Taj Mahal Hotel</a>, Tata Strive Entrepreneurship Programme and Confederation of Indian Industries (CII) have come together to help youth in remote areas set up small eateries or stalls in their villages.</p><p>For this, they will provide financial, infrastructural and skill support to local students in collaboration with local governments and forest authorities to enable the youth to gain a sustainable livelihood.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>What’s More?</strong></h4><p>The first batch of 18 ‘foodpreneurs’ – students, including five women and two tribals from Chhindwara have been trained for professional certificate courses, and from next year, the Taj Group will train four batches of 20 students each.</p><p>Taj has set up a kitchen-cum-bakery in Chhindwara for the programme along with modules on financial literacy essentials for running a restaurant with planning and costs. A five star restaurant and five star treatment are coming to MP. Are you ready for it?</p><p><em>Read the full story <a href="http://food.ndtv.com/food-drinks/expect-five-star-food-in-mp-villages-courtesy-taj-cii-1638175" target="_blank">here</a>.</em></p><p><em>Featured Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Taj Mahal Hotels</strong></em></p>

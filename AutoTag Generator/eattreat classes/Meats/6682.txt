6682	There’s Nothing Fishy About This Crispy Batch Of Fish ‘n’ Chips	<p>Our version of Britain’s favourite pub food, this Fish n Chips recipe is our particular pick for wintery evenings when we don&#8217;t want anything too spicy. And we definitely recommend the tartare sauce to go with this savoury filler.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Serves:</strong> 4</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prep Time:</strong> 20-25 minutes</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cook Time:</strong> 25 minutes</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Elements</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>For the chips</strong></p><p style="text-align: center;">4 large potatoes, peeled, cut into strips and soaked in cold water</p><p style="text-align: center;">Vegetable oil</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>For the fish batter</strong></p><p style="text-align: center;">1 cup all-purpose flour</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 teaspoon baking powder</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 teaspoon salt</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 teaspoon ground black pepper</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 cup milk</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 egg</p><p style="text-align: center;">Vegetable oil</p><p style="text-align: center;">680 gm cod fillets</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Method</strong></h4><p><strong>For the chips</strong></p><p>Heat oil in a pan and fry the potato strips until tender. Once crispy, take out of the pan and drain off the excess oil on a kitchen towel. Fry them a second time just before serving for added crispness.</p><p><strong>For the fish batter</strong></p><p>In a bowl mix together the flour, baking powder, salt and pepper. Pour in the milk, crack the egg and stir well until the mixture is smooth. Set aside for about 20 minutes.</p><p>Douse the fish in the batter, making sure all sides are evenly coated.</p><p>Heat oil in a pan and fry the batter-covered fish until it turns a crisp golden brown. Drain off the excess oil on a kitchen towel and serve hot with crispy chips.</p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of:  <strong><a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/10413717@N08/9355126330/">Smabs Sputzer</a> via <a href="https://visualhunt.com/">Visualhunt.com</a></strong></em></p>

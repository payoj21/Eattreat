6813	Easy Seafood Risotto With A Hint Of Saffron To Warm Your Insides	<p>A seafood risotto dish that packs a punch. We love our risotto like we like our Italians: memorable and outstandingly delicious. And when you put seafood into the mix, there&#8217;s nothing else to be done but to face the monster of craving that you&#8217;ve created. Here&#8217;s to a happy lunch with this seafood risotto!</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Serves: </strong>2</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prep Time: </strong>20 minutes</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cook Time: </strong>20-25 minutes</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Elements:</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">A pinch of saffron</p><p style="text-align: center;">¼ cup hot water</p><p style="text-align: center;">3½ cups vegetable stock</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 tablespoon olive oil</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ medium onion, diced</p><p style="text-align: center;">50 grams fennel, thinly sliced</p><p style="text-align: center;">150 grams Arborio rice (risotto rice)</p><p style="text-align: center;">50 grams Parmesan cheese</p><p style="text-align: center;">230 grams mussels, cleaned and de-bearded (remove the fuzzy hair sticking out)</p><p style="text-align: center;">130 grams shrimp, peeled and deveined (remove black string from inside the back)</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 tablespoon unsalted butter</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula:</strong></h4><p>Soak the saffron in hot water for about 20 minutes.</p><p>Add the vegetable stock to a saucepan and heat.</p><p>Heat the olive oil in a pan and add in the onion and fennel. Let this cook for about two minutes before adding the rice. After about three-five minutes, pour the saffron water and a ladle of the hot vegetable stock into the rice. Stir until the liquid is fully absorbed and the rice is tender. Stir in the Parmesan cheese.</p><p>Add in the mussels, followed by a ladle of the vegetable stock. Cover and cook for three minutes until the mussels have opened. Next, throw in the shrimp and cook for about two minutes. Lastly, stir in the butter and serve hot.</p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong><a href="http://albumarium.com/55536303767073476c201900-risotto/555367767670737e0c6d0a00" target="_blank">Taku via Flickr</a></strong></em></p>

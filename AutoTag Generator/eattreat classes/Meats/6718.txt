6718	A Shrimp & Fish Burger Recipe For When You’re Tired Of Your Usual Juicy Lucy	<p>Home-made fish burgers that taste of the sea. Too upset that your Goa plans didn&#8217;t work out? We&#8217;re doing a batch of <a href="http://eattreat.in/tag/seafood/" target="_blank">seafood recipes</a> that will give you all the feels of sun, sand and seafood. So go ahead and try this easy shrimp and fish burger recipe.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Serves: </strong>2-4</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prep Time:</strong> 15 minutes, 1 hour resting time</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cook Time:</strong> 15-20 minutes</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Elements</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;"><b></b>250 gm fish, skinned, boned and finely chopped</p><p style="text-align: center;">100 gm shrimp, finely chopped</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 red chillis, deseeded and finely chopped</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 teaspoon grated ginger</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 cloves garlic, crushed</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 egg, beaten</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 tablespoons mayonnaise</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ tablespoon Worcestershire sauce</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 tablespoon fish sauce</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 lime, juiced and grated for zest</p><p style="text-align: center;">80 gm breadcrumbs</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 tablespoon oil</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula</strong></h4><p>Place together the fish, shrimps, parsley, chilli, ginger, garlic, beaten egg, mayonnaise, Worcestershire sauce, fish sauce, lime zest, lime juice and breadcrumbs in a large bowl. Mix everything well together. Using your hands, shape out 6&#215;8 cm burger patties from this mixture. Transfer them onto a plate/ tray, cover and refrigerate for an hour.</p><p>Heat oil in a pan, and fry the patties over medium heat for about four minutes on each side under golden and crisp.</p><p>Generously spread mayonnaise over the burger buns, layer with lettuce leaves, onions and tomatoes and any other topping of your choice. Place the fried patty in the middle. Serve with a side of crispy French fries.</p><p>Try this fish burger recipe at your own risk, cos you&#8217;ll be flooded with orders!</p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Madishetty Manasa</strong></em></p>

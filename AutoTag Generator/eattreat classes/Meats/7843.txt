7843	Lucknow’s Famous Tunday Kababi Is Coming To This Tunday Kababi Festival In Delhi!	<p>Who doesn&#8217;t love hot, smooth, melt-in-the-mouth kebabs on a winter day? (Or, on any day for that matter.) We&#8217;ve got your back for all your kebab cravings. The Tunday Kababi Festival at JW Marriott New Delhi Aerocity is going to serve you the best kebabs from Lucknow.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></h4><p>The most famous name when it comes to kebabs in Lucknow is Tunday Kababi, with their melt-in-the-mouth <strong>parathas</strong> and <strong>Galavati Kababs</strong>. More than a hundred years old, Tunday Kababi situated in the Chowk area of Lucknow is a regular haunt of kabab lovers. Tucked away in a small lane in the bustling centre of the old city, the name and fame of this eatery has gone beyond Lucknow, and even India.</p><p>In Urdu, <em>tunda</em> means ‘without an arm.’ The legacy finds it&#8217;s origins from in Haji Murad Ali (Tunday Kebabi), who used to feed the aristocrats&nbsp;in the old days of Lucknow, even though he was handicapped, was able to earn repute and respect for his speciality—preparing delicious meaty morsels with only one hand!</p><h4><strong>What&#8217;s In It For You?</strong></h4><p>Chef Mohammad Usman, grandson of the famous Tunday Kababi of Lucknow is coming to launch the food festival that is scheduled to run from 16-26 Feb at K3, the all-day dining restaurant at the JW Marriot New Delhi Aerocity. They&#8217;re going to have all your favourite kebabs here at the festival, so you should not miss it.&nbsp;</p><p>So what are you waiting for?

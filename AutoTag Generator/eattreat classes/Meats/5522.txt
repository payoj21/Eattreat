5522	8 Places In India That Food Lovers Must Visit To Get Their Fill	<p>‘Tis the holiday season! Holidays mean taking off for a vacation, lapping up new places, taking in the sights and sounds, and gorging on new delicacies. If you’re a keen explorer of gastronomical delights, and are thirsty for an adventure that involves loads of food, give our curated list of food destinations and places in India a look. We’re sure you’ll end up putting each on your bucket list.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Kolkata</strong></h4><p>There’s something about Kolkata that speaks old world charm. It could be the architectural grandeur of the city, or the trams. We like to think it is the food. Kolkata’s famed street fare – <strong>puchkas</strong> and <strong>spicy jhal muri</strong>, followed by sweet and delectable <strong>sondesh</strong> and<strong> rasgullas</strong> are a must try! Also don’t miss river fish (hilsa and betki) in mustard as well as sweets made from Nolen Gur.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Jaipur</strong></h4><p>The Pink City with its charming lanes and havelis make for a picturesque vacation spot. Sample authentic Rajasthani fare like <strong>Pyaaz Kachori</strong> or the famous rich lassi.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Hyderabad</strong></h4><p>We bet ‘Biryani’ is the first thing that popped up in your mind when you read ‘Hyderabad’! Make a visit to this city for its epicurean delights like the <strong>Haleem</strong>, <strong>seekh kebabs</strong> and <strong>chaakna</strong> (spicy stew).</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Lucknow</strong></h4><p>Everyone knows about Lucknow’s famed Awadhi fare and the awesome street food. Think <strong>Tundey ke Kebab</strong> (soft, melt-in-your-mouth kebabs made with lamb mince), or authentic <strong>Awadhi biryani</strong>. Also try the<strong> rabdi, lassi and kulfi</strong>.</p><p><a href="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Amrati_or_Jalebi.jpg"><img title = "8 Places In India That Food Lovers Must Visit To Get Their Fill  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5576" src="http://eattreat.in/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Amrati_or_Jalebi.jpg" alt = "hyderabadi goan delhi Category:Meats Biryani bengali   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Amrati_or_Jalebi.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Amrati_or_Jalebi-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Amrati_or_Jalebi-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Delhi</strong></h4><p>Delhi takes the number one spot on the food places in India list. From the street food like <strong>papdi-chaat</strong>, <strong>dahi bhalle</strong>, <strong>gol gappe</strong>, <strong>Chandni Chowk’s jalebis</strong> to the wide array of different cuisines available here for epicurean exploration, Delhi has <a href="http://eattreat.in/old-delhi-desserts/" target="_blank">something for everyone</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Goa</strong></h4><p>Known more popularly as the party capital of India, Goa has an interesting variety of food to offer. Try the authentic <strong>Goan fish curry</strong>, <strong>Goan sausage and pao</strong>, <strong>Vindaloo</strong> and<strong> Sorpotel</strong>. You could hit a shack for these, or try a local restaurant. There’s many options to choose from.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"></h4><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Shillong</strong></h4><p>Thinking of exploring the North-East? Don’t miss the traditional north-eastern delicacies at Shillong like steaming hot and wholesome<strong> Thukpa</strong>, or the <strong>pork momos</strong> at Broadway Hotel. Baked savouries, chowmein and other <em>khasi</em> delights are also not to be missed.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Mumbai</strong></h4><p>Amchi Mumbai is also one of the top food destinations of India. From the ubiquitous <strong>Vada Pav</strong> near CST, to buttery <strong>Pav Bhaji</strong> at Chowpatti – there is SO much to sample. Mumbai also has a lot of different cuisines and a broad spectrum of establishments to explore.</p><p><em>Photo Courtesy: Wikimedia Commons</em></p>

3782	Chicken With Quinoa & Baby Spinach For When You’re Staying In	<p>Enjoy this recipe of chicken and spinach on a bed of quinoa with a balsamic reduction for when you&#8217;re looking to make some healthy comfort food.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Serves:</strong> 2 &#8211; 3</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prep Time:</strong> 15 mins</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cook Time:</strong> 15 &#8211; 20mins</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Elements:</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">1 tablespoon olive oil</p><p style="text-align: center;">4 cloves garlic, chopped</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 boneless chicken thighs</p><p style="text-align: center;">110 grams baby spinach</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ cup chicken broth</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar</p><p style="text-align: center;">3 tomatoes, chopped</p><p style="text-align: center;">¼ cup quinoa, cooked</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula:</strong></h4><p>Heat the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium-high heat.</p><p>Add the chopped garlic and sauté. Add the chicken thighs and cook for about four minutes on each side or until cooked through. Remove the chicken and set aside.</p><p>In the same pan, place the spinach and cook for a few minutes until the leaves wilt. Remove from the pan and set aside.</p><p>Lower the heat to medium, add the chicken broth, balsamic vinegar and stir, scraping the pan to remove any browned bits.</p><p>Add in the tomatoes, bring to a boil and let simmer for about three to five minutes.</p><p>Place the cooked quinoa in a serving bowl. Place the spinach and chicken on the quinoa, top with the balsamic-tomato reduction and serve!</p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Madishetty Manasa</strong></em></p>

3639	Mutton Delicacies You Can Try In Delhi That Aren’t Just From Karim’s	<p>Now that the Navratras are over, and you’ve force-fed yourselves all the the packets of <em>vrat ka khaana</em> to finish them off; it’s time for you to breathe and binge on all the gorgeous mutton dishes that Delhi has to offer. Here’s our list of fave places, apart from the ever popular Karim’s, for you to explore.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Ashok Meat Shop</strong></h4><p>This place is open only from 12 pm to 3 pm, but their <strong>Mutton Curry</strong> is so good and so famous, that their stocks are over within an hour of opening. So, if you’re ready to brave the crowd waiting for this place to open, you should definitely go there. It’s definitely worth the wait.</p><p>Drop in at 5820/42, Subhash Chowk, Sadar Thana, Azad Market</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Nazeer Delicacies</strong></h4><p><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/10/10321091_706335326072274_3591683455908080495_o.jpg"><img title = "Mutton Delicacies You Can Try In Delhi That Aren&#039;t Just From Karim&#039;s  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3657" src="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/10/10321091_706335326072274_3591683455908080495_o.jpg" alt = "north Indian non vegetarian mutton iconic   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/10321091_706335326072274_3591683455908080495_o.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/10321091_706335326072274_3591683455908080495_o-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/10321091_706335326072274_3591683455908080495_o-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Nazeer&#8217;s</strong></em></p><p>You need to go here for their amazing <strong>Mutton Qorma</strong>, best had with <strong>Sheermal</strong>/<strong>Laal Roti</strong>. They also make a great <strong>Nahari</strong>, but it is available only on Saturdays. They’re located at different places in the city, so they’re pretty accessible too.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/nazeerdelicacies/?hc_ref=SEARCH&amp;fref=nf">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Al Jawahar</strong></h4><p>Their <strong>Nihari</strong> is to die for, and you can get it only if you can reach there before 12 in the afternoon. Their <strong>Badam Pasanda</strong> is so good, your mind will be blown once you’ve licked off every bit of it from your plate and fingers. Even their regular <strong>Mutton Qorma</strong> is pretty damn good. You have to eat it to believe it.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/AlJawahardelhi6/">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Nizam’s Kathi Kabab</strong></h4><p><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/10/13123185_10153439976205933_1195472078267951957_o.jpg"><img title = "Mutton Delicacies You Can Try In Delhi That Aren&#039;t Just From Karim&#039;s  Mutton Delicacies You Can Try In Delhi That Aren&#039;t Just From Karim&#039;s  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3658" src="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/10/13123185_10153439976205933_1195472078267951957_o.jpg" alt = "north Indian non vegetarian mutton iconic   north Indian non vegetarian mutton iconic   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/13123185_10153439976205933_1195472078267951957_o.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/13123185_10153439976205933_1195472078267951957_o-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/13123185_10153439976205933_1195472078267951957_o-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Nizam&#8217;s</strong></em></p><p>Nizam’s is known for their humongous <strong>Mutton Rolls</strong> that you can’t finish in one go if you don’t have a huge appetite. The juicy meat makes you want to take your time savouring every single bite.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Nizams-Kathi-Kabab-294743390932/">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Ghalib Kabab Corner</strong></h4><p>Everything at Ghalib’s comes highly recommended, but you need to try their <strong>Mutton Seekh Kebabs</strong>. So soft and juicy, you’re instantly transported to an ambiance where Qawwali singers serenade you with their tunes. They have limited seating but the food is worth the wait.</p><p>Drop in at Shop 57, Ghalib Road, Near Lal Mahal, Nizamuddin</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Aap Ki Khatir</strong></h4><p>One bite and the <strong>Kakori Kebabs</strong> instantly melt in your mouth like butter. Their <strong>Mutton Qorma</strong> is also pretty great, and you should get these with their <strong>Shahi Paratha</strong>. Truly makes for a great meal.</p><p>Address: 1, C-Block, DDA Market, SDA</p><p><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/10/5172117726_dbcdf83df8_b.jpg"><img title = "Mutton Delicacies You Can Try In Delhi That Aren&#039;t Just From Karim&#039;s  Mutton Delicacies You Can Try In Delhi That Aren&#039;t Just From Karim&#039;s  Mutton Delicacies You Can Try In Delhi That Aren&#039;t Just From Karim&#039;s  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3641" src="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/10/5172117726_dbcdf83df8_b.jpg" alt = "north Indian non vegetarian mutton iconic   north Indian non vegetarian mutton iconic   north Indian non vegetarian mutton iconic   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/5172117726_dbcdf83df8_b.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/5172117726_dbcdf83df8_b-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/5172117726_dbcdf83df8_b-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong><a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/nadircruise/5172117726/">NA.dir</a> via <a href="https://visualhunt.com/photos/food-images/">VisualHunt</a></strong></em></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Anand Restaurant</strong></h4><p>Anand is hidden away in the bylanes of Connaught Place but is usually quite crowded. They’re pretty well known for their biryanis, and you must go there for their <strong>Mutton Biryani</strong> that they serve with a little gravy. Though a little too spicy, it is very delicious and super easy on your pocket .</p><p>Address: 15/96, Scindia House, Connaught Place</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Al Kauser Al Kakori</strong></h4><p>Al Kauser is one of the most popular places for <strong>Mutton Tikkas</strong>, <strong>Galouti Kebabs</strong>, and <strong>Mutton Kakori Kebabs</strong> in Delhi. We’ve been told that their <strong>Mutton Dum Biryani</strong> is worth looking out for too! *slurp*</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/AlkauserSafdarjungEnclave/">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Ashirbad Snacks Corner</strong></h4><p>Located in Market No. 3 in CR Park, Aashirwad is a well-known destination for their juicy, delicious <strong>Mutton Chops</strong> that come at an extremely low price. While you’re in CR Park, you should also go to Market No. 1 for some delicious <strong>mutton cutlets</strong> that you can find at any of the stalls there.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/ashirbadcaterers/">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Delhi Canteen</strong></h4><p>You need to ring up The Delhi Canteen for it’s scrumptious <strong>Mutton Belliram</strong> and<strong> Mutton Nihari</strong>. If you’ve been craving for some spicier preparations, you should go there just for their<strong> Laal Maas ki Handi</strong>. So good, that you’ll be dreaming of it for days after.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/thedelhicanteen/">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Andhra Bhavan Canteen</strong></h4><p>One does not simply list down places for great mutton dishes, and not mention Andhra Bhavan’s<strong> Mutton Fry</strong>. If you’ve ever known anyone who’s been there, you will be told about the long queue that you have to stand in, just to get in. Now you know why.</p><p>Explore them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/AndhraBhavanCanteen/?fref=ts">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Afghan Darbar</strong></h4><p>Located in Lajpat Nagar, this hidden gem of a place is run by a couple from Afghanistan. The <strong>Mutton Chopan Kebab</strong> and <strong>Mantu</strong> (steamed dumplings made with chopped mutton and topped with yogurt and dal sauce) come highly recommended. The other great thing about this place is that the prices aren’t too high, and the portions are generous.</p><p>Drop in at E-96, Lajpat Nagar II</p><p><em>Featured Image Courtesy Of: <strong><a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/n0r/1472584136/">n0r</a> via <a href="https://visualhunt.com/">Visual Hunt</a> </strong></em></p>

6474	Lahori Chicken Tikka Recipe That Will Give You Melt-In-The-Mouth Chunks Of Chicken	<p>Chef Raminder Bakshi&#8217;s amazing Lahori Chicken Tikka recipe is easy to make if you have a tandoor at home. A wonderful snack during the winter evenings or with a side of dal makhani and naan, this is a fool-proof meal that you&#8217;ll love making over and over again.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Elements</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">3 kg chicken thigh tikka</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 ½ spoon salt</p><p style="text-align: center;">2 spoon red chilli powder</p><p style="text-align: center;">4 spoon ginger garlic paste</p><p style="text-align: center;">200 ml lemon juice</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 spoon garam masala</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 spoon crushed black pepper</p><p style="text-align: center;">4 spoon red chilli paste</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Second Marination</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">100 gms Amul Cheese</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 spoon roast chana powder</p><p style="text-align: center;">3 eggs</p><p style="text-align: center;">200 gms cream fresh</p><p style="text-align: center;">400 gms curd</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Mix all the ingredients except second marination and keep for 20 minutes.</span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">mix all the ingredients of second marination and mix with the first marinade.</span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Keep it in fridge for 6 hours before cooking in tandoor.</span></p><p>This Lahori Chicken Tikka recipe doesn&#8217;t take as much time to cook as it takes to prep, so make sure you plan this in advance.</p><p><em>Representative Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Wikimedia Commons</strong></em></p>

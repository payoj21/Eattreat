5468	Not Mincing Our Words With These Fantastic Mince Pies In Delhi	<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">What is Christmas without having the staple x-mas fare like hearty mince pies to fill you up? We love mince pies, especially the freshly baked kind made the traditional way. Don’t want to make it at home? Here’s a handy list of our picks for mince pies this season.</span></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Theo’s, Noida</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Theo’s is doing a limited edition Grandma’s minced pies this festive season, so pre-book your batch before they run out!</span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Follow them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/theosfood/" target="_blank">here</a>.</span></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Lavaash Deli by Saby</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Lamb mince pies at <a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//mehrauli-restaurants-luxury-dinner/">Lavaash Deli by Saby</a> are to die for. With a crispy pie crust, stuffed with lamb mince and topped with Kalimpong cheese, this one’s a sure shot Christmas delight.</span></p><p>Follow them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/lavaashbysaby/?fref=ts" target="_blank">here</a>.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>G’s Patisserie by Gauri Varma</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Gauri Varma is a home baker based out of Kalkaji. She’s doing a special range of mince pies this season. Chicken or Lamb mince pies with bacon, thyme and fresh herbs. You can even check out her special Christmas menu, she has a tonne of marvels to ring in the festive season.</span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Follow her <a href="https://www.facebook.com/gpatisserie/?fref=ts" target="_blank">here</a>. </span></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Elma’s Bakery, Bar &amp; Kitchen</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Chef’s special, classic Irish style minced lamb pie with mashed potato crust is what we’re betting on, to make our Christmas even merrier.</span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Follow Elma’s <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Elmasbakery/" target="_blank">here</a>. </span></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Caara Café, British Council</strong></h4><p><a href="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/12/13962559_1824749041082336_7718361178889718584_n.jpg"><img title = "Not Mincing Our Words With These Fantastic Mince Pies In Delhi  "class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5501" src="http://www.eattreat.in/magazine/magazine//wp-content/uploads/2016/12/13962559_1824749041082336_7718361178889718584_n.jpg" alt = "snacks meat festival christmas Category:Meats   " width="847" height="507" srcset="http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/13962559_1824749041082336_7718361178889718584_n.jpg 847w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/13962559_1824749041082336_7718361178889718584_n-300x180.jpg 300w, http://eattreat.in/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/13962559_1824749041082336_7718361178889718584_n-768x460.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 847px) 100vw, 847px" /></a></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Caara Café is doing traditional lamb mince pies from the 18</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">th</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> till the end of December. Perfect picks for the festive season.</span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Follow them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/caaracafe/?fref=ts" target="_blank">here</a>. </span></p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>The Chatter House</strong></h4><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Chatter house does a mean Shepherd’s Pie which has lamb ragout, topped with mashed potatoes, gratinated and served with garlic bread. A must try this festive season.</p><p>Follow them <a href="https://www.facebook.com/tpmdirtyapron/">here</a></p>

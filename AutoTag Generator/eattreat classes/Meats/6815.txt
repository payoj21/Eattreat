6815	An Easy Coastal Fish Curry That’ll Remind You Of Your Beach Vacation	<p>A contender for our favourite fish curry – this one never fails to impress! Not your usual <a href="http://eattreat.in/nisteachi-coddi-goan-fish-curry-recipe/">Goan fish curry</a>, this <a href="http://eattreat.in/seafood-risotto-italian-recipe/">seafood recipe </a>for fish curry is just the thing you need to remind you of the many flavours of the coast, especially those without coconut and cashews.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Serves:</strong> 2-3</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Prep Time:</strong> 10-15 minutes, 20 minutes resting time</p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Cook Time:</strong> 20-25 minutes</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Elements:</strong></h4><p style="text-align: center;">2 teaspoons lemon juice</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ teaspoon salt</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 teaspoon turmeric powder</p><p style="text-align: center;">500 grams Surmai fish, chopped into piece</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ teaspoon cumin seeds</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ teaspoon garlic paste</p><p style="text-align: center;">1 cup onion paste</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ cup tomato puree</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ teaspoon red chilli powder</p><p style="text-align: center;">½ teaspoon coriander powder</p><p style="text-align: center;">Water</p><p style="text-align: center;">Coriander leaves for garnishing</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Formula:</strong></h4><p>Add the lemon juice to a bowl and stir in ½ teaspoon each of salt and turmeric powder. Add the fish to this mixture and set aside to marinate for half an hour. Heat oil in a pan and fry the fish. Set aside.</p><p>In the same pan, add in the cumin seeds, and once they splutter add the garlic paste and sauté for about a minute before adding the onion paste. Once the onion paste starts to turn light pink, add in the tomato puree, red chilli powder, coriander power and remaining turmeric powder. Give everything a good mix.</p><p>Pour in half a glass of water, stir well and then add in the fried fish you had set aside. Cover the pan and cook for about five minutes on low heat. Season with salt to taste, and garnish with coriander leaves before serving with steamed rice.</p>

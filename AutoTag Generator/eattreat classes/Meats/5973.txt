5973	KFC’s New Smart Restaurant? Let AI Help You Decide Your Order	<p>The popular fast food chain KFC is working on creating a new and interesting dining experience for its customers and just recently launched it’s first AI (Artificial Intelligence) powered outlet in Beijing.</p><h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>First Things First</strong></h4><p>China’s largest search engine, Baidu Inc. has tied up with KFC to start its first smart restaurant in the Financial Street Area, just in time for Christmas. A great gift this is, indeed.</p><p>The machines installed in the restaurant will recognise the customer’s face, sex, age, mood, and other features through pictures taken with them. This will help the machine recommend suitable food and set meals to diners and complete the ordering process.</p><p>The machines will also be able to recognise your face and remember your order history and dining habits the next time you go to the KFC outlet so your order is placed faster. You can even interact with the machine and change facial expressions while making your order choices, take pictures with the machine and save them to your phone.</p><p><em>Read the full story <a href="http://www.huffingtonpost.in/2016/12/26/kfc-opens-ai-powered-outlet-in-china-to-reccomend-food-to-the-cu/" target="_blank">here</a>.</em></p><p><em>Photo Courtesy Of: <strong>Wikimedia Commons</strong></em></p>

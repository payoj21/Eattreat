porky slider the ideal meat cut for pulled pork is the shoulder or butt of the pig formula the pork shoulder is first brined for 24 hours in a brining liquid of cold water brown sugar apple cider vinegar bay leaf peppercorn and garlic it is then braised in stock in a pressure cooker for three hours “the pressure cooker technique of braising the meat is fast and gives us the desired result” says rohan once the pork is braised you apply the secret dry rub their dry rub recipe includes an intense combination of garlic powder onion powder rock salt mustard powder hot paprika celery salt cayenne pepper and more drizzle some apple juice cover it in foil and smoke it in the oven with hickory and apple wood for seven hours once done remove the bone and separate the juicy cooked pork into threads using your hands “we serve our porky with a nocream slaw simply cabbage onion carrots and apple cider vinegar this is also the classic louisiana slaw” he says but you can use your own fave

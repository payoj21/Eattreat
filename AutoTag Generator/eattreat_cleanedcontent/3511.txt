 hidden away in defence colony the culinary pitaara is this tiny little place that serves up quite a variety of flavourful dishes to its patrons in a nutshell the culinary pitaara serves north indian and chinese fare out of a small space in sukhdev market it has only recently opened and has quickly gained popularity among the people living in the vicinity perfect spot since they barely have any seating arrangements it is best to order food from here whether its a full meal that you are looking for or just appetisers to satisfy hunger pangs you can either order in or dine in at this small but clean and friendly establishment on the silver platter the butter chicken kulcha is a must try it comes with a butter chicken gravy thats wellseasoned and delicious you will find yourself not being able to stop at just one or even after you are full because they taste so good the honey chilli lotus stems dal makhanwali chipotle murg tikka spring rolls and the kung pao chicken are all worth a shot too you should definitely get these when you are visiting or ordering in bitter pill while some of their dishes stood apart quite clearly you can avoid getting the sui mai and the papad dahi ke kebab which were both a little soggy food for thought all in all we feel that you should ring up the culinary pitaara whenever you have those hunger pangs during the day the dishes are quite decently priced and the quantities are pretty great too explore them here the butter chicken kulcha at the culinary pitaara is just something else

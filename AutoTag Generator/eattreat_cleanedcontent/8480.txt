 an easy dip recipe for your next party an arabic dip that will add the right heat to your kebabs and tandoori snacks we are loving this muhammara dip recipe and hopefully you will tooand serves 34 prep time 10 minutes overnight resting time ingredients 1 cup breadcrumbs ½ cup extra virgin olive oil 2 teaspoons tahini ½ cup walnuts 2 small red bell peppers chopped 1 small onion chopped 1 tablespoon chili paste 1½ tablespoons tomato paste 2½ tablespoons pomegranate molasses 1½ teaspoons ground cumin 1½ teaspoons saltand method add the breadcrumbs in a bowl and pour in the olive oil and tahini mix well cover and set aside overnight at room temperature place the walnuts on a lined baking tray and bake in the oven at 200 degrees celsius for five minutes till crisp grind the roasted walnuts in a blender until they reach a texture similar to breadcrumbs and add to the breadcrumb mixture mix together well place the chopped peppers onion chilli paste and tomato paste in a blender and puree add this mixture along with the pomegranate molasses cumin and salt to the breadcrumbs give everything a good mix drizzle some olive oil over the top and serve with toasted pita breadand chefs tip in typical syrian style serve your spicy muhammara dip with a platter of grilled kebabs muhammara is a syrian dip recipe that’ll go superbly with your grilled kebabs
